數據品質討論：三大法人買賣超資料由台灣證券交易所每日盤後公布，品質可靠。FinMind API 提供便捷的存取方式，但免費版可能有部分交易日遺漏。我們以 yfinance 的 TAIEX 日線數據作為報酬率計算基礎，兩者的交易日均已對齊。

為何選擇 2013 年起：FinMind API 的法人買賣超數據從 2013 年起較為完整。此外，2013 年後台灣市場的法人參與度和資訊透明度都有所提升，分析結果更具現代意義。

Data quality: Institutional net buy/sell data is published by TWSE daily after market close and is reliable. FinMind API provides convenient access, though the free tier may have occasional date gaps. We use yfinance TAIEX data for return calculations, with trading days aligned.

Why start from 2013: FinMind institutional data is more complete from 2013 onward. Additionally, post-2013 institutional participation and information transparency improved, making results more relevant.

2.2 數據品質與處理2.2 Data Quality and Processing

法人資料合併：FinMind 提供的三大法人買賣超數據按日發布，我們將外資、投信、自營商分別處理，同時計算三大法人合計淨買超。數據經以下處理：(1) 與 TAIEX 交易日對齊，排除非交易日；(2) 缺漏值以前一交易日填補（carry forward）；(3) 計算前瞻報酬（T+1, T+5, T+20, T+60）時排除不足期間的樣本。

市場狀態判定：以 TAIEX 120 日移動平均線為分界：收盤價高於 120 日均線定義為「牛市」，低於為「熊市」。選擇 120 日均線的原因：(1) 約半年，能反映中期趨勢；(2) 不會因短期波動頻繁切換狀態；(3) 在投資實務中被廣泛使用作為趨勢判斷基準。

五分位分組：每日的法人淨買超金額按歷史分佈分為五等分（quintile）。Q1 = 賣超最多的 20% 天數（推估散戶買超最多），Q5 = 買超最多的 20% 天數（法人最看好）。分組使用全樣本百分位而非滾動窗口，以確保各 quintile 的樣本量平衡。

Data merging: FinMind provides daily institutional net buy/sell data; we process foreign, trust, and dealer separately while computing total institutional net buy. Processing: (1) align with TAIEX trading days; (2) carry forward for missing values; (3) exclude insufficient-period samples when computing forward returns (T+1, T+5, T+20, T+60).

Market regime classification: Using 120-day SMA of TAIEX: close above 120-day MA = "bull," below = "bear." 120 days chosen because: (1) ~half year, reflecting mid-term trend; (2) avoids frequent state-switching; (3) widely used in practice as trend benchmark.

Quintile grouping: Daily institutional net buy sorted into quintiles by historical distribution. Q1 = most selling 20% (estimated most retail buying), Q5 = most buying 20% (institutional most bullish). Full-sample percentiles used for balanced sample sizes.

2.3 分析方法2.3 Methods

  • Event Study（事件研究法）：將法人淨買超金額分為五分位，以 Q5（買超最多的 20% 天數）為訊號觸發日，計算 T+1 至 T+60 的累積異常報酬（CAR）。——為什麼用這個方法？Event Study 是金融學檢驗資訊效率的標準工具，能清楚呈現信號觸發後的報酬走勢。
  • Quintile 分析：比較五分位組的前瞻報酬（T+1, T+5, T+20, T+60），檢驗是否存在從 Q1 到 Q5 的單調遞增關係。——為什麼？如果不是單調關係，代表買超金額大小不等於信號強度。
  • Permutation Test（隨機排列檢定）：隨機打亂法人買賣超序列 10,000 次，每次重新計算 Q5 的 CAR，比較真實 CAR 是否落在隨機分佈的極端位置。——為什麼？這是最嚴格的非參數檢定，不需要任何分佈假設，直接回答「信號是否優於純粹的運氣」。
  • Granger 因果分析：檢驗法人買賣超是否統計上「領先」大盤報酬（而非只是同步或落後）。——為什麼？即使法人買超和股市上漲同時發生，也不代表法人能預測股市。我們需要因果方向的證據。
  • 連續買超分析：檢驗連續 1/3/5/10 天買超是否增強訊號。——為什麼？直覺上，法人連買多天比只買一天應該更有意義，但需要數據驗證。
  • 牛熊市分拆：將樣本分為牛市（大盤在 120 日均線以上）和熊市（以下），分別檢驗法人信號效果。——為什麼？牛市中追漲容易賺錢，不代表策略有效；熊市才是真正的考驗。
  • Event Study: Sort net buy into quintiles, use Q5 (top 20% buy days) as trigger, compute CAR from T+1 to T+60. — Why? Standard tool for testing information efficiency in finance.
  • Quintile analysis: Compare forward returns (T+1, T+5, T+20, T+60) across quintiles for monotonicity. — Why? Non-monotonic patterns suggest buy amount does not equal signal strength.
  • Permutation Test: Shuffle net buy 10,000 times, recompute Q5 CAR each time, test if real CAR is extreme. — Why? The most rigorous non-parametric test requiring no distributional assumptions, directly testing "better than luck."
  • Granger causality: Test whether institutional flow statistically "leads" market returns. — Why? Concurrent correlation does not imply prediction. We need directional causation evidence.
  • Consecutive analysis: Test whether 1/3/5/10 consecutive buy days strengthen the signal. — Why? Intuition says multi-day buying should be more meaningful, but data must verify.
  • Bull/bear split: Separate into bull (above 120-day MA) and bear (below) markets. — Why? Trend-following profits easily in bull markets; bear markets are the true test.

2.4 交易成本假設2.4 Transaction Cost Assumptions

本研究的基準分析未扣除交易成本，以便與學術文獻直接比較。但讀者必須注意：跟單策略涉及頻繁交易，每次買賣的成本包括券商手續費（0.1425%*2 = 0.285%）和交易稅（賣出 0.3%），單次來回約 0.585%。如果每週交易一次，年交易成本可達 30%——遠超任何可能的超額報酬。即使使用折扣券商（手續費 6 折），年交易成本仍可能超過 20%。

這也是 Barber et al. (2009) 發現散戶年均虧損 3.8% 的重要原因之一：不是判斷力差，而是交易成本太高。

Our baseline analysis excludes transaction costs for academic comparability. However, readers must note: copy-trading involves frequent trading, with each round-trip costing commission (0.1425%*2 = 0.285%) plus tax (0.3% on sell), ~0.585% per round trip. Weekly trading could cost ~30% annually — far exceeding any possible excess return. Even with discount brokers, annual costs may exceed 20%.

This is a key reason why Barber et al. (2009) found retail investors lose 3.8% annually: not poor judgment, but excessive transaction costs.
