緒論Introduction

1.1 背景1.1 Background

「跟著法人買就對了」是台灣股市最常見的投資信條之一。每天收盤後，媒體大幅報導三大法人買賣超資訊，許多散戶據此做出隔日的交易決策。各大財經節目的名嘴經常以「外資今天買超 XXX 億」作為看多的理由，彷彿法人的每一筆交易都蘊含著對未來的精準預判。

這個策略的隱含假設是：法人（特別是外資）擁有資訊優勢——他們有專業的研究團隊、更早接觸到企業訊息、擁有更先進的分析工具。因此，其買賣方向能預測未來股價走勢。用日常比喻，就像考試時偷看學霸的答案——學霸通常是對的。

然而，這個假設值得嚴格質疑：(1) 法人買賣超數據有一天的延遲（T+1 才公布），你看到「答案」時考試可能已經結束了；(2) 法人可能是追隨趨勢（動能投資）而非擁有內部資訊——學霸可能只是在猜，只是猜對的機率高一點；(3) 當大量散戶同時追蹤同一信號時，信號本身可能因為太多人使用而失效（擁擠效應）；(4) 每次跟單都要付出交易成本，頻繁交易會大幅侵蝕報酬。

"Follow institutional investors" is one of the most popular investment mantras in Taiwan. Media extensively reports daily institutional net buy/sell data, and many retail investors make next-day trading decisions based on this. Financial TV commentators routinely cite "foreign investors bought XX billion today" as a bullish signal, as if every institutional trade contains precise future predictions.

The implicit assumption: institutions (especially foreign) have information advantage — professional research teams, earlier access to corporate information, more advanced analytical tools. Think of it as copying the smart student's exam answers.

However, this assumption deserves rigorous scrutiny: (1) data has one-day delay (published at T+1) — by the time you see the "answers," the exam may already be over; (2) institutions may be trend-following rather than informed — the smart student might just be guessing better; (3) mass retail following may degrade the signal (crowding effect); (4) each trade incurs transaction costs, and frequent trading significantly erodes returns.

1.2 台灣三大法人制度1.2 Taiwan Institutional Investor System

理解「三大法人」的組成，對正確解讀數據至關重要：

外資（Foreign Institutional Investors）：包括外國投資銀行、對沖基金、退休基金、主權財富基金、全球 ETF 被動資金等。這是台灣市場最具影響力的法人群體——外資持有台股比例長期超過 40%。然而，「外資」並非鐵板一塊：長期持有的退休基金和短線進出的對沖基金，策略天差地遠。公開數據將這些迥異的投資者合併計算，這是本研究的重要限制之一。

投信（Investment Trust）：國內投信基金公司，管理台灣民眾的基金資產。投信的交易行為可能受到基金贖回壓力（散戶贖回時被迫賣出）、績效排名競爭（季末「做績效」）等因素影響，其交易不完全反映投資判斷，部分是被動行為。

自營商（Proprietary Dealers）：證券商自有資金部門，以短線交易和避險為主。自營商的交易量較小但頻率最高，且很大一部分是避險或套利交易（如 ETF 折溢價套利），不具有方向性預測意義。因此，自營商的買賣超數據的「信號」含量最低。

為什麼散戶愛看法人買賣超？因為它是台灣市場獨有的、每日即時公開的資訊，取得門檻極低（證交所網站、各大財經 App）。這種「免費的捷徑」極具吸引力——但正如 Andrew Lo 的 AMH 所述，當太多人使用同一資訊源時，其獲利能力必然消退。

Understanding the composition of "three institutional investors" is crucial for correct data interpretation:

Foreign Institutional Investors: Including investment banks, hedge funds, pension funds, sovereign wealth funds, and global ETF passive flows. The most influential institutional group in Taiwan — foreign ownership has consistently exceeded 40% of Taiwan stocks. However, "foreign" is heterogeneous: long-term pension funds and short-term hedge funds have vastly different strategies. Aggregating these diverse investors is a key limitation of this study.

Investment Trust: Domestic mutual fund companies managing retail fund assets. Trading may be influenced by fund redemption pressure (forced selling when retail redeems) and performance ranking competition (quarter-end "window dressing"), not purely reflecting investment judgment.

Proprietary Dealers: Securities firms' own capital, focused on short-term trading and hedging. Smallest volume but highest frequency, largely hedging or arbitrage (e.g., ETF premium/discount arb), with minimal directional predictive value.

Why do retail investors love institutional flow data? Because it is uniquely available in Taiwan — published daily for free (TWSE website, financial apps). This "free shortcut" is irresistibly attractive — but as Lo's AMH predicts, when too many people use the same information source, its profitability inevitably decays.

1.3 文獻回顧1.3 Literature Review

台灣市場的經典研究：Barber, Lee, Liu & Odean (2009) 利用台灣證券交易所的完整交易數據，發現台灣散戶每年因頻繁交易平均損失 3.8% 的報酬。這是全球少數能取得完整個人交易資料的研究之一，被發表在頂級期刊 Review of Financial Studies。其結論對「跟單」策略提出了根本性的質疑：如果散戶連自己做決定都在虧錢，靠跟單真的能翻盤嗎？

法人羊群效應：Lakonishok, Shleifer & Vishny (1992) 建立了衡量法人交易影響的 LSV 指標。Wermers (1999) 發現法人的羊群效應能加速價格向基本面調整——也就是說，法人「一窩蜂買」不一定是壞事，可能是市場在修正之前的錯誤定價。Sias (2004) 則證明法人之間確實存在相互跟隨的交易行為，這解釋了為什麼三大法人經常同買同賣。

動能效應：Grinblatt, Titman & Wermers (1995) 發現高達 77% 的基金經理人是動能投資者——也就是買近期上漲的股票、賣近期下跌的股票。這意味著法人的「買超」可能只是在追漲而非預測未來。如果法人只是用更大的資金在追漲，那跟著他們買就只是追漲的追漲。

資訊優勢半衰期：Yan & Zhang (2009) 在 Review of Financial Studies 發現，短線交易的法人比長線法人更具資訊優勢，但這種優勢衰減極快——通常在幾天內就消失。這對散戶跟單策略是壞消息：如果法人的優勢只存在於當天，而你隔天才知道，信號已經過期了。

台灣法人研究：Lu & Chen (2013) 研究台灣市場數據，發現法人的羊群交易確實包含資訊成分，但其預測力在公開揭露後大幅衰減。這與本研究的假說（H3）直接相關。

外資與市場穩定性：Choe, Kho & Stulz (1999) 以韓國市場為例，發現外資交易不一定會使市場更不穩定。Boyer & Zheng (2009) 則指出，法人資金流入與同期報酬高度正相關，但與未來報酬的關係較弱——再次暗示法人可能是追漲而非預測。

Classic Taiwan study: Barber, Lee, Liu & Odean (2009) used complete trading data from Taiwan Stock Exchange, finding retail investors lose 3.8% annually from frequent trading. Published in Review of Financial Studies, this raises fundamental questions about copy-trading strategies.

Institutional herding: LSV (1992) established institutional trading impact measures. Wermers (1999) found herding accelerates price adjustment toward fundamentals. Sias (2004) confirmed cross-institutional following, explaining why institutions often buy/sell together.

Momentum: Grinblatt, Titman & Wermers (1995) found 77% of fund managers are momentum investors — buying recent winners and selling recent losers. Institutional "buying" may just be chasing trends, not predicting.

Information decay: Yan & Zhang (2009, RFS) found short-horizon institutions have more information advantage, but it decays extremely quickly — usually within days. Bad news for copy-trading: if advantage only exists on day 0, and you learn on day 1, the signal has expired.

Taiwan institutional: Lu & Chen (2013) found institutional herding in Taiwan contains information, but predictive power decays significantly after public disclosure — directly related to our H3.

Foreign and stability: Choe, Kho & Stulz (1999) found foreign trading does not necessarily destabilize markets. Boyer & Zheng (2009) noted institutional flows are highly correlated with concurrent returns but weakly with future returns — again suggesting momentum rather than prediction.

1.4 研究假說1.4 Hypotheses

編號 假說 方法 為何重要
H1 法人淨買超後 T+1~T+60 存在正超額報酬 Event Study CAR + t-test 最基本的有效性檢驗——法人買超後市場真的會漲嗎？
H2 外資預測力 > 投信 > 自營商 分法人 CAR 比較 確定「跟誰」最有價值，避免把所有法人混為一談
H3 T+1 延遲後超額報酬大幅衰減 CAR 衰減分析 散戶最核心的實務問題——你隔天才知道，來得及嗎？
H4 連續買超天數增加，訊號強度遞增 Spearman 趨勢檢定 直覺上「連買多天」應該更可靠，但數據是否支持？

H1 是最基本的問題：法人大量買超之後，市場是否真的會漲？我們用事件研究法（Event Study）計算累積異常報酬（CAR），看看 Q5 買超後 1-60 天的實際表現。如果 H1 不成立，後面的假說就失去了討論基礎。

H2 探討三大法人之間的差異：一般認為外資（有全球視野和龐大研究團隊）的預測力最強，投信次之，自營商最弱。但這是否有數據支持？如果三類法人的效果沒有差異，代表買超信號可能不是來自資訊優勢，而是純粹的資金推動。

H3 是最關鍵的實務問題：即使法人信號有效，散戶隔天才看到資料——這一天的延遲會讓超額報酬消失多少？如果衰減超過 50%，代表散戶能捕捉到的部分已經非常有限。

H4 測試「信號強度」假說：如果法人連續多天買超同一方向，是否代表信號更可靠？直覺上，連續買超像是更強烈的「共識」。但如果連續天數增加反而報酬下降，可能代表趨勢即將反轉（「物極必反」）。

# Hypothesis Method Why It Matters
H1 Positive CAR after institutional net buy (T+1~T+60) Event Study + t-test Most basic effectiveness test - does the market rise after institutional buying?
H2 Foreign > Trust > Dealer predictive power Cross-institution CAR Determines "who" is most worth following
H3 T+1 delay significantly reduces CAR CAR decay analysis Core practical question - can you still profit one day late?
H4 Consecutive buy days strengthen signal Spearman trend test Intuitively "multi-day buying" should be more reliable - does data agree?

H1 is most fundamental: does the market rise after institutional heavy buying? If H1 fails, later hypotheses lose their foundation.

H2 explores institution differences: if no difference between types, the signal may come from capital push rather than information advantage.

H3 is the most critical practical question: if decay exceeds 50%, the portion retail can capture is already very limited.

H4 tests signal strength: if returns decline with more consecutive days, it may signal impending trend reversal ("what goes up must come down").

1.5 研究貢獻1.5 Research Contribution

本研究的主要貢獻包括：

  1. 嚴格的統計檢定：使用 Permutation Test（隨機排列檢定 10,000 次）作為因果推論工具，直接回答「法人信號是否優於隨機」。多數既有研究僅使用 t 檢定（假設常態分佈），而 Permutation Test 不需任何分佈假設，是最「乾淨」的檢驗方式。
  2. 資訊延遲的量化：首次精確量化 T+0 vs T+1 的 CAR 差異，為散戶提供明確的「資訊衰減率」數據。這是文獻中較少觸及但對實務最重要的面向。
  3. 多維度全面評估：結合事件研究、五分位分析、Granger 因果、牛熊分拆、連續天數等 6 種方法，從不同角度交叉驗證。任何單一方法可能有偏差，多方法交叉可提高結論可信度。
  4. 跨層級穩健性：同時分析市場層級（加權指數）與個股層級（台積電、鴻海），檢驗結論是否因分析層級不同而改變。
  5. 親民的表達：以日常生活比喻解釋統計概念，讓非專業讀者也能理解研究方法和結論。
  1. Rigorous statistical testing: 10,000-permutation test as causal inference tool — most existing studies only use t-tests (assuming normality). Permutation test is the "cleanest" approach.
  2. Information delay quantification: First precise quantification of T+0 vs T+1 CAR difference, providing retail investors with clear "information decay rate" data — rarely addressed in literature but practically most important.
  3. Multi-dimensional evaluation: 6 methods (event study, quintile, Granger, bull/bear, consecutive, permutation) for cross-validation from different angles, improving conclusion credibility.
  4. Cross-level robustness: Both market-level (TAIEX) and stock-level (TSMC, Hon Hai) analysis.
  5. Accessible communication: Everyday analogies for statistical concepts, enabling non-specialist understanding.
