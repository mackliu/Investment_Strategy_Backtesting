A.0 關於本研究A.0 About This Study

本研究屬於「用數據說話：投資策略回測研究」系列的第九項實驗（V-009）。整個系列共 13 項實驗，分別驗證市面上常見的投資論點。本實驗聚焦於台灣市場最普遍的散戶交易策略——跟著三大法人買賣超方向操作。

每天收盤後，法人買賣超數據是台灣財經媒體報導的頭條焦點，也是各大投資 App 的首頁重點。數以百萬計的散戶投資人依賴這些數據做出交易決策。然而，我們的分析顯示，這種廣泛被信奉的策略在實務上幾乎不可能獲利。希望本研究能幫助投資人用更科學的眼光審視這些看似「理所當然」的投資習慣。

所有程式碼和數據均可在 GitHub 上取得，確保研究的可重現性。

This study is Experiment V-009 in the "Let Data Speak: Investment Strategy Backtesting" series. The full series comprises 13 experiments testing common investment beliefs. This experiment focuses on the most prevalent retail trading strategy in Taiwan — following institutional net buy/sell direction.

Institutional flow data is the headline focus of Taiwan financial media after market close, and the front-page feature of major investment apps. Millions of retail investors rely on this data. Yet our analysis shows this widely believed strategy is practically unprofitable. We hope this helps investors evaluate these "obvious" investment habits more scientifically.

All code and data are available on GitHub for reproducibility.

A.3 散戶反向指標A.3 Retail Contrarian Indicator

由於台灣市場的散戶買賣超數據無法直接取得，我們以「法人淨賣超」作為「散戶淨買超」的近似值。下圖展示法人買超最少（= 推估散戶買超最多）到法人買超最多的各 quintile 後續報酬。

Since Taiwan retail net buy/sell data is not directly available, we approximate "retail net buy" as "institutional net sell." The chart shows forward returns from Q1 (least institutional buying = most retail buying, estimated) to Q5 (most institutional buying).

A.4 季節性分析A.4 Seasonal Analysis

法人買賣超存在季節性模式：例如年底外資可能因為年度結帳而調整部位，年初則可能重新配置。下圖顯示各月份的平均法人淨買超金額，可觀察是否有明顯的季節性規律。

Institutional net buy/sell shows seasonal patterns: for example, foreign investors may adjust positions for year-end closing, then reallocate at the start of the year. The chart shows average institutional net buy by month to observe seasonal regularities.
