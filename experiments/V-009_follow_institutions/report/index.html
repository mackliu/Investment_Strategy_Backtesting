V-009: 跟著法人買就對了V-009: Follow Institutional Investors

研究問題：追蹤法人（外資、投信、自營商）買賣超方向，散戶是否真能獲得超額報酬？法人資訊優勢在公開揭露後是否仍然存在？

Research Question: Can retail investors earn excess returns by following institutional investors (foreign, trust, dealer) net buy/sell signals? Does institutional information advantage persist after public disclosure?

「跟著法人走」是台灣散戶最熱門的交易策略之一——每天收盤後，無數投資人緊盯三大法人買賣超數據，希望藉此「搭順風車」獲利。但這真的有效嗎？用日常生活來比喻，這就像看到隔壁桌的美食家點了什麼菜，自己也跟著點——問題是，你看到他點菜時，菜已經在做了（資訊延遲），而且全餐廳的人都在學他點（信號擁擠）。

本研究以 2013-2025 年台灣加權指數與三大法人買賣超數據，檢驗法人淨買超的預測能力。三大法人合計淨買超 Q5 在 T+5 日的 CAR 為 0.281%。外資 CAR = 0.365%，投信 = 0.214%，自營商 = 0.253%。

"Follow the institutions" is one of the most popular retail trading strategies in Taiwan — every day after market close, countless investors watch institutional net buy/sell data, hoping to "ride the wave." But does it really work? Think of it like watching a food critic order at the next table and copying their order — the problem is, by the time you see what they ordered, the food is already being prepared (information delay), and everyone else in the restaurant is copying too (signal crowding).

Using 2013-2025 TAIEX and institutional net buy/sell data, this study tests institutional predictive power. Total institutional Q5 net buy CAR at T+5 is 0.281%. Foreign = 0.365%, Trust = 0.214%, Dealer = 0.253%.

上圖展示三大法人每日淨買賣超與加權指數的走勢。法人買賣超方向往往與大盤走勢同步——但究竟是法人帶動市場（預測力），還是市場帶動法人交易（追漲殺跌）？這正是本研究要釐清的核心問題。

The chart shows daily institutional net buy/sell alongside TAIEX. Institutional flow often moves with the market — but does institutional flow drive the market (predictive power), or does the market drive institutional trading (momentum)? This is the core question.

主要發現Key Findings

  1. 法人買超有微弱預測力但不顯著：Q5 淨買超 T+5 日 CAR = 0.281%（t = 3.48，p = 0.0005），但 Permutation 檢定 p = 0.2627，無法排除隨機性。Cohen's d = 0.139（小效果量），經濟意義有限。
  2. 資訊衰減 88%：T+0（假設同日知道）CAR = 1.001%，T+1（隔日公布）僅 0.118%——超額報酬的 88% 在當天就已實現。散戶拿到的是「過期的入場券」。
  3. 牛熊市完全反轉：牛市 T+5 CAR = +0.624%（勝率 65.6%），熊市 = -0.925%（勝率 39.2%）。法人買超在熊市中反而預測下跌——這不是預測力，而是動能效應。
  4. 連續買超的反直覺發現：連續 1 天買超後 T+5 報酬 = 0.219%，但連續 10 天後反而為 -0.232%——「物極必反」，連續買超越久不代表越好。
  5. 散戶是可靠的反向指標：Q1（法人賣超最多 = 散戶買超最多推估）後續報酬最差，Q5（法人買超最多）最佳——印證 Barber et al. (2009) 散戶年虧 3.8% 的發現。
  1. Weak, insignificant predictive power: Q5 net buy T+5 CAR = 0.281% (t = 3.48, p = 0.0005), but Permutation test p = 0.2627, cannot rule out randomness. Cohen's d = 0.139 (small effect).
  2. Information decay 88%: T+0 (hypothetical same-day) CAR = 1.001%, T+1 (next-day publication) only 0.118% - 88% of excess return realized on day 0. Retail gets an "expired ticket."
  3. Bull/bear complete reversal: Bull market T+5 CAR = +0.624% (win rate 65.6%), bear market = -0.925% (win rate 39.2%). Institutional buying in bear markets actually predicts decline - not prediction, but momentum.
  4. Consecutive buy paradox: 1-day consecutive buy T+5 return = 0.219%, but 10-day consecutive = -0.232% - "what goes up must come down," longer streaks are not better.
  5. Retail as reliable contrarian: Q1 (most institutional selling = most retail buying) has worst forward returns; Q5 (most institutional buying) has best - confirming Barber et al. (2009) finding of retail losing 3.8% annually.
