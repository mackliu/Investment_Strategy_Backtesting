V-011：加密貨幣是數位黃金V-011: Cryptocurrency Is Digital Gold

結論Conclusion

回到最初的研究問題：比特幣真的是「數位黃金」嗎？經過對三個關鍵維度的系統性檢驗——避險能力、抗通膨特性、投組分散效果——數據給出了清晰而一致的答案。

Returning to our original research question: is Bitcoin truly 'digital gold'? After systematically examining three key dimensions -- hedging ability, inflation protection, and portfolio diversification -- the data provides a clear and consistent answer.

本研究以 2014-10 至 2025-11 共約 11 年的數據，從避險、抗通膨、投組分散三個維度檢驗「比特幣是數位黃金」的論述。主要結論如下：

  1. BTC 不是避險資產：在股市壓力日，BTC 與股市同向下跌。BTC-黃金相關性不穩定，無法提供一致的避險保護。（H1/H2 結果）
  2. BTC 不具抗通膨特性：BTC 報酬與 CPI 變化的相關性接近零，OLS 迴歸無顯著關聯。BTC 的價格由加密特有因子驅動。（H3 結果）
  3. 小量配置有分散效果但增加尾部風險：在 60/40 投組加入 1-5% BTC，Sharpe 從 0.769 變為 0.990，但最大回撤惡化至 -27.8%。（H4 結果）
  4. 機構化加劇同步性：2021 年後，BTC 與 SPY 的相關性顯著上升，BTC 越來越像「高 beta 科技股」而非「數位黃金」。
  1. BTC is not a hedge: During stock stress days, BTC falls alongside stocks. BTC-Gold correlation is unstable, offering no consistent hedging protection. (H1/H2 results)
  2. BTC does not hedge inflation: Near-zero correlation between BTC returns and CPI changes, no significant OLS relationship. BTC price is driven by crypto-specific factors. (H3 results)
  3. Small allocation diversifies but adds tail risk: Adding 1-5% BTC to 60/40, Sharpe changes from 0.769 to 0.990, but max drawdown worsens to -27.8%. (H4 results)
  4. Institutionalization increases co-movement: Post-2021, BTC-SPY correlation rises significantly, BTC increasingly resembles a "high-beta tech stock" rather than "digital gold."

結論：「數位黃金」是一個有吸引力但經不起數據檢驗的論述。BTC 是一種高報酬、高風險的投機性資產，具有獨特的非對稱報酬特性，但不應被視為黃金的替代品。投資者若以「避險」或「保值」為目的配置 BTC，很可能會失望。

Conclusion: "Digital gold" is an attractive but empirically unsupported narrative. BTC is a high-return, high-risk speculative asset with unique asymmetric return properties, but should not be considered a gold substitute. Investors allocating BTC for "hedging" or "preservation" are likely to be disappointed.

未來研究方向Future Research Directions

  • ETF 效應：BTC 現貨 ETF 上市後（2024 年 1 月），機構化對 BTC 統計特性的影響？ETF 資金流入是否進一步增強 BTC 與股市的連動性，削弱其分散效果？
  • 半減期因果：BTC 半減期（halving）周期與價格的因果關係是否可以用 Granger 因果檢定驗證？還是「半減期利多」只是一種自我實現的預言？
  • DeFi 避險潛力：DeFi 資產（如 ETH staking yield、穩定幣收益）是否提供更好的通膨對沖？其收益率是否與通膨有更穩定的正相關？
  • 跨國貨幣環境：在不同貨幣環境下（美元 vs 日圓 vs 新台幣計價），BTC 的避險特性是否有差異？對於台灣投資者而言，匯率波動是否改變了 BTC 的風險-報酬特性？
  • 極端事件擴展：納入更多加密貨幣市場特有危機（如 2023 年矽谷銀行倒閉、2024 年中東衝突），系統性比較 BTC 和黃金在不同類型危機中的表現差異。
  • ETF Effect: How does the BTC spot ETF launch (Jan 2024) affect BTC statistical properties? Do ETF inflows further strengthen BTC-stock co-movement, weakening diversification benefits?
  • Halving Causality: Can the BTC halving cycle-price relationship be validated with Granger causality tests? Or is the 'halving bullish' narrative just a self-fulfilling prophecy?
  • DeFi Hedging Potential: Do DeFi assets (ETH staking yield, stablecoin yields) offer better inflation hedging? Is their yield more stably correlated with inflation?
  • Cross-Currency Environments: Does BTC hedging ability differ across currency environments (USD vs JPY vs TWD)? For Taiwanese investors, does exchange rate volatility alter BTC's risk-return characteristics?
  • Extended Extreme Events: Include more crypto-specific crises (2023 SVB collapse, 2024 Middle East conflict) to systematically compare BTC and gold performance across different crisis types.
