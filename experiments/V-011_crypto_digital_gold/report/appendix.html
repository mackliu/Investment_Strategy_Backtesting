A.3 正態性檢定A.3 Normality Tests

為什麼要檢定正態性：許多統計方法（如 t 檢定、OLS 迴歸）假設資料呈常態分布。如果日報酬不服從常態分布（特別是有「肥尾」現象），用傳統方法可能低估極端風險。這就是為什麼我們在投組分析中使用 Block Bootstrap——它不假設任何特定分布。

結果：所有資產的日報酬均不服從常態分布（Jarque-Bera p < 0.001），BTC 的峰態和偏態最為極端。這意味著 BTC 的「肥尾」效應最嚴重——極端暴跌（或暴漲）的頻率遠高於鐘型曲線的預測。在風險管理上，這代表「以為 BTC 單日最大虧損不會超過 X%」的直覺往往過於樂觀。

Why test normality: Many statistical methods (t-tests, OLS regression) assume normal distributions. If daily returns are non-normal (especially with 'fat tails'), traditional methods may underestimate extreme risks. This is why we use Block Bootstrap in portfolio analysis -- it assumes no specific distribution.

Results: All assets' daily returns reject normality (Jarque-Bera p < 0.001), with BTC showing the most extreme kurtosis and skewness. BTC's 'fat tail' effect is most severe -- extreme crashes (or surges) occur far more often than a bell curve predicts. For risk management, this means the intuition that 'BTC's max single-day loss will not exceed X%' is often too optimistic.
