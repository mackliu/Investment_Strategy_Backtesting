V-011: 加密貨幣是數位黃金V-011: Cryptocurrency Is Digital Gold

研究問題：比特幣真的是「數位黃金」嗎？它是否具備黃金的避險與抗通膨特性？加入投資組合是否有助於分散風險？

Research Question: Is Bitcoin truly "digital gold"? Does it share gold's hedging and inflation-protection properties? Does adding it to a portfolio help diversify risk?

本研究以 2014-10 至 2025-11 的數據，從三個維度檢驗「數位黃金」論述：(1) 避險能力、(2) 抗通膨特性、(3) 投組分散效果。

BTC 年化報酬 40.2% 遠超黃金（7.7%）與 S&P 500（9.3%），但年化波動度高達 56.0%（黃金 12.3%、SPY 14.7%），最大回撤 -83.4%。BTC 是高報酬高風險資產，而非穩定的避風港。

Using data from 2014-10 to 2025-11, this study examines the "digital gold" thesis across three dimensions: (1) hedging ability, (2) inflation protection, (3) portfolio diversification.

BTC annualized return of 40.2% far exceeds gold (7.7%) and S&P 500 (9.3%), but annualized volatility reaches 56.0% (gold 12.3%, SPY 14.7%), with max drawdown of -83.4%. BTC is a high-return, high-risk asset, not a stable safe haven.

上圖以對數尺度呈現 BTC、黃金、S&P 500 與美國長期公債的累積報酬。BTC 的成長幅度遠超其他資產，但波動劇烈，經歷多次 50% 以上的回撤。

The chart shows cumulative returns of BTC, Gold, S&P 500, and TLT on log scale. BTC growth far exceeds other assets but with extreme volatility, experiencing multiple 50%+ drawdowns.

主要發現Key Findings

  • 非避險資產：BTC 在股市崩盤日與股市同向下跌，與黃金的避險行為截然不同。
  • 相關性不穩定：BTC 與黃金的相關性在不同時期劇烈變化，危機時並未呈現「避風港」特徵。
  • 抗通膨薄弱：BTC 報酬與 CPI 變化的相關性接近零，不具有效的通膨對沖能力。
  • 小量配置有分散效果：在 60/40 投組中加入 5% BTC，Sharpe 從 0.769 變為 0.990，但最大回撤從惡化至 -27.8%。
  • Not a hedge: BTC drops alongside stocks during market crashes, opposite to gold's hedging behavior.
  • Unstable correlation: BTC-Gold correlation varies dramatically across periods, showing no safe-haven trait during crises.
  • Weak inflation hedge: BTC returns show near-zero correlation with CPI changes.
  • Small allocation diversifies: Adding 5% BTC to 60/40 changes Sharpe from 0.769 to 0.990, but max drawdown worsens to -27.8%.
