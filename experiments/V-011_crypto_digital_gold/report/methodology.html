加密貨幣全年無休交易（24/7），但本研究以 UTC 日收盤價為準，並與傳統金融市場交易日對齊。缺失值採前向填充（forward fill）處理。ETH 起始日期較晚（約 2017 年 11 月），因此部分分析僅涵蓋 BTC。

數據品質控管：加密貨幣早期數據品質參差不齊——2014 年前各交易所價差可達數百美元，就像同一款球鞋在不同店面的標價相差三成。本研究選擇 2014 年 10 月作為起始點，因為此時 BTC 已有較成熟的交易所基礎設施（Coinbase、Bitstamp 等），價格數據的可靠性大幅提升。yfinance 提供的 BTC-USD 為多交易所加權平均價格，而非單一交易所報價。此外，我們對所有資產進行了缺失值檢查、異常報酬篩選（日報酬超過 50% 的極端值標記），以及前後資料源交叉驗證，確保分析基礎的穩固。

Cryptocurrencies trade 24/7, but this study uses UTC daily close prices aligned with traditional market trading days. Missing values are forward-filled. ETH starts later (~Nov 2017), so some analyses cover only BTC.

Data Quality Control: Early crypto data quality varies significantly -- pre-2014 price discrepancies between exchanges could reach hundreds of dollars, like the same sneakers being priced 30% differently across stores. We chose October 2014 as the start date because BTC had developed more mature exchange infrastructure (Coinbase, Bitstamp) by then, greatly improving data reliability. yfinance provides BTC-USD as a multi-exchange weighted average, not a single exchange quote. We also performed missing value checks, extreme return screening (daily returns exceeding 50%), and cross-source validation for all assets.

2.2 時期劃分2.2 Sub-Period Definition

加密貨幣市場在研究期間經歷了巨大的結構性變化，因此本研究將數據劃分為三個階段——就像研究一個人的成長，把幼年、青春期、成年分開來看，才能理解行為模式的演變：

  • 早期（2014-2017）：散戶主導，交易所基礎設施不成熟，BTC 從 <$500 漲至近 $20,000。這個階段像是「蠻荒西部」——缺乏監管、市場操縱常見、交易所安全事件頻傳（如 Mt.Gox 破產）。
  • 過渡期（2018-2020）：ICO 泡沫破裂、監管加強、COVID 崩盤、CME 期貨上線。市場開始「成熟化」——泡沫清洗淘汰了不良項目，衍生品市場的建立為機構提供了對沖工具。
  • 機構化（2021-2025）：特斯拉、MicroStrategy 大舉買入 BTC，BTC 現貨 ETF 獲批，機構資金大量流入。BTC 的持有者結構從散戶轉向機構，市場行為模式也隨之改變——波動度下降但與傳統資產的連動性上升。

為什麼需要分期：一個從「極客玩具」演變為「機構資產」的市場，其統計特性不可能保持不變。若只看全期平均數據，可能得出「BTC 與黃金無關」的結論，卻忽略了「機構化後兩者關係可能正在改變」的重要趨勢。分期分析讓我們捕捉到這些結構性轉變。

The crypto market underwent massive structural changes during the study period, so we divide the data into three phases -- like studying a person's growth by separating childhood, adolescence, and adulthood to understand behavioral evolution:

  • Early (2014-2017): Retail-dominated, immature exchange infrastructure, BTC rose from <$500 to ~$20,000. This phase was like the 'Wild West' -- minimal regulation, frequent market manipulation, and exchange security incidents (e.g., Mt.Gox bankruptcy).
  • Transition (2018-2020): ICO bubble burst, regulatory tightening, COVID crash, CME futures launch. The market began 'maturing' -- bubble cleansing eliminated bad projects, and derivatives markets gave institutions hedging tools.
  • Institutional (2021-2025): Tesla/MicroStrategy bought BTC heavily, spot BTC ETF approved, institutional capital inflows. BTC holder composition shifted from retail to institutional, changing market behavior -- volatility decreased but correlation with traditional assets increased.

Why sub-periods matter: A market that evolved from 'geek toy' to 'institutional asset' cannot have constant statistical properties. Looking only at full-period averages might conclude 'BTC is unrelated to gold' while missing the important trend that 'their relationship may be changing post-institutionalization.' Sub-period analysis captures these structural shifts.

2.3 分析方法2.3 Analysis Methods

為什麼需要多維度分析：僅看報酬率無法全面評估「數位黃金」論述——就像不能只看一個人的跑步速度就說他是「田徑選手」，還要看跳高、投擲等全能表現。真正的黃金替代品需要在避險、抗通膨、投組分散三個維度都表現良好。

  • 相關性分析：滾動相關係數（90 天窗口）+ Fisher z-transform 檢定危機/正常期差異。相關性就像兩人的步調——黃金與股市「反向而行」（危機時黃金漲），BTC 是否也如此？90 天窗口讓我們觀察這個關係隨時間的變化。
  • 條件報酬分析：在 SPY 日跌幅超過 2% 的日子，比較各資產報酬（Baur & Lucey 2010 框架）。這是「壓力測試」——像地震演練，看各資產在「最需要保護的時刻」表現如何。
  • 通膨迴歸：OLS 迴歸分析 BTC 月報酬與 CPI 年增率的關係。如果 BTC 真能抗通膨，通膨上升時 BTC 報酬應該也跟著上升，就像金價隨物價上漲一樣。
  • 投組建構：在 60/40 基準上分別加入 1%/2%/5% BTC，以 Bootstrap 產生 Sharpe 與 MaxDD 的信賴區間。這回答了最實際的問題：「到底該放多少 BTC 到投資組合裡？」

Why multi-dimensional analysis: Looking at returns alone cannot fully evaluate the 'digital gold' thesis -- just as you cannot call someone an 'athlete' based solely on running speed; you also need to check jumping and throwing. A true gold substitute must perform well across hedging, inflation protection, and portfolio diversification.

  • Correlation analysis: 90-day rolling correlation + Fisher z-transform to test crisis vs normal differences. Correlation is like two people's pace -- gold 'walks opposite' to stocks (rises during crises). Does BTC do the same? The 90-day window reveals how this relationship evolves over time.
  • Conditional returns: Asset returns on SPY >2% down days (Baur & Lucey 2010 framework). This is a 'stress test' -- like an earthquake drill, examining how each asset performs when protection is most needed.
  • Inflation regression: OLS regression of BTC monthly returns on CPI YoY change. If BTC truly hedges inflation, BTC returns should rise with inflation, just as gold prices rise with the price level.
  • Portfolio construction: Adding 1%/2%/5% BTC to 60/40 base, Bootstrap CI for Sharpe and MaxDD. This answers the most practical question: 'How much BTC should I actually put in my portfolio?'

2.4 統計方法2.4 Statistical Methods

  • Fisher z-transform：將相關係數轉換為常態分布，以檢定兩組相關係數是否有顯著差異。適用於 H1 的危機/正常期相關性比較。
  • One-sample t-test：檢定 BTC 在市場壓力日的平均報酬是否顯著不為零。
  • OLS 迴歸：估計 BTC 報酬對通膨變化的敏感度（beta），並以 R-squared 衡量解釋力。
  • Block Bootstrap：保留時間序列結構的重抽樣法（10,000 次），產生 Sharpe 與 MaxDD 的 95% 信賴區間。
  • 顯著水準：alpha = 0.05，所有檢定均報告效果量（Cohen's d 或 r）。
  • Fisher z-transform: Converts correlations to normal distribution to test whether two correlations differ significantly.
  • One-sample t-test: Tests whether BTC mean return on stress days is significantly different from zero.
  • OLS Regression: Estimates BTC return sensitivity to inflation (beta), with R-squared for explanatory power.
  • Block Bootstrap: Resampling (10,000 iterations) preserving time-series structure for Sharpe and MaxDD CIs.
  • Significance level: alpha = 0.05, all tests report effect sizes (Cohen's d or r).

2.5 交易成本與再平衡假設2.5 Transaction Cost & Rebalancing Assumptions

投資組合分析採用以下交易成本假設：加密貨幣交易手續費 0.1%（主流交易所如 Binance 的 maker/taker 費率），傳統 ETF 交易手續費依美股券商標準（趨近零佣金）。再平衡頻率為年度（每年 1 月第一個交易日），偏離目標權重超過 5% 時觸發額外再平衡。

本研究未計入以下成本：(1) 加密貨幣出入金手續費（約 0.5-1%）；(2) 台幣兌美元匯兌成本；(3) 加密貨幣的資本利得稅（台灣目前尚無明確規範）。因此，實際執行成本可能高於模擬結果，特別是小額投資者。

Portfolio analysis assumes the following transaction costs: crypto trading fees of 0.1% (major exchange maker/taker rates), traditional ETF fees per US brokerage standards (near-zero commission). Rebalancing is annual (first trading day each January), with additional rebalancing triggered when weights deviate more than 5% from targets.

This study does not include: (1) crypto on/off-ramp fees (~0.5-1%); (2) TWD/USD exchange costs; (3) crypto capital gains tax (currently undefined in Taiwan). Actual execution costs may exceed simulation results, especially for small investors.
