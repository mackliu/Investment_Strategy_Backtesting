V-008：房地產是最好的投資？V-008: Is Real Estate the Best Investment?

研究問題：房地產真的是最好的投資嗎？計入持有成本、交易成本、槓桿效應後，買房的真實報酬率為何？和股市相比又如何？

Research Question: Is real estate truly the best investment? What is the real return after accounting for holding costs, transaction costs, and leverage? How does it compare to the stock market?

「有土斯有財」是台灣根深蒂固的觀念——許多長輩視買房為人生最重要的理財決策，認為房價「只會漲不會跌」。這個觀念有多深入人心？根據內政部統計，台灣自有住宅率高達約 85%，遠超多數已開發國家。然而，當我們用數據檢視這個信仰時，故事可能和你想像的不太一樣。

本研究以信義房價指數（估計值）與 0050 ETF 進行全面比較，涵蓋 2001 年至 2025 年的數據。全國房價指數名目年化報酬約 6.6%，但扣除每年 1.5% 的持有成本（房屋稅、管理費、維修費等）後，淨報酬降至 5.1%；同期 0050 年化報酬為 11.3%。用生活化的比喻來說：買房就像開一家店，營業額（房價漲幅）看起來不錯，但扣掉房租、水電、人事等固定開銷（持有成本）後，淨利潤可能遠不如預期。

房價波動度（3.5%）雖低於股票（18.6%），但這主要是因為房價以季度計算、交易不頻繁，真實風險被平滑處理所致。槓桿（房貸）可將房產報酬放大至 17.4%，但同時也放大了風險——就像用信用卡投資，賺的時候很開心，跌的時候損失加倍。

"Owning land is owning wealth" is a deeply rooted belief in Taiwan — many elders view homeownership as the most important financial decision, believing prices "can only go up." With homeownership at ~85%, this belief runs deep. But when we examine it with data, the story may differ from expectations.

This study compares the Sinyi House Price Index (estimated) with the 0050 ETF, covering 2001-2025. The national HPI nominal annualized return is approximately 6.6%, but after deducting 1.5% annual holding costs (property tax, management fees, maintenance), net return drops to 5.1%. Meanwhile, 0050 returned 11.3% annually. Think of it like running a shop — revenue (price gains) looks good, but after rent, utilities, and labor (holding costs), net profit may disappoint.

While housing volatility (3.5%) is lower than stocks (18.6%), this is largely because housing is measured quarterly and trades infrequently, smoothing away true risk. Leverage (mortgage) can amplify housing returns to 17.4%, but also amplifies risk — like investing on credit cards: great when it goes up, devastating when it falls.

上圖以對數尺度比較房價指數、0050 ETF 與 CPI 的累積成長。對數尺度的好處是讓倍數成長更容易比較——每格等距代表相同的倍率增長。可以明顯看到 0050 的成長幅度遠超房價指數，而房價指數則僅略高於 CPI（通膨）。這意味著房地產扣除通膨後的「實質」報酬其實相當有限。

The chart above compares house price indices, 0050 ETF, and CPI on a log scale. Log scale makes proportional growth easier to compare — equal spacing means equal growth multiples. 0050 growth clearly far exceeds housing, while housing barely outpaces CPI (inflation). This suggests that real (inflation-adjusted) housing returns are actually quite modest.

主要發現Key Findings

  • 持有成本侵蝕報酬：每年 1.5% 的持有成本將房地產名目報酬 6.6% 降至淨報酬 5.1%。這就像一杯看起來滿的果汁，喝了才發現三分之一是冰塊。
  • 股市長期勝出：0050 年化 11.3% 明顯優於房地產淨報酬，而且流動性高、交易成本低、分散風險更佳。
  • 槓桿是雙面刃：房貸將報酬放大至 17.4%，但波動度也成倍增加。許多人把「借錢賺的錢」誤認為「房地產本身很會漲」。
  • 租 vs 買視情況而定：10 年期買房勝率 55%，20 年期 44%。答案取決於房價漲幅、利率、租金水準等多重因素。
  • Holding costs erode returns: Annual 1.5% costs reduce nominal 6.6% to net 5.1%. Like a full glass of juice that turns out to be one-third ice.
  • Stocks win long-term: 0050 at 11.3% clearly outperforms net housing returns, with higher liquidity, lower costs, and better diversification.
  • Leverage cuts both ways: Mortgage amplifies returns to 17.4% but also amplifies volatility. Many mistake "money made from borrowing" for "real estate being a great investment."
  • Rent vs buy depends: 10-year buy win rate: 55%, 20-year: 44%. The answer depends on price growth, interest rates, rent levels, and more.
