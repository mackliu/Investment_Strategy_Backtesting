V-013：4% 提領法則真的安全嗎？V-013: Is the 4% Rule Really Safe?

研究問題：「每年提領退休金的 4%，錢 30 年花不完」——這個源自 1998 年 Trinity Study 的黃金法則，在不同市場環境、不同提領策略下，真的安全嗎？本研究以美國 150 年 + 台灣 22 年數據全面檢驗。

Research Question: "Withdraw 4% annually and your money lasts 30 years" — this golden rule from the 1998 Trinity Study — is it truly safe across different markets and strategies? We test with 150 years of US + 22 years of Taiwan data.

摘要

4% 提領法則是退休理財中最廣為人知的經驗法則：退休時將年支出設為投資組合的 4%，之後每年依通膨調整提領金額，理論上資金可撐過 30 年退休期。這個源自 1998 年 Trinity Study 的結論，基於美國 1926-1995 年的歷史數據，至今仍被全球投資人奉為準則。

然而，隨著 2000 年代以來低利率環境、市場波動加劇，以及台灣投資人盲目套用美國經驗法則的風潮，我們有必要重新檢視這個「黃金法則」的適用條件與限制。

本研究以 Shiller CAPE Dataset（1871 年至今，涵蓋近 98 年的美國股債市場數據）及台灣 0050/0056 ETF 歷史數據為基礎，透過歷史路徑分析與 Block Bootstrap 模擬（10,000 次），系統性檢驗不同投資組合（100% 股票、60/40 股債配置）、不同提領率（2%-6%）、不同提領策略（固定金額、固定比例、動態調整 Guardrails）的 30 年存活率。同時，我們首次以相同方法驗證台灣市場的安全提領率，並比較市值型 ETF（0050）與高股息 ETF（0056）在退休提領情境下的表現差異。

Abstract

The 4% rule is the most widely cited guideline for retirement spending: set your annual withdrawal at 4% of initial portfolio value, adjust for inflation each year, and the money should last 30 years. This conclusion, derived from the 1998 Trinity Study using US data from 1926-1995, remains a cornerstone of retirement planning worldwide.

However, the post-2000 low-interest-rate environment, increased market volatility, and the trend among Taiwan investors to blindly apply US rules of thumb demand a rigorous re-examination of this "golden rule."

Using the Shiller CAPE Dataset (1871-present, covering ~98 years of US stock/bond data) and Taiwan 0050/0056 ETF history, we conduct historical path analysis and Block Bootstrap simulations (10,000 iterations) to systematically test 30-year survival rates across different portfolios (100% stock, 60/40 stock/bond), withdrawal rates (2%-6%), and strategies (fixed amount, fixed ratio, dynamic guardrails). We also provide the first comparable analysis for Taiwan and compare broad-market (0050) vs. high-dividend (0056) ETFs for retirement withdrawal.

上圖為美國市場以 Block Bootstrap（10,000 次模擬）估計的不同提領率 30 年存活率。每一個點代表在特定提領率下，退休投資組合在 30 年後仍有正餘額的機率。60/40 配置在 4% 提領率下存活率為 90.2%，100% 股票為 88.1%。隨著提領率上升，存活率急速下降——在 6% 提領率下，100% 股票組合的存活率僅剩 67.3%。可以明顯看出，提領率每增加 1 個百分點，破產風險就大幅攀升。

The chart above shows the 30-year survival rates from Block Bootstrap (10,000 simulations) across different withdrawal rates for the US market. Each point represents the probability of maintaining a positive balance after 30 years. At the benchmark 4% rate, 60/40 achieves 90.2% survival while 100% stock reaches 88.1%. As withdrawal rates increase, survival drops sharply — at 6%, the 100% stock portfolio survives only 67.3% of the time. Each additional percentage point in withdrawal rate dramatically increases ruin probability.

主要發現Key Findings

  1. 美國 4% 法則大致成立，但非萬無一失。60/40 配置 4% 存活率 90.2%（Bootstrap）/ 94.1%（歷史路徑），接近但未達 Trinity Study 的 95% 門檻。安全提領率（SWR，定義為存活率 >= 95% 的最高提領率）約 3.0%，低於傳統的 4%。即使是最「安全」的 60/40 配置，在 4% 提領下仍有約 10% 的破產機率，意味著每 10 位退休者就有約 1 位可能在 30 年內耗盡資金。
  2. 台灣市場安全提領率須更保守。0050 以 4% 提領的 30 年 Bootstrap 存活率為 94.9%（p=0.3294，無法拒絕 >= 95%，但樣本期間僅 22 年，信心有限）。台灣市場波動度更高（0050 年化波動 0.0% vs 美股 0.2%），建議採用 3-3.5% 為安全提領率。
  3. 動態調整策略是最關鍵的改進。Guardrails 策略（市場好時多提 120%、差時少提 80%）將存活率從固定金額的 89.9% 提升至 94.4%（差距 4.58 百分點，p<0.001）。固定比例策略雖然數學上永不歸零（存活率 100%），但退休後期的實際提領金額可能低至每月 30,532 元。
  4. 0050 vs 0056：高股息 ETF 並無退休提領優勢。0050 存活率 97.1%，0056 為 95.8%（差距 1.28 百分點，p=0.0006，0050 顯著較佳）。「靠配息退休」的心理安慰並不等於更高的存活機率。
  5. The US 4% rule roughly holds, but is not foolproof. 60/40 at 4%: 90.2% survival (Bootstrap) / 94.1% (historical), close to but below Trinity Study threshold of 95%. SWR (highest rate with >= 95% survival) is ~3.0%, below the traditional 4%. Even the "safest" 60/40 allocation has ~10% ruin probability at 4% — roughly 1 in 10 retirees could deplete their funds within 30 years.
  6. Taiwan requires a more conservative withdrawal rate. 0050 at 4%: 94.9% bootstrap survival (p=0.3294, cannot reject >= 95%, but only 22 years of data limits confidence). Taiwan market volatility is higher (0050 annualized vol 0.0% vs US stock 0.2%). Recommend 3-3.5% as the safe withdrawal rate.
  7. Dynamic guardrails are the most critical improvement. Guardrails (120% ceiling / 80% floor) boost survival from 89.9% (fixed amount) to 94.4% (+4.58 pp, p<0.001). Fixed ratio has 100% mathematical survival but minimum monthly withdrawal can drop to NTD 30,532.
  8. 0050 vs 0056: high-dividend ETF has no retirement advantage. 0050 survival: 97.1%, 0056: 95.8% (+1.28 pp, p=0.0006, 0050 significantly better). "Living off dividends" provides psychological comfort but not higher survival probability.

研究方法概述Methodology Overview

本研究採用兩種互補的分析方法：

  1. 歷史路徑分析：以美國 1871 年至今的 Shiller 數據，計算所有可能的 30 年退休窗口（共 68 條歷史路徑），還原每位「歷史上的退休者」實際會經歷的市場環境。
  2. Block Bootstrap 模擬：從歷史月報酬中以 12 個月為區塊隨機重抽，生成 10,000 條模擬路徑。相比歷史路徑的 68 條樣本，Bootstrap 能產生更多元的市場情境組合，提供更穩健的統計推論。

投資組合配置包含 100% 股票與 60/40 股債兩種，提領率測試範圍為 2%-6%，初始資本假設為 1000 萬元。三種提領策略分別模擬不同的退休金管理思維：固定金額（傳統 4% 法則）、固定比例（隨資產浮動）、動態調整 Guardrails（設上下限彈性提領）。

This study employs two complementary analytical approaches:

  1. Historical Path Analysis: Using Shiller data from 1871 to present, we compute all possible 30-year retirement windows (68 historical paths), replicating what each "historical retiree" would have experienced.
  2. Block Bootstrap Simulation: Random resampling from historical monthly returns in 12-month blocks, generating 10,000 simulated paths. Compared to 68 historical paths, bootstrap creates a richer set of market scenarios for more robust inference.

Portfolios tested include 100% stock and 60/40 stock/bond. Withdrawal rates range from 2%-6%, with initial capital of NTD 10000K. Three strategies represent different retirement spending philosophies: fixed amount (traditional 4% rule), fixed ratio (market-dependent), and dynamic guardrails (flexible with ceiling/floor).
