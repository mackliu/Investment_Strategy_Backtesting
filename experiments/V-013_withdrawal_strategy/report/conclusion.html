結論Conclusion

5.1 核心結論5.1 Key Conclusions

  1. 4% 法則在美國有條件成立，但並非絕對安全。以 150 年的 Shiller 數據進行 Bootstrap 模擬（10,000 次），60/40 配置在 4% 提領率下的 30 年存活率為 90.2%，100% 股票為 88.1%。安全提領率（SWR）約 3.0%（60/40）和 2.5%（100% 股票），均低於傳統的 4%。約每 10 位退休者就有 1 位可能在 30 年內耗盡資金。
  2. 台灣市場應採用更保守的提領率（3-3.5%）。0050 以 4% 提領的 Bootstrap 存活率為 94.9%（二項式檢定 p=0.3294，無法拒絕 >= 95%，但數據僅 22 年，統計檢定力有限）。台灣市場波動度更高（0.0% vs 美股 0.2%），且缺乏有效的債券配置選項，建議以 3-3.5% 為安全提領率出發點。
  3. 動態調整（Guardrails）是提升退休金壽命最有效的策略。僅需 20% 的支出彈性（市場好多提 120%，差少提 80%），就能將存活率從固定金額的 89.9% 提升至 94.4%（效果量 4.58 百分點，z=8.51，p<0.001）。這是四項檢定中效果最強的改進措施。
  4. 高股息 ETF（0056）在退休提領情境下無特殊優勢。0050 存活率 97.1% 顯著高於 0056 的 95.8%（p=0.0006），期末餘額中位數更是 0056 的近兩倍（13,135 萬 vs 7,322 萬）。「靠配息退休」的心理安慰不等於更高的退休安全性。
  5. 序列風險（Sequence-of-Returns Risk）是退休提領者的最大威脅。餘額路徑圖顯示，同樣的 4% 策略，最佳與最差情境的 30 年終值可相差超過 17802 倍。退休初期遭遇熊市的投資組合極易陷入「死亡螺旋」——資產縮水、提領佔比上升、加速消耗。動態調整是對抗序列風險最實際的工具。
  6. The 4% rule conditionally holds in the US, but is not absolutely safe. Bootstrap simulation (10,000 runs) with 150 years of Shiller data: 60/40 at 4% achieves 90.2% survival, 100% stock 88.1%. SWR is ~3.0% (60/40) and ~2.5% (100% stock), both below 4%. Roughly 1 in 10 retirees could deplete funds within 30 years.
  7. Taiwan should use a more conservative rate (3-3.5%). 0050 at 4%: 94.9% bootstrap survival (binomial p=0.3294, cannot reject >= 95%, but only 22 years of data limits power). Taiwan volatility (0.0% vs US 0.2%) and lack of effective bond allocation warrant a 3-3.5% starting point.
  8. Dynamic guardrails are the single most effective improvement. Just 20% spending flexibility (120% ceiling / 80% floor) raises survival from 89.9% to 94.4% (effect 4.58 pp, z=8.51, p<0.001) — the strongest effect among all four tests.
  9. High-dividend ETF (0056) has no retirement withdrawal advantage. 0050 survival (97.1%) significantly exceeds 0056 (95.8%), p=0.0006. Median terminal balance: 0050 at 13,1350K is nearly double 0056 at 7,3220K. "Living off dividends" provides psychological comfort, not higher retirement safety.
  10. Sequence-of-returns risk is the greatest threat to retirees. Balance paths show the same 4% strategy can produce terminal values differing by over 17802x between best and worst scenarios. Portfolios encountering early bear markets enter a "death spiral" — shrinking assets, rising withdrawal ratios, accelerating depletion. Dynamic guardrails are the most practical tool against sequence risk.

5.3 研究限制總結5.3 Limitations Summary

  • 台灣數據僅 22 年，Bootstrap 延伸至 30 年的結論需謹慎解讀。
  • 未計入交易成本、稅金及行為偏差。
  • 僅測試 30 年退休期間；FIRE 族群可能需要更長期間、更低提領率。
  • Bootstrap 可能低估極端序列風險。
  • 台灣市場結構（高集中度、地緣政治風險）與美國不同，直接比較需注意。
  • Taiwan data spans only 22 years; bootstrap to 30 years requires cautious interpretation.
  • Transaction costs, taxes, and behavioral biases not included.
  • Only 30-year horizons tested; FIRE retirees may need longer periods and lower rates.
  • Bootstrap may underestimate extreme sequence risk.
  • Taiwan market structure (high concentration, geopolitical risk) differs from the US; direct comparison requires care.

5.4 未來研究方向5.4 Future Research Directions

  1. 延長模擬期間至 40-50 年：針對 FIRE 族群的需求，測試超長期退休的安全提領率。預期 SWR 會進一步下降至 2.5-3%。
  2. 加入全球分散投資組合：以 MSCI World + Global Bond 建構投資組合，測試全球分散是否能提高台灣投資人的安全提領率。
  3. 納入交易成本與稅務：模擬實際的手續費、交易稅、以及可能的所得稅對存活率的影響。
  4. 行為因素模擬：加入「恐慌賣出」「過度消費」等行為偏差的隨機模型，評估行為因素對退休結果的影響程度。
  5. 台灣年金制度整合：將勞退/勞保年金納入模擬，評估公共年金搭配個人投資提領的整合退休方案。
  1. Extend to 40-50 year horizons: Test safe withdrawal rates for FIRE retirees with ultra-long retirement periods. SWR is expected to decrease further to 2.5-3%.
  2. Add globally diversified portfolios: Test MSCI World + Global Bond portfolios to see if global diversification improves safe withdrawal rates for Taiwan investors.
  3. Include transaction costs and taxes: Simulate the impact of actual commissions, transaction taxes, and potential income taxes on survival.
  4. Behavioral factor simulation: Add stochastic models for panic selling, overspending, and other behavioral biases to assess their impact on retirement outcomes.
  5. Taiwan pension system integration: Incorporate Labor Pension and Labor Insurance annuities to evaluate integrated retirement plans combining public pensions with private investment withdrawal.

5.5 一句話結論5.5 Bottom Line

4% 法則是一個有用的起點，但不是終點。台灣投資人應以 3-3.5% 為基準，採用動態調整策略，不要迷信「靠配息退休」，並定期檢視自己的退休計畫。退休理財不是「設定即忘記」——它需要持續的關注與適時的調整。

The 4% rule is a useful starting point, not the final answer. Taiwan investors should target 3-3.5%, adopt dynamic withdrawal strategies, avoid the "living off dividends" myth, and regularly review their retirement plan. Retirement finance is not "set and forget" — it requires ongoing attention and timely adjustment.

免責聲明：本研究使用歷史數據與 Bootstrap 模擬，結論為統計推論而非確定性預測。未來市場可能與歷史不同。本研究不構成投資建議。個人退休規劃應諮詢合格的財務顧問。
