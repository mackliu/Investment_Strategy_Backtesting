3.1 美國 Trinity Study 重現3.1 US Trinity Study Replication

我們首先以 Shiller CAPE Dataset 的長期數據重現 Trinity Study 的核心分析。下圖為歷史路徑分析結果：以每年為假想退休起點，計算不同投資組合在各提領率下的 30 年存活率。共有 68 條歷史路徑。

We first replicate the core Trinity Study analysis using the Shiller CAPE Dataset. The chart below shows historical path analysis: for each potential retirement start year, we compute 30-year survival rates across portfolios and withdrawal rates. A total of 68 historical paths are tested.

這張圖在看什麼：歷史路徑下不同提領率的 30 年存活率。

關鍵觀察：(1) 在 4% 提領率下，100% 股票的歷史存活率為 97.1%，高於 60/40 的 94.1%——這看似違反直覺，但因為歷史上美國股市長期報酬非常優異，純股票組合在多數歷史時期都能承受 4% 提領。(2) 隨著提領率上升至 5%，純股票的存活率急降至 80.9%，而 60/40 為 73.5%。(3) 歷史路徑的樣本量僅 68 條，統計推論力有限，因此我們補充 Bootstrap 模擬。

What this chart shows: Historical path 30-year survival rates by withdrawal rate.

Key observations: (1) At 4%, 100% stock historical survival (97.1%) exceeds 60/40 (94.1%) — counterintuitive, but the US stock market's exceptional long-term returns sustained pure equity portfolios in most historical periods. (2) At 5%, pure stock survival drops to 80.9%, while 60/40 is 73.5%. (3) Only 68 paths limits statistical power, so we supplement with bootstrap.

為比較歷史路徑與 Bootstrap 模擬的差異，下圖將兩種方法的 60/40 配置存活率並列：

To compare historical paths vs. bootstrap, the chart below juxtaposes 60/40 survival rates from both methods:

關鍵觀察：Bootstrap 模擬（10,000 次）的存活率普遍略低於歷史路徑——例如 60/40 在 4% 下，歷史為 94.1%，Bootstrap 為 90.2%。這是因為 Bootstrap 產生的隨機情境組合包含了比歷史上更極端的市場環境。Bootstrap 結果更具統計穩健性，後續分析主要以此為依據。

Key observation: Bootstrap (10,000 runs) survival rates are generally slightly lower — e.g., 60/40 at 4%: historical 94.1%, bootstrap 90.2%. Bootstrap generates more extreme scenarios than those observed historically. We primarily rely on bootstrap results for subsequent analysis.

3.2 安全提領率（SWR）3.2 Safe Withdrawal Rate

安全提領率（Safe Withdrawal Rate, SWR）定義為使 30 年存活率達到 95% 門檻的最高提領率。這是退休規劃中最關鍵的數字：提領率低於 SWR 時，退休金幾乎確定能撐過規劃期間。

The Safe Withdrawal Rate (SWR) is defined as the highest withdrawal rate achieving a 95% survival threshold over 30 years. This is the most critical number in retirement planning: rates below SWR virtually guarantee portfolio survival through the planning horizon.

這張圖在看什麼：SWR 曲線呈現每個提領率對應的存活率，與 95% 門檻的交叉點即為 SWR。

關鍵觀察：(1) 60/40 的 SWR 約 3.0%，100% 股票約 2.5%。兩者均低於傳統的 4%，與 Morningstar (2022) 下修至 3.3% 的方向一致。(2) 100% 股票的 SWR 反而低於 60/40，因為純股票的高波動性在提領階段是致命弱點——退休初期若遇到大幅下跌，提領又加速資產消耗，形成「死亡螺旋」。(3) 60/40 配置在低提領率（2%）時幾乎完美存活（99.8%），但在 6% 時存活率僅剩 55.9%。

對研究問題的意義：4% 法則所聲稱的「安全」提領率，在 Bootstrap 模擬下並非完全安全。保守的退休者應以 3.0% 為基準。

What this chart shows: The SWR curve maps each withdrawal rate to its survival rate; the intersection with the 95% threshold is the SWR.

Key observations: (1) 60/40 SWR is ~3.0%, 100% stock ~2.5%. Both are below the traditional 4%, consistent with Morningstar (2022) lowering to 3.3%. (2) 100% stock SWR is actually lower than 60/40, because pure equity volatility is a fatal weakness during withdrawal — early drawdowns compound with withdrawals into a "death spiral." (3) 60/40 at 2% achieves near-perfect survival (99.8%), but drops to 55.9% at 6%.

Implication: The "safe" rate claimed by the 4% rule is not entirely safe under bootstrap. Conservative retirees should target ~3.0%.

The heatmap below uses color to intuitively display survival rates across portfolio allocation and withdrawal rate combinations. Green indicates high survival (safe), red indicates low survival (dangerous), allowing retirement planners to instantly identify the safe zone.

關鍵觀察：(1) 左上角（低提領率 + 60/40）是最安全的區域，全部呈現深綠色。(2) 右下角（高提領率 + 100% 股票）是最危險的區域，逐漸轉為紅色。(3) 對角線上可以看到「安全與危險的分界線」大約落在 3-4% 提領率附近。(4) 在相同提領率下，60/40 的存活率始終高於 100% 股票，顯示退休提領階段的風險管理（加入債券）比追求最高報酬更重要。

(3) The "safe-to-dangerous boundary" falls around 3-4% withdrawal rate. (4) At any given rate, 60/40 consistently outperforms 100% stock, showing that risk management (adding bonds) matters more than maximizing returns during withdrawal.

3.4 餘額路徑3.4 Balance Paths

存活率是一個比例數字，但無法呈現退休過程中餘額的「動態故事」。下圖以 60/40 配置 4% 提領率為例，展示 50 條 Bootstrap 模擬路徑的餘額變化軌跡，讓我們看到每位「虛擬退休者」在 30 年間可能經歷的完全不同命運。

深色粗線為中位數路徑，藍色帶狀區域分別代表 25~75 與 5~95 百分位範圍，橘色細線為 5 條代表性個別路徑。

Survival rates are proportions that cannot show the "dynamic story" of retirement balance evolution. The chart below illustrates 50 bootstrap paths for a 60/40 portfolio withdrawing 4%, revealing the vastly different fates possible for each "virtual retiree" over 30 years.

The dark line is the median, blue bands show the 25th-75th and 5th-95th percentile ranges, and orange lines are 5 representative individual paths.

關鍵觀察：

  • 中位數路徑持續成長：30 年後中位數餘額為 39 百萬，遠高於初始的 10 百萬（1000 萬）。這意味著在「典型」的市場情境下，4% 提領率不但沒有花光退休金，反而留下了可觀的遺產。
  • 路徑分散巨大：最佳 5% 情境終值可達 178 百萬，但最差 5% 情境僅剩 0 百萬，甚至提前耗盡。同樣的策略、同樣的提領率，結果可以相差超過 17802 倍。
  • 路徑分岔的關鍵時點：仔細觀察可發現，路徑在退休後的前 5-10 年就開始明顯分岔。退休初期遇到牛市的路徑（橘色上行線）越滾越大、提領壓力相對越來越輕；退休初期遇到熊市的路徑（橘色下行線）則急劇下滑，一旦滑落到某個臨界點就難以翻身。
  • 破產路徑：50 條路徑中有 4 條在 30 年內歸零（8%），這些路徑在觸底歸零前通常呈現加速下滑的「死亡螺旋」——餘額越少，同等金額的提領佔比越大，加速消耗。

對研究問題的意義：路徑間的巨大差異正是「序列風險」（Sequence-of-Returns Risk）的體現。這告訴我們：退休規劃不能只看平均數或存活率，必須為最差情境做好準備。

Key observations:

  • Median path keeps growing: The 30-year median terminal balance is 39M, well above the initial 10M (NTD 10000K). In "typical" scenarios, 4% withdrawal not only doesn't deplete the fund but leaves a substantial legacy.
  • Massive path dispersion: The best 5% end at 178M, while the worst 5% end at 0M, potentially depleted early. Same strategy, same rate — outcomes can differ by over 17802x.
  • Critical divergence period: Paths begin diverging significantly in the first 5-10 years. Paths encountering bull markets early compound upward with decreasing withdrawal pressure; those hitting bear markets early decline sharply and rarely recover once below a critical threshold.
  • Depleted paths: 4 of 50 paths are depleted within 30 years (8%), typically showing an accelerating "death spiral" — as the balance shrinks, fixed withdrawals represent an ever-larger percentage, accelerating depletion.

Implication: The massive dispersion illustrates "sequence-of-returns risk." Retirement planning must prepare for worst-case scenarios, not just averages or survival rates.

3.5 提領策略比較3.5 Strategy Comparison

以下比較三種提領策略在 60/40 配置、4% 提領率下的表現。這是本研究的核心分析之一，回答「有沒有比原始 4% 法則更好的提領方式？」

We compare three withdrawal strategies on a 60/40 portfolio at 4%. This is one of the study's core analyses, answering "Is there a better withdrawal method than the original 4% rule?"

這張圖在看什麼：三種提領策略的 30 年存活率橫向比較。

關鍵觀察：

策略存活率中位終值（萬）30年總提領（萬）最低月提領
固定金額89.9%3,8441,90233,341
固定比例100.0%3,2962,37630,532
動態調整94.4%3,0222,17133,333

深入解讀：

  • 固定金額（89.9%）：最簡單但最危險。不管市場好壞都領相同金額，熊市時加速消耗。30 年總提領 1,902 萬，但約 10% 的退休者會提前破產。
  • 固定比例（100.0%）：數學上永不歸零，但生活品質不保證。最低月提領可能僅 30,532 元——如果市場長期低迷，月提領金額可能不足以維持基本生活開銷。換句話說，「帳面上不破產」不等於「退休成功」。
  • 動態調整（94.4%）：最佳平衡方案。存活率比固定金額高出 4.6 百分點（z=8.51，p<0.001），同時 30 年總提領 2,171 萬，高於固定金額的 1,902 萬。唯一的代價是生活費有波動——市場差時可能需要減少 20% 的支出。

What this chart shows: Side-by-side 30-year survival rates for three strategies.

Key observations:

Fixed Amount89.9%3,8441,90233,341

Analysis:

  • Fixed Amount (89.9%): Simplest but riskiest. Same amount regardless of market; accelerates depletion in bear markets. Total withdrawal 1,9020K over 30 years, but ~10% of retirees go bankrupt.
  • Fixed Ratio (100.0%): Mathematically never reaches zero, but minimum monthly withdrawal can drop to NTD 30,532 — potentially insufficient for basic living. "Not bankrupt on paper" is not "successful retirement."
  • Guardrails (94.4%): Best balance. Survival rate 4.6 pp higher than fixed amount (z=8.51, p<0.001), and total 30-year withdrawal (2,1710K) exceeds fixed amount (1,9020K). Trade-off: spending fluctuates — may need 20% reduction in bad markets.

3.6 台灣市場驗證3.6 Taiwan Market Validation

將相同的 Bootstrap 方法應用於台灣 0050 ETF 數據（2003-2025，22 年），檢驗 4% 法則是否在台灣市場同樣成立。需注意台灣數據較短，Bootstrap 模擬是從 22 年的歷史報酬中重抽組合成 30 年路徑，結果需謹慎解讀。

Applying the same bootstrap methodology to Taiwan 0050 ETF data (2003-2025, 22 years), we test whether the 4% rule holds in the Taiwan market. Note: with only 22 years of data, bootstrap assembles 30-year paths from shorter history — results require cautious interpretation.

這張圖在看什麼：台灣 0050 在不同提領率下的 Bootstrap 30 年存活率。

關鍵觀察：(1) 0050 以 4% 提領的存活率為 94.9%，表面上接近 95% 門檻，但二項式檢定顯示 p=0.3294（無法拒絕 >= 95%）。然而，這個「無法拒絕」可能只是因為統計檢定力不足——台灣數據僅 22 年，Bootstrap 模擬雖有 10,000 次，但底層數據的多樣性有限。(2) 在 3% 提領率下，存活率升至 98.2%，顯著更安全。(3) 0050 的年化報酬（12.3%）高於美國股票（10.2%），但波動度也更高（18.5% vs 15.4%），且缺乏債券配置的穩定效果（台灣沒有與美國公債同等的長期穩定資產配置選擇）。

對研究問題的意義：台灣投資人若直接套用美國的 4% 法則，面臨的風險可能比預期更高。建議以 3-3.5% 為安全提領率的出發點。

What this chart shows: Taiwan 0050 Bootstrap 30-year survival by withdrawal rate.

Key observations: (1) 0050 at 4%: 94.9% survival, seemingly near the 95% threshold. Binomial test: p=0.3294 (cannot reject >= 95%). However, this "failure to reject" may reflect insufficient test power — only 22 years of underlying data limits bootstrap diversity. (2) At 3%, survival rises to 98.2%, significantly safer. (3) 0050 annual return (12.3%) exceeds US stocks (10.2%), but volatility is also higher (18.5% vs 15.4%), and lacks the stabilizing effect of bond allocation.

Implication: Taiwan investors applying the US 4% rule may face higher risk than expected. Recommend starting from 3-3.5% as the safe withdrawal rate.

3.7 0050 vs 0056：市值型 vs 高股息3.7 0050 vs 0056: Broad Market vs High Dividend

台灣投資社群中最熱門的退休策略爭論之一：「該買 0050 賣股提領，還是買 0056 靠配息退休？」本節以相同的 Bootstrap 模擬方法，直接比較兩者在 4% 提領率下的表現。

One of the hottest retirement strategy debates in Taiwan: "Should I buy 0050 and sell shares, or buy 0056 and live off dividends?" This section directly compares both at 4% withdrawal using identical bootstrap methodology.

關鍵觀察：

  • 0050 存活率 97.1%，0056 存活率 95.8%——0050 反而更高。
  • 雙比例 z 檢定顯示差異顯著（z=3.45，p=0.0006，效果量 1.28 百分點，95% CI 約 0.5~2.0 百分點）。
  • 中位數期末餘額方面，0050 為 13,135 萬，0056 為 7,322 萬——0050 的期末餘額幾乎是 0056 的兩倍。
  • 0050 年化報酬 12.3% 遠高於 0056 的 7.7%，這個報酬差距在 30 年的複利效果下被大幅放大。

為什麼「靠配息退休」沒有優勢？V-003 已證實，配息只是將資產從股價轉為現金（除息日股價等額下跌），不會創造額外報酬。0056 為了維持穩定配息，選股偏向低成長股，犧牲了長期資本增值。在提領情境下，這個報酬劣勢直接轉化為更低的存活率。

Key observations:

  • 0050 survival: 97.1%, 0056: 95.8% — 0050 is actually higher.
  • Two-proportion z-test: z=3.45, p=0.0006, effect size 1.28 pp, 95% CI ~0.5-2.0 pp. Statistically significant.
  • Median terminal balance: 0050 at 13,1350K vs 0056 at 7,3220K — 0050 ends with nearly double.
  • 0050 annual return (12.3%) far exceeds 0056 (7.7%); this gap compounds dramatically over 30 years.

Why "living off dividends" has no advantage: V-003 demonstrated that dividends are merely asset-to-cash transfers (price drops by dividend amount). 0056, to maintain stable dividends, selects low-growth stocks, sacrificing long-term capital appreciation. In withdrawal scenarios, this return disadvantage directly translates to lower survival rates.

檢定結果解讀

  • H1（台灣 4% vs 95%）：p=0.3294，無法拒絕虛無假說。台灣 4% 存活率 94.9% 統計上不顯著低於 95%。但這不代表「台灣 4% 是安全的」——只是數據不足以下定論。信賴區間橫跨 95% 門檻，實務上仍應保守處理。
  • H2（60/40 vs 100% 股票）：z=4.69，p<0.001，效果量 2.06 百分點。60/40 存活率顯著高於 100% 股票。雖然效果量不大（約 2 百分點），但在退休規劃中，每 1 百分點的存活率差異都代表實際的破產風險差距。
  • H3（Guardrails vs 固定金額）：z=8.51，p<0.001，效果量 4.58 百分點。動態調整策略顯著提升存活率，效果量約 4.6 百分點——這是四項檢定中效果最強的，顯示「如何提領」比「提領多少」更重要。
  • H4（0050 vs 0056）：z=3.45，p=0.0006，效果量 1.28 百分點。0050 存活率顯著高於 0056。「靠配息退休」不但沒有優勢，反而略輸給賣股提領。

Test Result Interpretation

  • H1 (Taiwan 4% vs 95%): p=0.3294, fail to reject null. Taiwan 4% survival 94.9% is not statistically below 95%. However, this does not mean "Taiwan 4% is safe" — just insufficient data to conclude. The CI spans the 95% threshold; prudence is warranted.
  • H2 (60/40 vs 100% stock): z=4.69, p<0.001, effect 2.06 pp. 60/40 survival is significantly higher. Though the effect (~2 pp) seems small, each percentage point represents real ruin risk in retirement planning.
  • H3 (Guardrails vs fixed amount): z=8.51, p<0.001, effect 4.58 pp. Dynamic guardrails significantly improve survival — the largest effect among all four tests, suggesting "how you withdraw" matters more than "how much."
  • H4 (0050 vs 0056): z=3.45, p=0.0006, effect 1.28 pp. 0050 survival is significantly higher. "Living off dividends" has no advantage — it actually slightly underperforms selling shares.
