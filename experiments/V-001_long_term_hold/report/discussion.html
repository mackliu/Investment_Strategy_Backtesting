4. 討論4. Discussion

4.1 主要發現4.1 Key Findings

  • 「長期持有一定賺」的說法部分成立：持有 0050 或 0056 達 7 年以上，歷史上確實達到 100% 正名目報酬率（含交易成本），且平均年化報酬約 10.1%–9.9%。
  • 然而，短期持有風險不容忽視：持有 1 年的最差年化報酬為 -52.6%（0050），約有 25% 的機率出現虧損。即使持有 3 年，仍有約 7% 的虧損機率。
  • 經通膨調整後，實質報酬仍為正值但低於名目數字。三年以上持有期間的實質報酬率均為統計顯著，說明長期持有不僅能賺到名目報酬，更能打敗通膨。
  • 效果量隨持有期間遞增：Cohen's d 從 1 年的 0.60（中等）增至 5 年的 1.37（極大），顯示長期持有不僅統計顯著，且具有實質經濟意義。
  • The claim "long-term holding always profits" is partially supported: holding 0050 or 0056 for 7+ years has historically achieved 100% positive nominal returns (after transaction costs), with mean annualized returns of ~10.1%–9.9%.
  • However, short-term risk is not negligible: the worst-case 1-year return is -52.6% (0050), with about a 25% probability of loss. Even at 3 years, there is still a ~7% chance of negative returns.
  • After inflation adjustment, real returns remain positive but lower than nominal figures. Real returns for 3+ year periods are statistically significant, confirming that long-term holding not only earns nominal returns but also beats inflation.
  • Effect sizes increase with holding period: Cohen's d rises from 0.60 (medium) at 1 year to 1.37 (very large) at 5 years, demonstrating not just statistical but also practical economic significance.

4.2 注意事項與風險4.2 Caveats and Risks

  • 倖存者偏差：0050 和 0056 均為存續中的 ETF；已下市的基金未納入分析。如果將失敗的 ETF 也納入，整體報酬可能會更低。
  • 歷史有限：0050 約 22 年歷史；0056 僅約 18 年。長期持有（10-15 年）的非重疊樣本數非常少——例如 10 年期只有約 2 個獨立樣本（0050），統計推論的可信度因此受限。
  • 重疊報酬與有效樣本數：滾動報酬具有高度自相關。例如今天進場和明天進場的 5 年持有報酬幾乎相同（重疊率 99.96%）。Newey-West HAC 校正了標準誤，但名目樣本數（數千筆）遠大於有效獨立樣本數（僅數十甚至個位數）。投資人不應被大樣本數的表象所迷惑。
  • 過去不代表未來：台灣經濟在 2003-2025 年間整體表現良好（半導體產業蓬勃、GDP 成長穩定），但未來是否能延續此趨勢並不確定。歷史結果提供參考，但不構成保證。
  • Survivorship Bias: Both 0050 and 0056 are surviving ETFs; delisted funds are not analyzed. Including failed ETFs would likely lower overall returns.
  • Limited History: 0050 has ~22 years; 0056 only ~18 years. Long-period results (10-15Y) have very few non-overlapping samples — for example, 10-year returns have only ~2 independent observations (0050), limiting statistical inference reliability.
  • Overlapping Returns and Effective Sample Size: Rolling returns are highly autocorrelated. For instance, 5-year returns starting today vs tomorrow overlap by 99.96%. While Newey-West HAC corrects standard errors, the nominal sample size (thousands) vastly exceeds the effective independent sample size (dozens or even single digits). Investors should not be misled by the appearance of large sample sizes.
  • Past ≠ Future: Taiwan's economy performed well during 2003-2025 (booming semiconductor industry, stable GDP growth), but whether this trend continues is uncertain. Historical results provide guidance but not guarantees.

對保守型投資人：數據顯示持有 7 年以上在歷史上確實達到 100% 正報酬率。但投資人必須有心理準備承受持有期間可能超過 40% 的回撤。如果 2008 年那樣的腰斬會讓你恐慌賣出，那麼你的實際持有期間可能遠短於計劃——建議搭配債券或現金部位以降低波動。

For conservative investors: The data shows that 7+ year holding has historically achieved 100% positive returns. However, investors must be psychologically prepared for potential drawdowns exceeding 40% during the holding period. If a 2008-style 50% crash would cause you to panic-sell, your actual holding period may fall far short of planned — consider pairing with bonds or cash positions to reduce volatility.

For aggressive investors: This study does not support the 'just buy with eyes closed' narrative. Data shows that while entry timing is not the decisive factor, it still matters — entering at the 2008 bottom vs the 2007 peak can result in a 10+ percentage point difference in 5-year returns. Additionally, 0050 clearly outperforms 0056 in long-term returns; if the goal is capital growth rather than cash flow, the market-cap ETF is the better choice. Investors should replace the belief of 'guaranteed profit' with 'historically very likely to profit.'
