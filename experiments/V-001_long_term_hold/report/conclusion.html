5. 結論5. Conclusion

5.1 研究摘要5.1 Summary

本研究以 5517 個交易日的 0050 數據與 4403 個交易日的 0056 數據，系統性地檢驗「長期持有台灣 ETF 一定獲利」的投資論述。透過滾動報酬率分析、Newey-West HAC 統計檢定、區塊拔靴法信賴區間及多重比較校正，我們對 1 至 15 年持有期間進行了嚴謹的實證評估。

This study systematically tested the investment claim that 'long-term holding of Taiwan ETFs always profits,' using 5517 trading days of 0050 data and 4403 trading days of 0056 data. Through rolling return analysis, Newey-West HAC statistical tests, block bootstrap confidence intervals, and multiple comparison corrections, we rigorously evaluated holding periods from 1 to 15 years.

5.2 核心結論5.2 Core Conclusions

  1. 長期持有勝率極高：持有 0050/0056 達 7 年以上在歷史上達到 100% 正報酬率（含交易成本），平均年化報酬約 10.1%。
  2. 統計顯著性強健：所有持有期間的平均報酬均為統計顯著正值（Newey-West HAC t 檢定，Holm-Bonferroni 校正後 p < 0.05）。Cohen's d 效果量從 0.60 到 1.37，涵蓋中等至極大效果。Bootstrap 95% CI 均不包含零。
  3. 實質報酬為正：經通膨調整後，3 年以上持有期間仍維持統計顯著的正實質報酬；與銀行定存相比亦有顯著超額報酬。
  4. 「一定賺」為過度簡化：持有 1 年的虧損機率仍有約 25%，最差年化報酬為 -52.6%。投資人可能面臨超過 40% 的過程回撤。更準確的表述是：「長期持有在歷史上非常可能獲利，但並非零風險」。
  1. Very high long-term win rate: Holding 0050/0056 for 7+ years has historically achieved a 100% positive return rate (after transaction costs), with mean annualized returns of ~10.1%.
  2. Robust statistical significance: Mean returns are statistically significant across all holding periods (Newey-West HAC t-test, Holm-Bonferroni adjusted p < 0.05). Cohen's d effect sizes range from 0.60 to 1.37, spanning medium to very large effects. Bootstrap 95% CIs exclude zero throughout.
  3. Positive real returns: After inflation adjustment, holding periods of 3+ years maintain statistically significant positive real returns; excess returns over bank deposits are also significant.
  4. 'Always profits' is an oversimplification: The 1-year loss probability is still ~25%, with a worst-case annual return of -52.6%. Investors may face drawdowns exceeding 40% along the way. A more accurate statement: 'long-term holding has historically been very likely to profit, but is not risk-free.'

研究限制總結：本研究受限於倖存者偏差（僅分析存續中 ETF）、有限的歷史數據（最長約 22 年）、重疊報酬導致的有效樣本數偏低，以及「過去不等於未來」的根本限制。所有結論應理解為「基於歷史數據的觀察」，而非對未來的預測。

Summary of limitations: This study is constrained by survivorship bias (only surviving ETFs analyzed), limited historical data (~22 years maximum), low effective sample sizes due to overlapping returns, and the fundamental limitation that past performance does not predict future results. All conclusions should be understood as 'observations based on historical data,' not predictions about the future.
