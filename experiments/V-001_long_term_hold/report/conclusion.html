V-001：長期持有是否一定獲利？V-001: Does Long-Term Buy-and-Hold Always Profit?

5. 結論5. Conclusion

5.1 研究摘要5.1 Summary

本研究以 5517 個交易日的 0050 數據與 4403 個交易日的 0056 數據，系統性地檢驗「長期持有台灣 ETF 一定獲利」的投資論述。透過滾動報酬率分析、Newey-West HAC 統計檢定、區塊拔靴法信賴區間及多重比較校正，我們對 1 至 15 年持有期間進行了嚴謹的實證評估。

This study systematically tested the investment claim that 'long-term holding of Taiwan ETFs always profits,' using 5517 trading days of 0050 data and 4403 trading days of 0056 data. Through rolling return analysis, Newey-West HAC statistical tests, block bootstrap confidence intervals, and multiple comparison corrections, we rigorously evaluated holding periods from 1 to 15 years.

5.2 核心結論5.2 Core Conclusions

  1. 長期持有勝率極高：持有 0050/0056 達 7 年以上在歷史上達到 100% 正報酬率（含交易成本），平均年化報酬約 10.1%。0050 的整體 CAGR 為 12.3%，含息累積成長約 13.4 倍。
  2. 統計顯著性強健：所有持有期間的平均報酬均為統計顯著正值（Newey-West HAC t 檢定，Holm-Bonferroni 校正後 p < 0.05）。Cohen's d 效果量從 1 年的 0.60（中等）到 5 年的 1.37（極大），涵蓋中等至極大效果。Bootstrap 95% CI 在所有期間均不包含零，例如 0050 一年期 CI 為 [5.9%, 19.5%]。
  3. 實質報酬為正：經通膨調整後，3 年以上持有期間仍維持統計顯著的正實質報酬（實質 Cohen's d 5 年期為 1.28）。與銀行定存（年利率約 1.5%）相比亦有顯著超額報酬，5 年期超額報酬勝率達 91.4%。三層檢定全數通過，全面支持長期持有 ETF 的投資價值。
  4. 「一定賺」為過度簡化：持有 1 年的虧損機率仍有約 25%，最差年化報酬為 -52.6%，5% CVaR 為 -32.6%。投資人在持有過程中可能面臨超過 56% 的回撤。更準確的表述是：「長期持有在歷史上非常可能獲利，但並非零風險——投資人需有足夠的時間跨度和心理韌性來度過市場低迷期」。
  1. Very high long-term win rate: Holding 0050/0056 for 7+ years has historically achieved a 100% positive return rate (after transaction costs), with mean annualized returns of ~10.1%. 0050's overall CAGR is 12.3%, with cumulative total return growth of ~13.4x.
  2. Robust statistical significance: Mean returns are statistically significant across all holding periods (Newey-West HAC t-test, Holm-Bonferroni adjusted p < 0.05). Cohen's d ranges from 0.60 (medium) at 1 year to 1.37 (very large) at 5 years. Bootstrap 95% CIs exclude zero for all periods, e.g., 0050 1-year CI is [5.9%, 19.5%].
  3. Positive real returns: After inflation adjustment, 3+ year periods maintain statistically significant positive real returns (real Cohen's d at 5 years: 1.28). Compared to bank deposits (~1.5%/year), excess returns are also significant, with 5-year excess win rate at 91.4%. All three layers of testing pass, comprehensively supporting the investment value of long-term ETF holding.
  4. 'Always profits' is an oversimplification: 1-year loss probability is ~25%, worst annual return -52.6%, 5% CVaR -32.6%. Drawdowns may exceed 56% during the holding period. A more accurate statement: 'long-term holding has historically been very likely to profit, but is not risk-free — investors need sufficient time horizon and psychological resilience to weather market downturns.'

研究限制總結：(1) 倖存者偏差——僅分析存續中的 0050 和 0056，已下市 ETF 的投資人經歷可能截然不同；(2) 有限的歷史數據——最長約 22 年（0050），在金融市場的尺度中仍屬短期，且主要涵蓋一個大的牛市週期；(3) 有效樣本數偏低——重疊報酬使 5 年期的名目數千筆數據實際上僅約 4-5 個獨立觀測；(4) 單一市場——台灣結果受益於半導體產業，不可直接推廣至其他國家；(5) 過去不等於未來——結構性轉變可能使未來數十年與過去大不相同。所有結論應理解為「基於歷史數據的觀察」，而非對未來的預測。

Summary of limitations: (1) Survivorship bias — only surviving ETFs (0050, 0056) analyzed; delisted ETF investors may have had very different experiences; (2) Limited history — ~22 years maximum (0050), still short by financial market standards, primarily covering one major bull cycle; (3) Low effective sample size — overlapping returns mean thousands of nominal 5-year observations represent only ~4-5 independent observations; (4) Single market — Taiwan's results benefit from the semiconductor industry and cannot be directly generalized to other countries; (5) Past does not predict future — structural shifts may make the next decades very different. All conclusions should be understood as 'observations based on historical data,' not predictions.

5.3 未來研究方向5.3 Future Research

  • 與定期定額策略比較（V-002）：定期定額是否能進一步降低進場時機風險？對於無法一次投入大筆資金的投資人，DCA 的長期表現是否與單筆投入相近？
  • 股息再投入 vs 提領策略（V-003, V-013）：「高股息一定好」的說法是否成立？退休族群的最佳提領率為何？0056 的配息策略在退休階段是否真的優於 0050？
  • 國際市場交叉驗證：台灣的長期持有結果是否為特例？與美國（SPY）、日本（1306.T）、歐洲等市場比較，能否找到普遍性的「長期持有最低安全年限」？（詳見 V-005）
  • 納入更多 ETF 標的：將分析擴展至 006205（富邦上證）、00692（富邦公司治理 100）等不同類型的 ETF，檢驗「長期持有」是否適用於所有 ETF 或僅限特定類型。
  • 最佳持有期間研究：利用效用函數理論，結合投資人的風險偏好（風險趨避係數），計算不同風險屬性投資人的最適持有期間，而非僅看勝率。
  • Compare with DCA strategy (V-002): Can dollar-cost averaging further reduce entry timing risk? For investors unable to invest a lump sum, does DCA's long-term performance match lump-sum investing?
  • Dividend reinvestment vs withdrawal (V-003, V-013): Is 'high dividends are always better' true? What is the optimal withdrawal rate for retirees? Does 0056's dividend strategy truly outperform 0050 during retirement?
  • International cross-validation: Are Taiwan's buy-and-hold results a special case? Comparing with the U.S. (SPY), Japan (1306.T), and Europe, can we identify a universal 'minimum safe holding period'? (See V-005)
  • More ETF targets: Extend analysis to include different ETF types (e.g., 006205 FTSE China, 00692 Corporate Governance 100) to test whether 'buy and hold' applies to all ETFs or only certain types.
  • Optimal holding period: Using utility function theory with investor risk aversion coefficients, compute the optimal holding period for different risk profiles, rather than simply looking at win rates.
