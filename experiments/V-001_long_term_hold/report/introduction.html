V-001：長期持有是否一定獲利？V-001: Does Long-Term Buy-and-Hold Always Profit?

1. 緒論1. Introduction

1.1 研究背景1.1 Background

「只要持有 0050/0056 五到十年，一定賺錢」是台灣投資社群中最常見的投資論述之一。在 YouTube、PTT、社群媒體上，這個觀點被廣泛傳播，鼓勵新手投入股市。這句話表面上有歷史數據支撐，但「一定」二字卻隱含了對風險的忽視。

這類過度簡化的論述存在兩個潛在危險：首先，它可能導致投資人低估短期波動的衝擊——若投資人在 2008 年高點進場後面臨 50% 回檔，即使最終獲利，中途的心理壓力是否能承受？其次，「一定」的措辭暗示零風險，這與投資的本質相悖。

因此，我們需要以科學方法嚴謹檢驗這個論點：不是僅看幾個成功案例，而是系統性地計算每一個歷史進場點的實際報酬，並進行統計檢定來量化「幾乎一定」和「真的一定」之間的差距。

"Just hold 0050/0056 for 5-10 years and you'll definitely make money" is one of the most common investment claims in Taiwan's social media. Across YouTube, PTT forums, and social platforms, countless financial influencers use this argument to encourage newcomers to invest. While the claim has some historical basis, the word "definitely" glosses over the reality of risk.

Such oversimplified claims carry two potential dangers: First, they may lead investors to underestimate the impact of short-term volatility — if an investor entered at the 2008 peak and faced a 50% drawdown, could they withstand the psychological pressure even if the investment eventually recovered? Second, the "guaranteed" framing implies zero risk, which contradicts the fundamental nature of investing.

This is why we need rigorous scientific testing: rather than cherry-picking a few success stories, we systematically compute actual returns for every historical entry point and apply statistical tests to quantify the gap between "almost certainly" and "truly certain."

1.2 文獻回顧1.2 Literature Review

長期持有股票的學術研究可追溯至 Jeremy Siegel 的經典著作《Stocks for the Long Run》（1994，至今已出六版）。Siegel 分析美國股市超過 200 年的數據，發現股票在 20 年以上的持有期間幾乎總是優於債券和通膨。然而，該研究的結論主要基於美國市場——一個在 20 世紀經歷了前所未有成功的經濟體，存在所謂的「美國例外論」(U.S. exceptionalism) 偏誤。

Dimson、Marsh 與 Staunton（2002）在《Triumph of the Optimists》中分析了 16 個已開發國家百年以上的股市報酬，發現長期持有股票的正報酬並非美國獨有現象——全球多數市場的股票在 20 年以上持有期間均提供正實質報酬。但他們也強調，不同市場之間的報酬差距極大：日本、義大利等國的長期報酬遠低於美國。這提醒我們，台灣的結果不必然能直接套用美國的歷史經驗。

針對台灣市場，本土研究相對有限。台灣股市於 1962 年開市，但早期資料品質不佳；ETF 產品更是 2003 年才上市。少數台灣本土研究（如林丙輝 2010 年代的台灣加權指數長期報酬研究）發現台股長期年化報酬約在 8-10% 之間，但這些研究多聚焦於加權指數而非 ETF，且未使用嚴謹的統計檢定來處理重疊報酬問題。本研究使用完整的 ETF 還原價格數據，有助填補此空白。

學術界也指出長期持有分析的方法論陷阱：Boudoukh et al. (2008) 警告重疊報酬率（overlapping returns）會嚴重膨脹 t 統計量，導致偽顯著。例如，5 年滾動報酬在相鄰交易日間重疊率高達 99.96%，傳統 t 檢定可能誤將實際無顯著性的結果判定為顯著。為此，本研究採用 Newey-West HAC 校正與區塊拔靴法來處理此問題。

Campbell 與 Viceira（2002）的《Strategic Asset Allocation》進一步指出，即使長期持有股票平均報酬為正，報酬率的均值回復特性並不能消除「投資人困境」——一個 30 歲的投資人面對的是 30 年不確定性，而非 30 個獨立的一年期決策。長期風險不應被時間分散效果的表象所掩蓋。

Academic research on long-term equity holding traces back to Jeremy Siegel's classic Stocks for the Long Run (1994, now in its 6th edition). Siegel analyzed over 200 years of U.S. market data, finding that stocks almost always outperformed bonds and inflation over 20+ year periods. However, his conclusions are primarily based on the U.S. market — an economy that experienced unprecedented success in the 20th century, subject to so-called "U.S. exceptionalism" bias.

Dimson, Marsh, and Staunton (2002) in Triumph of the Optimists analyzed over a century of equity returns across 16 developed nations, finding that long-term positive equity returns are not unique to the U.S. — most global markets delivered positive real returns over 20+ year horizons. However, they emphasized that cross-country return dispersion is substantial: Japan and Italy's long-term returns fell far below the U.S. This reminds us that Taiwan's results may not simply mirror U.S. historical experience.

For Taiwan's market, local research is relatively limited. The Taiwan Stock Exchange opened in 1962, but early data quality is poor; ETF products only launched in 2003. The few Taiwan-focused studies (e.g., domestic TAIEX long-term return analyses from the 2010s) found annualized returns of roughly 8-10%, but these mostly examined the weighted index rather than ETFs and did not apply rigorous statistical corrections for overlapping returns. This study uses complete dividend-adjusted ETF price data to help fill this gap.

The academic literature also highlights methodological pitfalls in long-term holding analysis: Boudoukh et al. (2008) warned that overlapping returns severely inflate t-statistics, leading to spurious significance. For example, 5-year rolling returns on adjacent trading days overlap by 99.96%, meaning traditional t-tests might declare insignificant results as significant. To address this, our study employs Newey-West HAC correction and block bootstrap methods.

Campbell and Viceira (2002) in Strategic Asset Allocation further noted that even if long-term average equity returns are positive, the mean-reverting nature of returns does not eliminate the 'investor's dilemma' — a 30-year-old investor faces 30 years of uncertainty, not 30 independent one-year decisions. Long-term risk should not be obscured by the illusion of time diversification.

我們採用三層階層式檢定架構，由寬鬆到嚴格逐步提高標準：第一層問的是最基本的問題——投資人是否能賺到正報酬（名目值），這是「存股有用嗎」的最低門檻；第二層扣除通膨後，投資是否仍能保值增值——台灣年均通膨約 1-2%，若名目報酬僅略高於零，扣除通膨後可能實質虧損；第三層則與替代方案（銀行定存利率約 1.5%/年）比較，檢驗 ETF 是否提供了足以補償風險的超額報酬——如果承受股市波動卻只獲得與定存相當的報酬，那麼承擔風險就不值得。只有三層全部通過，才能較有信心地說「長期持有 ETF 確實值得」。

We employ a three-layer hierarchical testing framework that progressively raises the bar: Layer 1 asks the most basic question — can investors earn a positive return (nominal), the lowest bar for 'does stock-picking work?'; Layer 2 tests whether returns still preserve and grow purchasing power after inflation — Taiwan's average annual inflation is ~1-2%, meaning marginally positive nominal returns could represent real losses; Layer 3 compares against the alternative (bank deposits ~1.5%/year), testing whether ETFs provide sufficient excess returns to compensate for risk — if enduring stock market volatility yields only deposit-like returns, the risk is not worth taking. Only if all three layers pass can we confidently say that long-term ETF holding is worthwhile.

1.4 研究貢獻1.4 Research Contribution

本研究與現有文獻的差異在於：(1) 使用完整的還原股價而非僅看價格報酬，正確反映股息再投入效果——本研究顯示股息貢獻了 0050 約一半的長期累積報酬，忽略股息將嚴重低估 ETF 的投資價值；(2) 採用 Newey-West HAC 校正處理重疊報酬的自相關問題，而非使用可能低估標準誤的傳統 t 檢定——這是長期持有研究中最容易被忽略但影響最大的方法論問題；(3) 以環形區塊拔靴法提供不依賴常態假設的信賴區間，考量報酬分佈的左偏與厚尾特性；(4) 同時分析市值型（0050）與高股息型（0056）兩檔 ETF，比較不同投資風格的長期表現；(5) 提供三層階層式假說檢定（名目報酬、實質報酬、超額報酬），全面評估長期持有的投資價值。

此外，本研究將統計顯著性與實質顯著性（效果量、信賴區間）並重，避免僅以 p 值做結論。在投資領域，一個統計顯著但效果量微小的結果，可能在扣除交易成本後變得毫無意義——因此我們同時報告 Cohen's d 和 Bootstrap 95% CI，讓讀者判斷結果的經濟實質意義。

This study differs from existing literature in several ways: (1) it uses fully dividend-adjusted prices rather than price-only returns, correctly reflecting the impact of dividend reinvestment — our analysis shows dividends contributed roughly half of 0050's long-term cumulative returns, meaning ignoring dividends severely underestimates ETF investment value; (2) it employs Newey-West HAC correction to handle autocorrelation in overlapping returns, instead of traditional t-tests that may underestimate standard errors — this is the most commonly overlooked yet most impactful methodological issue in long-term holding studies; (3) it provides distribution-free confidence intervals via circular block bootstrap, accounting for the left-skewed, fat-tailed nature of return distributions; (4) it simultaneously analyzes both a market-cap ETF (0050) and a dividend-focused ETF (0056), comparing long-term performance across different investment styles; and (5) it provides a three-layer hierarchical hypothesis testing framework (nominal, real, excess returns) for comprehensive assessment of long-term holding value.

Furthermore, this study places equal emphasis on statistical significance and practical significance (effect sizes, confidence intervals), avoiding conclusions based solely on p-values. In investment contexts, a statistically significant but tiny effect size may become meaningless after transaction costs — hence we report both Cohen's d and Bootstrap 95% CI, allowing readers to judge the economic substance of the results.
