B. 實質報酬檢定（第二層）B. Real Return Tests (Layer 2)

第二層檢定扣除消費者物價指數（CPI）通膨後，檢驗實質報酬是否仍為正值。結果顯示 3 年以上持有期間的實質報酬率均為統計顯著，但幅度略低於名目數字——這提醒投資人，表面上的高報酬有一部分被通膨「吃掉」。短期（1-2 年）的實質報酬勝率也略低於名目勝率，因為通膨侵蝕了邊際的正報酬。

Layer 2 tests examine whether returns remain positive after deducting CPI inflation. Results show that real returns for 3+ year periods are statistically significant, though slightly lower than nominal figures — a reminder that some of the apparent high returns are 'eaten' by inflation. Short-term (1-2Y) real return win rates are also slightly lower than nominal win rates, as inflation erodes marginal positive returns.

C. 基準比較檢定（第三層）C. Benchmark Tests (Layer 3)

第三層檢定比較 ETF 報酬是否超過替代方案（銀行定存利率約 1.5%/年）。結果顯示兩檔 ETF 在所有持有期間的超額報酬均為統計顯著。這意味著長期持有 ETF 不僅能賺到正報酬，更提供了優於「無風險」替代方案的風險溢酬——這為承擔股市波動的投資人提供了合理補償。

Layer 3 tests compare ETF returns against the alternative (bank deposit rate ~1.5%/year). Results show that both ETFs generate statistically significant excess returns across all holding periods. This means long-term ETF holding not only earns positive returns but also provides a risk premium above the 'risk-free' alternative — offering reasonable compensation for investors who bear stock market volatility.

D. 月報酬率熱力圖D. Monthly Heatmaps

月報酬率熱力圖以顏色深淺呈現每個月份的報酬表現（綠色=正報酬，紅色=負報酬）。橫軸為月份（1-12 月），縱軸為年份。投資人可從中觀察是否存在季節性效應——例如台股是否有特定月份表現特別好或特別差。從圖中可以看出，雖然個別月份有明顯的正負值，但整體而言沒有一致的季節性規律，支持「進場月份對長期報酬影響有限」的結論。

The monthly return heatmaps display each month's performance using color intensity (green = positive, red = negative). The x-axis shows months (1-12), the y-axis shows years. Investors can observe whether seasonal patterns exist — for example, whether certain months consistently outperform or underperform. The charts show that while individual months have notable positive or negative values, there is no consistent seasonal pattern overall, supporting the conclusion that entry month has limited impact on long-term returns.
