2.1 數據來源2.1 Data

數據品質控制：還原股價（adj_close）以 2025 年中為錨定點向前推算，確保歷史除權息事件正確反映。我們將本地資料庫數據與 yfinance 線上數據（2009 年起可用）進行交叉驗證，兩者在重疊期間一致。CPI 數據取自行政院主計總處（1981 年至今），用於實質報酬計算。

Price data was exported from a local MySQL database with dividend-adjusted close prices (adj_close). 0050 has 5517 trading days (2003-06-30 to 2025-11-25), and 0056 has 4403 trading days (2007-12-26 to 2025-11-25).

Data quality control: Adjusted close prices use mid-2025 as the anchor point and are back-calculated to correctly reflect all historical corporate actions (dividends, splits). We cross-validated local database data against yfinance online data (available from 2009), finding consistency in the overlapping period. CPI data from Taiwan's DGBAS (1981–present) is used for real return calculations.

2.2 分析架構2.2 Analysis Framework

  • 滾動分析：以每個交易日為起點，計算持有 N 年後的報酬——為什麼？滾動分析涵蓋所有可能的進場時點，避免只看特定日期而產生偏差。這是最全面但樣本間高度重疊的方法。
  • 年度進場：以每年第一個交易日為起點——為什麼？模擬投資人每年年初進場的實際決策情境，提供直覺的年度視角，且各年度樣本獨立性較高。
  • 月份效應：以每月第一個交易日為起點——為什麼？檢驗進場月份是否影響長期表現，若存在季節效應（如「五窮六絕」），投資人可據此調整時機。
  • Rolling Analysis: Every trading day as entry point, compute return after N years — Why? Rolling analysis covers every possible entry point, avoiding bias from selecting specific dates. It is the most comprehensive method, though samples are highly overlapping.
  • Vintage Year: First trading day of each year as entry point — Why? Simulates the real-world scenario of investors entering at the start of each year, providing an intuitive annual perspective with higher sample independence.
  • Entry Month Effect: First trading day of each month as entry point — Why? Tests whether the entry month affects long-term performance. If seasonal effects exist (e.g., the "Sell in May" effect), investors could adjust their timing accordingly.

交易成本對長期持有策略的影響遠小於頻繁交易策略——因為買賣只發生一次。以官定費率計算，單次來回成本約 0.385%，年化到 5 年持有期間僅約 0.077%/年，幾乎可忽略不計。但為確保結論的穩健性，我們在附錄中提供三種成本情境（零成本、折扣券商約 0.271%、官定費率約 0.385%）的敏感度分析。結果顯示三種情境下的勝率差異不超過 1 個百分點，證明交易成本並非影響結論的關鍵因素。

Transaction costs have a much smaller impact on buy-and-hold strategies than on frequent trading strategies — since buying and selling occurs only once. At official rates, the round-trip cost is ~0.385%, which annualized over a 5-year holding period amounts to only ~0.077%/year, essentially negligible. However, to ensure robustness, the appendix provides sensitivity analysis across three cost scenarios (zero cost, discount broker ~0.271%, official rate ~0.385%). Results show win rate differences of less than 1 percentage point across scenarios, confirming that transaction costs are not a critical factor for the conclusions.

2.4 統計方法2.4 Statistical Methods

  • Newey-West HAC t 檢定（校正自相關）——因為滾動報酬具有高度自相關（持有 5 年的報酬在相鄰交易日間幾乎完全重疊），傳統 t 檢定會嚴重低估標準誤，Newey-West 方法可修正此偏誤。
  • 環形區塊拔靴法（10,000 次重抽）——不依賴報酬率服從常態分佈的假設，以重抽樣方式建構信賴區間。使用環形區塊（circular block）以保留時間序列的相依結構。
  • Holm-Bonferroni 多重比較校正——同時檢定 7 個持有期間會導致族系錯誤率膨脹，Holm-Bonferroni 方法逐步校正 p 值，在控制型 I 錯誤的同時保有較高檢定力。
  • Cohen's d 效果量（含 95% 信賴區間）——p 值只說明是否顯著，Cohen's d 則量化效果的實質大小（0.2 小、0.5 中、0.8 大）。搭配 95% 信賴區間，提供更完整的推論。
  • Newey-West HAC t-test (autocorrelation-robust) — Because rolling returns are highly autocorrelated (5-year returns on adjacent trading days overlap almost completely), traditional t-tests severely underestimate standard errors. The Newey-West method corrects this bias.
  • Circular Block Bootstrap (10,000 replications) — Does not assume normally distributed returns; constructs confidence intervals via resampling. Uses circular blocks to preserve the dependency structure of time series data.
  • Holm-Bonferroni multiple comparison correction — Testing 7 holding periods simultaneously inflates the family-wise error rate. The Holm-Bonferroni method progressively adjusts p-values, controlling Type I error while maintaining higher statistical power than the simpler Bonferroni correction.
  • Cohen's d effect size with 95% CI — P-values only indicate significance; Cohen's d quantifies the practical magnitude of the effect (0.2 = small, 0.5 = medium, 0.8 = large). Combined with 95% confidence intervals, it provides a more complete inference picture.
