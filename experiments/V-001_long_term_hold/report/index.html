V-001：長期持有是否一定獲利？V-001: Does Long-Term Buy-and-Hold Always Profit?

研究問題：長期持有台灣 ETF（0050/0056）是否「一定」能獲得正報酬？不同持有期間的勝率、報酬分佈與風險特徵為何？

Research Question: Is it true that holding Taiwan ETFs (0050/0056) long-term "always" yields positive returns? What are the win rates, return distributions, and risk characteristics across different holding periods?

摘要Abstract

本研究檢驗「長期持有台灣 ETF 必定獲利」的常見投資論述。使用 5517 個交易日的 0050 數據（2003–2025，約 22 年）與 4403 個交易日的 0056 數據（2007–2025，約 18 年），計算 1 至 15 年持有期間的滾動年化報酬率。統計顯著性以 Newey-West HAC t 檢定進行，並以 Holm-Bonferroni 校正多重比較。

核心發現：0050 持有 7 年以上達到 100% 正報酬率，即使扣除交易成本亦然；持有 1 年的勝率約 75%，最差年化報酬為 -52.6%。效果量（Cohen's d）隨持有期間增長——從 1 年的 0.60（中等效果）提升至 5 年的 1.37（極大效果）。所有檢定在 Holm-Bonferroni 校正後仍具統計顯著性。結論：長期持有確實大幅提高獲利機率，但「一定賺」的表述過於武斷——更準確的說法是「歷史上非常可能獲利」。

This study examines the common investment claim that long-term holding of Taiwan ETFs guarantees positive returns. Using 5517 trading days of 0050 data (2003–2025, ~22 years) and 4403 trading days of 0056 data (2007–2025, ~18 years), we compute rolling annualized returns for 1 to 15 year holding periods. Statistical significance is assessed via Newey-West HAC t-tests with Holm-Bonferroni correction for multiple comparisons.

Core findings: 0050 achieves a 100% win rate at 7+ year holding periods, even after transaction costs. The 1-year win rate is approximately 75%, with a worst-case annualized return of -52.6%. Effect sizes (Cohen's d) increase with holding period — from 0.60 (medium) at 1 year to 1.37 (very large) at 5 years. All tests remain statistically significant after Holm-Bonferroni correction. Conclusion: long-term holding substantially raises the probability of positive returns, but 'guaranteed profit' is an overstatement — 'historically very likely' is more accurate.

主要發現Key Findings

  • 勝率隨持有期間遞增：0050 持有 1 年勝率 75%、3 年 93%、5 年 95%、7 年以上 100%。持有期間每延長一倍，虧損機率約下降一半。
  • 0056 表現相近：持有 1 年勝率 75%，7 年以上達到 100%，趨勢與 0050 一致。但 0056 長期 CAGR（7.9%）低於 0050（12.3%），反映高股息策略在資本成長方面的劣勢。
  • 統計顯著且效果量大：所有持有期間的平均報酬在 Holm-Bonferroni 校正後均為顯著正值（校正後 p < 0.05）。Cohen's d 效果量從 1 年的中等（d = {gh("0050", 1, "cohens_d"):.2f}）躍升至 5 年的極大（d = {gh("0050", 5, "cohens_d"):.2f}），說明不僅在統計上顯著，在經濟上也有實質意義。
  • 實質報酬為正：經通膨調整後，0050 一年期實質報酬勝率仍達 73.4%，5 年期更達 92.3%。長期持有確實能打敗通貨膨脹。
  • 最差情境仍可控：0050 持有 1 年最差年化報酬為 -52.6%，5% VaR 為 -18.9%，5% CVaR 為 -32.6%。持有 5 年以上，最差情境已收斂至年化 -5.2%，顯示時間確實能有效降低尾端風險。
  • Win rate increases with holding period: 0050 at 1Y: 75%, 3Y: 93%, 5Y: 95%, 7Y+: 100%. For each doubling of the holding period, the loss probability roughly halves.
  • 0056 shows similar pattern: 1Y win rate 75%, reaching 100% at 7+ years — consistent with 0050. However, 0056's long-term CAGR (7.9%) trails 0050 (12.3%), reflecting the dividend strategy's capital-growth disadvantage.
  • Statistically significant with large effects: All holding periods show significant positive mean returns after Holm-Bonferroni correction (adjusted p < 0.05). Cohen's d rises from medium (d = 0.60) at 1 year to very large (d = 1.37) at 5 years, confirming not just statistical but economic significance.
  • Positive real returns: After inflation adjustment, 0050's 1-year real return win rate is still 73.4%, rising to 92.3% at 5 years. Long-term holding truly beats inflation.
  • Worst-case still manageable: 0050's worst 1-year return is -52.6%, with 5% VaR at -18.9% and 5% CVaR at -32.6%. At 5+ years, worst-case narrows to -5.2% annualized, showing that time effectively reduces tail risk.
