研究方法Methodology

2.1 數據來源與品質2.1 Data Sources and Quality

本研究使用以下數據，共同分析期間為 2006-05 ~ 2025-10（234 個月）：

  • 0050（台灣50 ETF）：本地資料庫含息調整收盤價（adj_close），已套用 2025 年 4:1 拆股調整。資料完整無缺漏。0050 追蹤台灣市值最大的 50 家公司（以台積電為首），是台灣投資人最常使用的大盤代理指標。使用含息調整價是為了正確反映股息再投資的效果——這一點對台股特別重要，因為台灣上市公司的平均殖利率約 3-4%。
  • SPY（SPDR S&P 500 ETF）：追蹤美國標普500 指數，代表全球最大的股票市場。yfinance 下載（auto_adjust=True），以收盤價計算月報酬。SPY 是全球流動性最高的 ETF，價格發現效率極佳。
  • TLT（iShares 20+ Year Treasury Bond ETF）：追蹤美國 20 年以上公債，代表「避險資產」。在經濟衰退或股市大跌時，投資人通常會湧入公債（flight to quality），推升公債價格、壓低利率。但在升息環境下，長期公債價格會大幅下跌（如 2022 年），因此 TLT 不是「零風險」資產。
  • GLD（SPDR Gold Shares）：追蹤黃金現貨價格。黃金被視為「亂世資產」——在地緣政治風險、通膨飆升或貨幣信心危機時表現良好，但在正常市場環境中不產生任何現金流（不像股票有股息、債券有利息），純粹依賴價格上漲獲利。
  • VNQ（Vanguard Real Estate ETF）：追蹤美國 REITs（不動產投資信託），代表商業不動產資產類別。REITs 結合了不動產的通膨保護特性和股票的流動性，但在金融危機中（如 2008 年）跌幅可能與股票一樣慘。
  • USD/TWD 匯率：yfinance USDTWD=X，過濾 20-40 異常值後轉月頻。美元資產報酬以 (1+r_USD)*(1+r_FX)-1 轉換為台幣計價。匯率轉換至關重要：一位台灣投資人最終能用來消費的金額是台幣，因此必須以台幣計價來衡量真實報酬。

數據品質檢核：所有資產的月報酬數據經過三道品質檢查：(1) 缺失值檢測——若某月缺失，以前後月插值；(2) 極端值過濾——匯率資料過濾超出 20-40 合理範圍的異常值；(3) 共同期間對齊——以最短可用期間為準，確保所有資產的觀察期完全一致。所有資產統一取每月最後一個交易日的收盤價，確保計算月報酬的時間點一致。

This study uses the following data over a common period of 2006-05 ~ 2025-10 (234 months):

  • 0050 (Taiwan 50 ETF): Local database adjusted close (adj_close), with 2025 4:1 split adjustment applied. Complete with no gaps. 0050 tracks Taiwan's 50 largest companies (led by TSMC) and is the most common broad-market proxy. Using adjusted prices correctly accounts for dividend reinvestment — especially important for Taiwan stocks with ~3-4% average yield.
  • SPY (SPDR S&P 500 ETF): Tracks the S&P 500, representing the world's largest equity market. Downloaded via yfinance (auto_adjust=True). SPY is the most liquid ETF globally with excellent price discovery.
  • TLT (iShares 20+ Year Treasury Bond ETF): Tracks US Treasuries with 20+ year maturity, representing the "safe haven" asset class. During recessions or market crashes, investors typically rush into Treasuries (flight to quality), pushing bond prices up. But during rate hikes (e.g., 2022), long-term bond prices can plunge — TLT is not a zero-risk asset.
  • GLD (SPDR Gold Shares): Tracks gold spot prices. Gold is considered a "crisis asset" — performing well during geopolitical risk, inflation spikes, or currency confidence crises. But in normal markets, it generates no cash flow (unlike stock dividends or bond coupons), relying purely on price appreciation.
  • VNQ (Vanguard Real Estate ETF): Tracks US REITs, representing commercial real estate. REITs combine real estate's inflation protection with stock-like liquidity, but can fall as hard as stocks during financial crises (e.g., 2008).
  • USD/TWD exchange rate: yfinance USDTWD=X, filtered for 20-40 range outliers, converted to monthly. USD asset returns converted to TWD via (1+r_USD)*(1+r_FX)-1. Currency conversion is essential: a Taiwanese investor's purchasing power is in TWD, so true returns must be measured in TWD.

Data quality checks: Three quality checks on all monthly returns: (1) Missing value detection — interpolated from adjacent months if missing; (2) Outlier filtering — FX data filtered for values outside 20-40 reasonable range; (3) Common period alignment — trimmed to shortest available period, ensuring all assets have identical observation windows. All assets use month-end closing prices for consistent monthly return calculation.

2.2 投資組合配置2.2 Portfolio Construction

本研究建構 7 個投資組合進行比較，涵蓋從單一市場到多資產的配置：

  • 100% 台股（0050）：作為「不分散」的基準。許多台灣投資人的實際配置就是如此——全部押在台股。
  • 100% 美股（SPY）：另一個「不分散」的基準。有些投資人認為美股「永遠漲」，全部配置美股。
  • 股60/債40：最經典的「懶人配置」。過去 40 年（1980-2020 的長期債券牛市）表現極佳，但 2022 年升息環境中股債同跌，讓人重新檢視這個配置的適用性。
  • 三分法（股/債/金）：加入黃金作為第三個資產類別。黃金與股票的相關性低，且在通膨環境中能提供額外保護——彌補 60/40 在高通膨時期的弱點。
  • 五資產等權（20%/20%/20%/20%/20%）：最大化分散。不做任何「聰明」的判斷，每個資產類別相同權重——這是一種「承認自己不知道哪個會贏」的謙虛策略。
  • 實務配置（50% 台股 + 海外分散）：反映台灣投資人的「本土偏好」——台灣人習慣將大部分資金留在台灣，小部分配置海外。這種配置是否已經足夠分散？
  • 風險平價近似：按各資產波動度的倒數分配權重，讓每個資產對組合風險的貢獻相等。這是「不押注任何資產會特別好」的風險管理思維。

所有組合採用年度再平衡（每年年初回到目標權重），期間各資產按實際報酬漂移。選擇年度而非月度或季度再平衡，是為了平衡「維持目標配置」和「減少交易成本」兩個目標。實務上，過於頻繁的再平衡會產生不必要的手續費和稅負。

We construct 7 portfolios ranging from single-market to multi-asset:

  • 100% Taiwan (0050): The "no diversification" benchmark. Many Taiwanese investors are actually allocated this way — all in Taiwan stocks.
  • 100% US (SPY): Another "no diversification" benchmark. Some investors believe US stocks "always go up" and go all-in.
  • Stock 60/Bond 40: The classic "lazy portfolio." Performed excellently during the 40-year bond bull market (1980-2020), but 2022's simultaneous stock-bond decline prompted reassessment of this allocation.
  • Three-way (stock/bond/gold): Adding gold as a third asset class. Gold's low correlation with stocks and inflation protection addresses 60/40's weakness during high-inflation periods.
  • Five-asset EW (20% each): Maximum diversification. No "smart" allocation — equal weight to each class. This is a humble strategy admitting "I don't know which will win."
  • Practical (50% Taiwan + overseas): Reflects Taiwanese home bias — keeping most money domestic, with small overseas allocation. Is this already sufficient diversification?
  • Risk parity approximation: Weights inversely proportional to volatility, equalizing each asset's risk contribution. A risk management mindset of "not betting on any single asset."

All portfolios use annual rebalancing (reset to target weights each January), with assets drifting during the year. Annual rather than monthly/quarterly rebalancing balances "maintaining target allocation" with "minimizing transaction costs." In practice, overly frequent rebalancing generates unnecessary fees and taxes.

2.3 風險指標2.3 Risk Metrics

本研究使用三個互補的風險指標，因為單一指標無法完整描述風險。用體檢報告來比喻：量血壓只能反映心血管健康，驗血糖反映代謝功能，照 X 光反映骨骼狀態——你需要多種檢查才能全面了解身體狀況。投資風險也一樣：

  • 最大回撤（MaxDD）：從高點到低點的最大跌幅，衡量最糟情境的損失幅度。這是投資人「肚子痛指數」——你投入 100 萬，看到帳面最多虧了多少？如果 MaxDD 是 -40%，代表你的 100 萬曾經一度只剩 60 萬。很多人就是在這個時候忍不住「停損」（在最低點賣出），把帳面虧損變成真實虧損。MaxDD 對投資人的心理衝擊最直接。
  • CVaR（5%）：條件風險值，即最差 5% 情境下的平均損失。比 VaR（只告訴你「95% 的情況下損失不會超過多少」）更進一步——CVaR 告訴你「如果真的掉進那最慘的 5% 情境，平均會虧多少？」這就像保險公司不只想知道「很少失火」，更想知道「一旦失火，平均損失多大？」——後者才是決定保費的關鍵。
  • 年化波動度：月報酬標準差乘以 sqrt(12)，衡量整體波動程度。波動度高代表價格上上下下劇烈——即使最終賺了錢，過程中的心臟負擔也很大。波動度低代表價格走勢平穩，投資人晚上比較好睡覺。

Three complementary risk metrics are used, because no single metric fully captures risk. Like a health checkup: blood pressure alone only shows cardiovascular health, blood sugar shows metabolism, X-rays show bones — you need multiple tests for a complete picture. Investment risk is the same:

  • Max Drawdown (MaxDD): Peak-to-trough decline, measuring worst-case loss. This is the investor's "stomach-ache index" — you invest NT$1M, what's the most you see lost on paper? If MaxDD is -40%, your NT$1M once shrank to NT$600K. Many investors capitulate at this point (selling at the bottom), turning paper losses into real ones. MaxDD has the most direct psychological impact.
  • CVaR (5%): Conditional Value-at-Risk — the average loss in the worst 5% of scenarios. Goes beyond VaR (which only says "95% of the time, losses won't exceed X") by asking "if we're in that worst 5%, how bad does it get on average?" Like an insurer not just wanting to know "fires rarely happen" but "when fire does happen, what's the average loss?" — the latter determines premiums.
  • Annualized Volatility: Monthly return std * sqrt(12), measuring overall fluctuation. High volatility means wild price swings — even if you profit in the end, the ride is nerve-wracking. Low volatility means smoother performance and better sleep.

2.4 統計方法2.4 Statistical Methods

本研究使用三種統計方法來檢驗三個假說。每種方法都是針對特定問題的「最佳工具」：

  • Block Bootstrap（區塊自助法）：10,000 次區塊重抽（block size = 12 個月），計算五資產組合與單一資產 MaxDD 差異的 95% 信賴區間。為什麼用「區塊」而非逐月重抽？因為金融市場有「動量效應」——這個月跌了，下個月也容易跌（報酬具時間序列相依性）。如果把每個月打散隨機抽，就會破壞這種連續性，導致模擬出來的「假危機」比真實危機溫和。以 12 個月為一個區塊重抽，能保留這種連續性。想像在玩撲克牌時，不是一張一張洗牌，而是以一疊為單位重排——這樣每疊內的順序（連續幾個月的走勢）就被保留下來了。
  • Fisher z-transform：比較危機期間 vs 正常期間的相關係數差異。直接比較兩個相關係數是不行的——因為相關係數的分佈不是常態的（越接近 +1 或 -1 越偏）。Fisher z-transform 會先將相關係數轉換到一個分佈接近常態的空間，然後再做比較。這就像量體溫用攝氏和華氏的轉換——數字不同，但反映的是同一個真實溫度。危機期間定義為：2008 金融海嘯、2011 歐債危機、2015 中國股災、2018 升息恐慌、2020 COVID、2022 升息+通膨。
  • 非線性曲線擬合：以 sigma(N) = sigma_sys + sigma_idio / sqrt(N) 模型擬合個股模擬數據。這個公式的直覺意義是：持有 N 檔股票的風險 = 無法消除的市場風險 + 可消除的個股風險 / sqrt(N)。隨著 N 增加，分母越大、個股風險越小，但市場風險是一個固定的「地板」。通過擬合可以精確估計這個「地板」有多高——也就是說，即使你買了全市場所有股票，風險最低只能降到多少。

Three statistical methods are used to test three hypotheses. Each is the optimal tool for its specific question:

  • Block Bootstrap: 10,000 block resamples (block size = 12 months) for 95% CI on MaxDD difference between five-asset and single-asset portfolios. Why "blocks" instead of individual months? Financial markets have momentum — a down month tends to be followed by another down month (serial dependence). Shuffling individual months would destroy this continuity, making simulated "crises" milder than real ones. 12-month blocks preserve this structure. Imagine shuffling cards in stacks rather than individually — the order within each stack (consecutive months of trends) is preserved.
  • Fisher z-transform: Compares correlation coefficients between crisis and normal periods. Directly comparing two correlations is invalid because correlation distributions are non-normal (skewed near +/-1). Fisher z-transform first converts correlations to a near-normal space, then compares. Like converting Celsius to Fahrenheit — different numbers reflecting the same temperature. Crisis periods defined as: 2008 GFC, 2011 European debt, 2015 China crash, 2018 rate scare, 2020 COVID, 2022 rate hikes + inflation.
  • Nonlinear curve fitting: Fits sigma(N) = sigma_sys + sigma_idio / sqrt(N) to stock simulation data. Intuitive meaning: risk of holding N stocks = irreducible market risk + reducible idiosyncratic risk / sqrt(N). As N grows, the denominator increases and idiosyncratic risk shrinks, but market risk is a fixed "floor." Fitting precisely estimates this floor — how low can risk go even if you buy every stock in the market?

2.5 交易成本假設2.5 Transaction Cost Assumptions

本研究的主要分析不含交易成本，理由如下：(1) 年度再平衡頻率低，每年僅一次買賣調整，成本影響有限；(2) 各組合的再平衡成本差異不大（都是年度一次），因此不影響組合間的相對比較；(3) 現代 ETF 的交易成本已大幅降低（台股 ETF 手續費約 0.1425%，賣出交易稅 0.1%；美股 ETF 多數零手續費）。

但讀者應注意：如果採用更頻繁的再平衡策略（如月度或觸發式），交易成本會顯著增加。此外，台灣投資人購買海外 ETF 還需考慮匯款手續費和可能的信託管理費，這些在長期持有的情境下影響較小，但在頻繁調整時不可忽視。

Our main analysis excludes transaction costs because: (1) annual rebalancing means only one round of trades per year, with limited cost impact; (2) rebalancing costs are similar across portfolios (all annual), so relative comparisons are unaffected; (3) modern ETF trading costs are very low (Taiwan ETF: ~0.1425% fee + 0.1% tax; US ETFs: mostly commission-free).

However, readers should note: more frequent rebalancing (monthly or threshold-based) would significantly increase costs. Additionally, Taiwanese investors buying overseas ETFs incur wire transfer fees and potential trust management fees — minor for long-term holding but significant with frequent adjustments.
