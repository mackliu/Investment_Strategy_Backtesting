V-006：分散投資就能降低風險？V-006: Does Diversification Always Reduce Risk?

研究問題：「不要把雞蛋放在同一個籃子裡」是投資圈最常見的建議之一。但分散投資真的能在所有市場環境下降低風險嗎？在市場崩盤時，分散投資還有效嗎？

Research Question: "Don't put all your eggs in one basket" is one of the most common investment advice. But does diversification really reduce risk in all market conditions? Does it still work during market crashes?

摘要Abstract

「不要把所有雞蛋放在同一個籃子裡」可能是投資界最古老也最普遍的建議。但這個看似簡單的道理，實際操作起來充滿了細節和陷阱。你需要幾個籃子？裝什麼雞蛋？而且更重要的問題是：如果整棟房子都在地震，不管你有幾個籃子，雞蛋都可能全碎。本研究就是要用數據來回答這些問題。

本研究以台灣投資人的視角，分析五類資產（台股 0050、美股 SPY、美債 TLT、黃金 GLD、不動產 VNQ）的跨資產分散效果，並以台股個股模擬檢驗同市場分散的邊際效益。分析期間為 2006-05 ~ 2025-10（共 234 個月）。

核心發現：五資產等權組合年化波動度 10.0%，較 100% 台股的 18.8% 降低 8.8 個百分點——分散投資確實有效。但最大回撤仍達 -24.2%。在 2008 金融海嘯等極端事件中，資產間相關性顯著上升（就像地震時所有籃子同時搖晃），分散保護效果大幅衰減。

跨資產分散比同市場分散更有效：從 1 檔台股增加到 50 檔的波動度降幅有限（只能消除個股風險），而跨入不同資產類別（尤其債券與黃金）才能實質降低系統性風險。分散的邊際效益遞減，5 類資產已能捕捉大部分分散效益。

'Don't put all your eggs in one basket' may be the oldest and most universal investment advice. But this seemingly simple wisdom is full of practical nuances. How many baskets? What goes in them? And crucially: if the whole house is shaking in an earthquake, all baskets may break regardless. This study uses data to answer these questions.

This study analyzes cross-asset diversification across five asset classes (0050, SPY, TLT, GLD, VNQ) from a Taiwanese investor's perspective, and examines marginal benefits of same-market diversification through stock simulation. Period: 2006-05 ~ 2025-10 (234 months).

Key findings: Five-asset EW portfolio volatility 10.0%, down 8.8 pp from 100% Taiwan (18.8%) — diversification works. But max drawdown still reaches -24.2%. During extreme events like the 2008 GFC, correlations surge (all baskets shaking simultaneously), drastically reducing protection.

Cross-asset is more effective than same-market diversification: adding more Taiwan stocks has limited effect (only eliminates idiosyncratic risk), while adding different asset classes (especially bonds and gold) meaningfully reduces systematic risk. Marginal benefits diminish — five asset classes capture most diversification benefits.
