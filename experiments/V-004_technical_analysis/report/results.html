3.2 交易成本的影響3.2 Transaction Cost Impact

計入交易成本後，所有策略的績效進一步惡化。交易頻率最高的 MACD（年均 10 次交易）和 MA(5,20)（年均 8 次）受影響最大。MA(5,20) 的成本拖累高達 3.28 個百分點。

這反映了一個重要的實務問題：即使某個策略在不含成本下有微弱的正超額報酬，交易成本也會輕易抹去這個優勢。

After costs, all strategies deteriorate further. The highest-frequency MACD (avg 10 trades/year) and MA(5,20) (avg 8) are hit hardest. MA(5,20)'s cost drag is 3.28 pp.

This highlights a critical practical issue: even if a strategy has marginal positive excess returns before costs, transaction costs easily eliminate the advantage.

3.3 統計檢定結果3.3 Statistical Test Results

Permutation Test 結果：所有 8 個策略在不含成本下均無法拒絕虛無假說（p 值範圍 0.1478 ~ 0.7912），Holm-Bonferroni 校正後 p 值全部 = 1.000。無一策略的超額報酬達到統計顯著。

Bootstrap 95% CI 結果：多數策略的信賴區間包含 0 或完全為負，進一步確認無顯著超額報酬。Cohen's d 效果量均 < 0.05（微小效果），表示即使有差異，實際意義也極為有限。

Permutation Test results: All 8 strategies (before costs) fail to reject the null hypothesis (p-values range 0.1478 ~ 0.7912). After Holm-Bonferroni correction, all adjusted p-values = 1.000. No strategy achieves statistically significant excess returns.

Bootstrap 95% CI: Most strategies' CIs include 0 or are entirely negative, further confirming no significant excess returns. Cohen's d effect sizes are all < 0.05 (trivial), meaning even if differences exist, their practical significance is negligible.
