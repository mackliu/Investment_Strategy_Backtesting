V-004：技術分析能預測股價？V-004: Can Technical Analysis Predict Stock Prices?

實證結果Results

以下依序呈現 8 個技術分析策略在不同面向的表現。每張圖表後附有詳細解讀，幫助讀者理解數據的含義。核心結論可以先劇透：沒有任何一個策略能打敗單純的買進持有。

結果分六大面向呈現：(1) 績效總覽——年化報酬、風險、交易頻率的完整比較；(2) 交易成本影響——從理想世界到現實世界；(3) 統計檢定——確認差異非巧合；(4) 持倉比例——揭示落後的根本原因；(5) 訊號範例——視覺化理解落後指標的本質；(6) 子期間穩健性——排除特定時期偏差。

Below we present 8 technical strategies across multiple dimensions. Each chart is followed by detailed interpretation. Spoiler: no strategy beats simple buy-and-hold.

Results are organized in six aspects: (1) Performance overview — complete comparison of returns, risk, and trading frequency; (2) Transaction cost impact — from ideal to real world; (3) Statistical tests — confirming differences are not coincidence; (4) Holding ratios — revealing the root cause of underperformance; (5) Signal example — visualizing the lagging indicator problem; (6) Sub-period robustness — ruling out period-specific bias.

The table below compares Buy & Hold with 8 technical strategies (before costs). This is the most important table in the study — if you only look at one result, this is it.

表格解讀：Buy & Hold 以年化 12.20% 的報酬率和 0.61 的 Sharpe ratio 領先所有策略。最佳技術策略 MA 10 50 年化僅 8.14%，落後 B&H 4.06 個百分點。注意觀察「持倉比」這一欄——它直接解釋了技術策略為何落後：策略有 30-70% 的時間在空倉（手上持有現金），而空倉期間的報酬是 0%。在一個長期向上的市場裡，少站在場內就等於少賺。

風險面：最大回撤方面，部分技術策略（如 KD、RSI）的回撤確實比 B&H（-55.7%）小，這是因為它們在大跌時碰巧空倉。但「降低回撤」的代價是「降低報酬」，而且降低報酬的幅度遠超降低回撤的幅度。波動率方面，B&H 的年化波動率為 19.9%，技術策略因為有一半以上時間報酬為 0%（空倉），波動率自然較低——但這不是「好的」低波動，而是「沒參與」的低波動。

逐策略觀察：MA(10,50) 持倉比最高（61%），年化 8.14%，是最接近 B&H 的策略，但即便如此仍落後 4.06 個百分點。KD(9,3,3) 持倉比最低（31%），年化僅 1.72%——超過 69% 的時間空倉，等於有七成時間站在場外看市場上漲。MA(50,200) 的黃金/死亡交叉策略（金融媒體最愛）交易次數最少，但空倉比例仍然顯著影響報酬。布林通道和 RSI 兩個均值回歸策略的持倉比也偏低，反映了嚴格的進出場條件導致大部分時間無持倉。

Table reading: B&H leads all strategies with 12.20% annualized and 0.61 Sharpe. Best strategy MA 10 50 achieves only 8.14%, trailing by 4.06 pp. Note the 'Hold%' column — it directly explains why TA underperforms: strategies are in cash 30-70% of the time (0% return), and in a long-term upward market, less time invested = less earned.

Risk side: Some strategies (KD, RSI) have lower max drawdowns than B&H (-55.7%) by being in cash during crashes. But the 'cost' of lower drawdown is lower returns — and the return reduction far exceeds the drawdown reduction. Volatility: B&H's annualized volatility is 19.9%; TA strategies show lower volatility simply because they earn 0% (cash) more than half the time — this is not 'good' low volatility, but 'absent' low volatility.

Per-strategy observations: MA(10,50) has the highest holding ratio (61%), annualized 8.14%, closest to B&H — yet still trails by 4.06 pp. KD(9,3,3) has the lowest holding ratio (31%), annualized only 1.72% — spending 69% of time watching from the sidelines. MA(50,200) golden/death cross (financial media favorite) has the fewest trades but cash drag still significantly hurts returns. Bollinger Bands and RSI (mean-reversion strategies) also show low holding ratios due to strict entry/exit thresholds.

累積報酬圖解讀：Buy & Hold 的深色粗線始終位於最上方，而且差距隨時間越來越大——這是複利效應的威力。B&H 累積報酬 1007%（即 1 元變 11.1 元），而最佳策略 MA 10 50 的累積報酬僅 412%。

即使技術策略在某些局部時段暫時領先（例如在大跌時空倉避開了下跌），但只要市場隨後反彈，沒有持倉的策略就會再度落後。技術分析策略的累積報酬曲線像一條條「階梯」——持有時跟市場一起漲，空倉時走平。長期下來，這些「走平」的片段累積起來，就是和 Buy & Hold 之間越來越大的差距。

特別注意 2008 年金融海嘯和 2020 年 COVID-19 崩盤期間：部分策略（如 MA 交叉）確實在這些急跌中因空倉而「避開了」下跌。然而，避開下跌之後，通常也會延遲重新進場（因為均線需要時間重新交叉），導致錯過了反彈初期最迅猛的漲幅。市場大跌後的反彈往往集中在最初的幾個交易日——這正是技術分析落後指標的致命弱點。

Cumulative return reading: B&H's dark thick line stays on top and the gap widens over time - the power of compounding. B&H cumulative return: 1007% (i.e., $1 becomes $11.1), while the best strategy MA 10 50 achieves only 412%.

Even when strategies temporarily lead by avoiding a crash (being in cash), once the market rebounds, cash-holding strategies fall behind again. TA cumulative curves look like "staircases" - rising with the market when invested, flat when in cash. Over time, these "flat" segments accumulate into an ever-growing gap vs B&H.

Note the 2008 financial crisis and 2020 COVID crash: some strategies (e.g., MA crossovers) did "avoid" the decline by being in cash. However, they also delayed re-entry (MAs need time to cross back), missing the most explosive initial rebound. Post-crash rebounds are often concentrated in the first few trading days - precisely the fatal weakness of lagging indicators.

3.2 交易成本的影響3.2 Transaction Cost Impact

上面的分析還是「理想世界」——不含交易成本。在現實世界中，每次買賣都要支付手續費和交易稅。下面的圖顯示加入成本後，各策略的報酬進一步惡化了多少。

The analysis above is the "ideal world" — no transaction costs. In reality, every trade incurs fees and taxes. The chart below shows how much worse each strategy gets after costs.

成本影響解讀：計入交易成本後，所有策略的績效進一步惡化。交易頻率最高的 MACD（年均 10 次交易）和 MA(5,20)（年均 8 次）受影響最大。MA(5,20) 的成本拖累高達 3.28 個百分點。

這反映了一個重要的實務問題：即使某個策略在不含成本下有微弱的正超額報酬，交易成本也會輕易抹去這個優勢。用比喻來說：就算你很會在超市挑便宜貨（微弱的擇時能力），如果每次去超市的油錢太貴（交易成本），省下來的錢還不夠付油錢。交易頻率越高，這個問題越嚴重。

Cost impact reading: After costs, all strategies deteriorate further. MACD (avg 10 trades/year) and MA(5,20) (avg 8) are hit hardest. MA(5,20) cost drag: 3.28 pp.

This highlights a critical practical issue: even marginal excess returns before costs get easily wiped out. Analogy: even if you are great at finding bargains at the supermarket (marginal timing ability), if gas to get there costs more than you save (transaction costs), the trip is not worth it. Higher frequency = worse.

3.3 統計檢定結果3.3 Statistical Test Results

光看績效表的數字，似乎已經很明顯——技術策略全部落後。但科學研究不能只看「大概」，需要正式的統計檢定來確認差異不是巧合。以下是 Permutation Test 的結果。

The performance table already seems conclusive — all TA strategies lag. But scientific research cannot rely on "looks like"; formal tests are needed to confirm the difference is not coincidence.

Permutation Test 結果：所有 8 個策略在不含成本下均無法拒絕虛無假說（p 值範圍 0.1478 ~ 0.7912），Holm-Bonferroni 校正後 p 值全部 = 1.000。無一策略的超額報酬達到統計顯著。用白話來說：這 8 個策略的表現，和「隨機決定何時買賣」沒有統計上的差別。技術分析指標給出的訊號，在統計上等同於擲銅板決定。

Bootstrap 95% CI：多數策略的信賴區間包含 0 或完全為負，進一步確認無顯著超額報酬。Cohen's d 效果量均 < 0.05（微小效果），表示即使有差異，實際意義也極為有限——就像兩杯水的溫度差了 0.01 度，理論上有差，但你根本感覺不到。

Permutation Test results: All 8 strategies (before costs) fail to reject the null (p-values range 0.1478 ~ 0.7912). After Holm-Bonferroni correction, all adjusted p-values = 1.000. In plain language: these 8 strategies perform no differently from random buy/sell decisions. TA signals are statistically equivalent to coin flips.

Bootstrap 95% CI: Most CIs include 0 or are entirely negative, confirming no significant excess returns. Cohen's d all < 0.05 (trivial), meaning any differences are practically meaningless — like two cups of water differing by 0.01 degrees: theoretically different but you cannot feel it.

排列檢定圖解讀：上圖的 8 個直方圖分別顯示各策略的 Permutation Test 結果。灰色柱狀為 10,000 次隨機排列的超額報酬分佈（代表「隨機擇時」的結果），紅色虛線為真實策略的超額報酬位置。

如果紅線在分佈的最右邊（前 5%），代表策略顯著優於隨機。但實際上，所有策略的紅線都落在分佈的中間或左側，代表它們的表現甚至不如部分隨機策略。這個結果極具震撼力——它直接說明了：仔細研究技術指標所做出的買賣決策，在統計上和「隨機決定何時買賣」沒有差別。

進一步觀察各策略的 p 值差異：最低 p 值為 0.1478（最接近顯著但仍遠未達標），最高 p 值為 0.7912。即使不做多重比較校正，也沒有任何策略的 p 值低於 0.05。校正後全部 = 1.000，代表結論極為穩健——不是因為門檻過高才不顯著，而是策略本身就沒有預測力。

Permutation test chart reading: The 8 histograms show each strategy's Permutation Test results. Gray bars = distribution of 10,000 random timing permutations (representing random buy/sell decisions); red dashed line = the real strategy's excess return.

If the red line falls in the rightmost 5%, the strategy significantly outperforms random. In reality, all red lines fall in the middle or left, meaning they perform no better than some random strategies. This result is striking — it directly demonstrates that carefully researched TA buy/sell decisions are statistically indistinguishable from random timing.

Examining p-value variation: the lowest is 0.1478 (closest to significant but still far from the threshold), highest is 0.7912. Even without multiple comparison correction, no strategy's p-value falls below 0.05. After correction, all = 1.000, confirming the conclusion is extremely robust — strategies lack predictive power, not because the bar is too high.

3.4 持倉比例分析3.4 Holding Ratio Analysis

技術分析策略的一大隱性成本是「空倉時間」。在多頭市場中，空倉意味著錯過上漲。下圖分析各策略有多少時間在場內（持倉）vs 場外（空倉）。

A hidden cost of technical strategies is "time out of market." In bull markets, cash = missing gains. The chart below shows how much time each strategy spends invested vs in cash.

持倉比例解讀：各策略的持倉比例從 31%（KD）到 61%（MA 10 50）不等。持倉比例越低的策略，錯失的市場上漲越多。一個簡單的推算：如果市場年化報酬 12%，持倉比例 50% 的策略理論上只能獲得約 6% 的報酬——即使它的擇時完全沒有價值（隨機決定持有或空倉）。

實際結果和這個簡單推算高度吻合，進一步證實技術分析的擇時沒有增加額外價值。我們可以做一個「擇時品質」的粗略檢驗：如果策略有正向擇時能力（在上漲日持有、下跌日空倉），其報酬應該高於「持倉比 x 市場報酬」。但數據顯示多數策略的報酬接近甚至低於此基線，代表技術指標不僅沒有選到好的日子，反而可能選到了稍差的日子。

為什麼空倉是最大成本？在長期上漲的市場中，每一天不在場都是一次「放棄」。台股在過去 21 年的日報酬率平均為正，這意味著持倉越多天，期望報酬越高。技術分析試圖「擇時」——只在上漲時持有——但結果顯示它無法準確區分上漲和下跌的日子。不如全部持有，把「隨機猜測哪天會漲」的徒勞省下來。

Holding ratio reading: Ratios range from 31% (KD) to 61% (MA 10 50). Lower ratios = more missed market gains. Simple math: if the market returns 12% annually, a 50% holding ratio strategy theoretically captures only ~6% - even with zero timing value.

Actual results closely match this calculation, confirming TA timing adds no extra value. A rough "timing quality" test: if strategies had positive timing ability (invested on up days, cash on down days), returns should exceed "holding ratio x market return." Data shows most strategies return near or below this baseline, suggesting TA signals select slightly worse-than-average days rather than better ones.

Why cash is the biggest cost: In a long-term rising market, every day out of the market is a "missed opportunity." Taiwan stocks averaged positive daily returns over the past 21 years, meaning more days invested = higher expected returns. TA tries to "time" - hold only during rises - but it cannot reliably distinguish up days from down days. Better to hold continuously and save the futile effort of guessing which days will rise.

3.5 訊號範例3.5 Signal Example

下圖以 MA(10,50) 為範例，展示近期的買賣訊號在股價圖上的分佈。綠色三角形為買進訊號，紅色倒三角為賣出訊號。觀察重點：(1) 訊號是否在「對的時間」出現？(2) 是否有明顯的「買在高點、賣在低點」的情況？

The chart below uses MA(10,50) as an example, showing recent buy/sell signals on the price chart. Green triangles = buy, red inverted triangles = sell. Key observations: (1) Do signals appear at the "right time"? (2) Are there cases of buying high and selling low?

從訊號圖可以看到技術分析的根本問題：均線交叉是「落後指標」——它在趨勢已經形成一段時間後才給出訊號。當黃金交叉出現時，股價往往已經漲了一段；當死亡交叉出現時，股價已經跌了一段。結果就是「追高殺低」——用生活化的比喻來說，就像看後照鏡開車：你只看到已經路過的路，不知道前面有什麼。

The signal chart reveals TA's fundamental problem: MA crossovers are "lagging indicators" — they signal after a trend has already been underway for some time. By the time a golden cross appears, prices have already risen; by the death cross, they have already fallen. The result is "buying high, selling low" — like driving by looking in the rearview mirror: you see where you have been, not what is ahead.

3.6 子期間穩健性3.6 Sub-Period Robustness

一個策略如果只在某個特定時期有效，其他時期無效，那它的實用價值就很有限。為了測試結論的穩健性，我們將分析期間分為兩組子期間（等分：2004-2013 / 2014-2025；結構斷點：2004-2016 / 2017-2025），分別檢驗策略績效。

A strategy effective only in specific periods has limited practical value. To test robustness, we split the analysis period into two sub-period sets (equal: 2004-2013/2014-2025; structural break: 2004-2016/2017-2025).

子期間結果：所有策略在各子期間均無法打敗 Buy & Hold。更重要的是，不同子期間之間的「最佳」策略也不一致——前半段表現較好的策略在後半段可能表現較差，反之亦然。這意味著即使你回顧過去的數據找到了「最佳」策略，它在未來的表現也無法保證。這正是 Sullivan et al. (1999) 所警告的數據窺探問題。

等分切分觀察：2004-2013 期間涵蓋了金融海嘯（2008）和歐債危機（2011），市場波動較大，但即使在這樣「有利於技術分析」的環境中（高波動理論上提供更多擇時機會），技術策略仍無法勝出。2014-2025 期間以穩定上漲為主，技術策略因空倉損失更為嚴重。

結構斷點觀察：2017 年後台股結構性變化（ETF 普及、外資活躍、散戶年輕化），市場效率理論上進一步提高。如果技術分析在「效率較低」的 2004-2016 期間都無效，那麼在「效率更高」的 2017-2025 期間更不太可能有效——數據也確認了這個預期。這與 Lo (2004) AMH 的框架一致：隨著市場參與者增加和資訊傳播加速，套利機會持續減少。

Sub-period results: All strategies fail to beat B&H in every sub-period. Importantly, the "best" strategy differs between periods - one that does relatively well in the first half may do poorly in the second. This means even if you find the "best" strategy in hindsight, its future performance is not guaranteed - exactly the data snooping problem Sullivan et al. (1999) warned about.

Equal split: The 2004-2013 period includes the financial crisis (2008) and European debt crisis (2011) with higher volatility, which theoretically favors TA (more timing opportunities). Yet TA strategies still fail. The 2014-2025 period is predominantly bullish, where cash drag is even more damaging for TA strategies.

Structural break: Post-2017, Taiwan market underwent structural shifts (ETF proliferation, active foreign investors, younger retail traders), theoretically increasing market efficiency. If TA was already ineffective in the "less efficient" 2004-2016, it is even less likely to work in the "more efficient" 2017-2025 - and data confirms this expectation. This aligns with Lo (2004) AMH: as participants increase and information spreads faster, arbitrage opportunities continuously diminish.
