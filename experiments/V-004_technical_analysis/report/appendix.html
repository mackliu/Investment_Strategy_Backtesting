關於本研究

本研究屬於「用數據說話：投資策略回測研究」系列的第四項實驗（V-004）。整個系列共 13 項實驗，分別驗證市面上常見的投資論點。本實驗聚焦於技術分析的有效性——這可能是台灣投資市場中流傳最廣、信徒最多的投資方法。我們希望透過嚴謹的數據分析，幫助投資人用科學的眼光審視這些「投資常識」。

所有程式碼、數據和分析結果均可在 GitHub 上取得，確保研究的可重現性和透明度。

Below are supplementary charts not discussed in detail in the main text, validating conclusions from different angles for readers who want to explore further.

About This Study

This study is Experiment V-004 in the "Let Data Speak: Investment Strategy Backtesting" series. The full series comprises 13 experiments, each testing a common investment belief. This experiment focuses on technical analysis effectiveness — arguably the most widely followed and believed investment method in Taiwan. We aim to help investors evaluate these "investment truisms" through rigorous data analysis.

All code, data, and results are available on GitHub, ensuring reproducibility and transparency.

A.2 回撤比較A.2 Drawdown Comparison

回撤圖展示各策略從歷史高點回落的幅度。技術分析策略唯一的「亮點」就是在這張圖上——部分策略在大跌時碰巧空倉，因此回撤較小。例如在 2008 年金融海嘯期間，B&H 的最大回撤達 -55.7%，而某些策略因為空倉而回撤較小。

但這個優勢需要和「長期報酬大幅落後」一起衡量：B&H 累積報酬 1007%，最佳技術策略僅 412%。為了避開一次大回撤（雖然之後市場也完全反彈了），你放棄了數倍的累積報酬——這筆交易是否划算？用數字來說：假設你投資 100 萬，B&H 最終變成 1107 萬；最佳技術策略最終只有 512 萬。多數理性投資人會選擇接受暫時的回撤以換取更高的終值。

Drawdown chart shows how far each strategy falls from peak. TA's only "bright spot" is here — some strategies have smaller drawdowns by being in cash during crashes. During the 2008 crisis, B&H drew down -55.7%, while some strategies had smaller drawdowns.

But this advantage must be weighed against much lower cumulative returns: B&H cumulative 1007%, best TA only 412%. To avoid one large drawdown (which the market fully recovered from), you give up multiples of cumulative return. In numbers: $1M invested, B&H ends at $1107K; best TA ends at $512K. Most rational investors would accept temporary drawdowns for higher terminal value.

A.3 年度訊號頻率A.3 Annual Signal Frequency

各策略每年產生的交易訊號數量。訊號頻率直接影響交易成本：短期策略（MA(5,20) 年均 ~15 次、MACD 年均 ~19 次）每年的成本佔年報酬 5-7%；長期策略（MA(50,200) 年均 ~1 次）成本較低。

但即使成本最低的長期策略，其報酬仍不及 Buy & Hold——因為空倉時間才是主要的拖累。這形成了一個「兩難困境」：交易頻率高的策略有更多「擇時機會」但成本太高；交易頻率低的策略成本較低但空倉太久。問題的根源不在於頻率設定不當，而在於技術指標本身缺乏可靠的預測力。

另一個有趣的觀察：訊號頻率在不同年份之間也有顯著變化——波動率高的年份（如 2008、2020）通常產生更多訊號，而穩定上漲的年份訊號較少。這反映了技術指標的本質：它們是價格變動的「反射」，而非「預測」。

Annual signal counts by strategy. Signal frequency directly impacts costs: short-term strategies (MA(5,20) avg ~15/year, MACD avg ~19/year) cost 5-7% of annual returns; long-term strategies (MA(50,200) avg ~1/year) have lower costs.

But even the lowest-cost strategies underperform B&H — cash drag is the main culprit. This creates a "dilemma": high-frequency strategies have more timing opportunities but excessive costs; low-frequency strategies have lower costs but too much cash time. The root problem is not frequency calibration but that indicators themselves lack reliable predictive power.

An interesting observation: signal frequency varies significantly across years — high-volatility years (2008, 2020) typically generate more signals, while steady bull years produce fewer. This reflects the nature of TA indicators: they are "reflections" of price movements, not "predictions."

A.4 統計檢定完整結果A.4 Complete Statistical Test Results

下表為所有策略的 Permutation Test、Bootstrap CI、Cohen's d 完整結果。

閱讀指南：

  • excess_return_ann：策略相對 B&H 的年化超額報酬。全部為負值，代表所有策略均落後。
  • perm_p_value：原始 Permutation Test p 值。若 < 0.05 代表策略顯著優於隨機。全部 > 0.05。
  • adjusted_p_value：Holm-Bonferroni 校正後的 p 值。全部 = 1.0000，代表經過多重比較校正後更加不顯著。
  • bootstrap_ci_low / _high：超額報酬的 95% 信賴區間。如果區間包含 0，代表超額報酬可能為零。多數策略的區間完全位於負值區域。
  • cohens_d：效果量指標。< 0.2 = 微小效果、0.2-0.5 = 小效果、0.5-0.8 = 中效果、> 0.8 = 大效果。本研究所有策略的 Cohen's d 均 < 0.05，屬於「微小」效果——即使統計上顯著（實際並非如此），實際差異也極為有限。

Complete Permutation Test, Bootstrap CI, and Cohen's d results.

Reading guide:

  • excess_return_ann: Strategy's annualized excess return vs B&H. All negative, meaning all strategies trail.
  • perm_p_value: Raw Permutation Test p-value. < 0.05 would indicate strategy significantly outperforms random. All > 0.05.
  • adjusted_p_value: After Holm-Bonferroni correction. All = 1.0000, even more insignificant after multiple comparison adjustment.
  • bootstrap_ci_low / _high: 95% CI of excess return. If includes 0, excess may be zero. Most are entirely in negative territory.
  • cohens_d: Effect size. < 0.2 = trivial, 0.2-0.5 = small, 0.5-0.8 = medium, > 0.8 = large. All strategies show Cohen's d < 0.05 (trivial) — even if statistically significant (they are not), practical differences would be negligible.

A.5 子期間績效表A.5 Sub-Period Performance Table

各策略在不同子期間的年化報酬與超額報酬。重點觀察：是否有策略在所有子期間都一致地接近 B&H？如果某策略在前半段接近但後半段大幅落後（或反之），代表其表現不穩定、不可靠。結果顯示所有策略在各子期間均落後 B&H，且「相對最佳」的策略在不同時期不同。

Annualized and excess returns by sub-period. Key question: does any strategy consistently approach B&H across all periods? If a strategy is close in one half but far behind in the other, its performance is unstable and unreliable. Results show all strategies trail B&H in every sub-period, with the "relatively best" strategy changing between periods.
