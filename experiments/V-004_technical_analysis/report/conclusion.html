V-004：技術分析能預測股價？V-004: Can Technical Analysis Predict Stock Prices?

結論Conclusion

5.1 核心結論5.1 Core Conclusions

本研究以 0050 ETF 為標的，測試 8 個常見技術分析策略（4 組 MA 交叉、KD、MACD、RSI、布林通道）在 2004-2025 年間（約 21 年、5264 個交易日）的績效。經過嚴謹的統計檢定（Permutation Test 10,000 次排列 + Holm-Bonferroni 多重比較校正 + Bootstrap 95% CI + Cohen's d 效果量），我們得出以下結論：

This study tested 8 common TA strategies (4 MA crossovers, KD, MACD, RSI, Bollinger Bands) on 0050 ETF over 2004-2025 (~21 years, 5264 trading days). After rigorous testing (10,000-permutation test + Holm-Bonferroni correction + Bootstrap 95% CI + Cohen's d), we conclude:

  1. 0/8 策略達到統計顯著：所有策略無一在統計上顯著優於 B&H。原始 Permutation p 值範圍 0.1478 ~ 0.7912（無一 < 0.05），Holm-Bonferroni 校正後全部 = 1.000。Cohen's d 效果量全部 < 0.05（微小效果）。B&H 年化 12.20%、Sharpe 0.61，最佳策略 MA 10 50 僅 8.14%。技術分析的擇時能力在統計上與隨機決策無異。
  2. 空倉是落後主因：技術策略有 39%-69% 的時間空倉。在年化 12.2% 的市場中，空倉時間越多，報酬越差。各策略的報酬大致等於「持倉比 x 市場報酬」，證明擇時未帶來額外價值。在長期多頭市場中，「不在場」是最大的成本。
  3. 交易成本使情況雪上加霜：含成本後，最佳策略從 8.14% 降至 6.72%。高頻策略（如 MA 5,20 年均 ~15 次交易）的年化成本拖累高達 3.3 個百分點。即使策略有微弱的擇時能力（實際上沒有），成本也足以抹去。
  4. 結論跨子期間穩健：無論是等分切分（2004-2013 / 2014-2025）或結構斷點切分（2004-2016 / 2017-2025），所有策略在每個子期間均無法打敗 B&H。「最佳」策略在不同時期不一致，排除了特定時期偏差，也與 AMH 預期的「效率隨時間提高」一致。
  5. MA(10,50) 相對最佳但仍不足：持倉比 61% 使其最接近 B&H，但仍落後 4.06 個百分點且統計不顯著。其「優勢」完全可用較高持倉比解釋，而非擇時能力。
  1. 0/8 strategies statistically significant: No strategy significantly outperforms B&H. Raw Permutation p-values range 0.1478 ~ 0.7912 (none < 0.05); all adjusted p-values = 1.000 after Holm-Bonferroni. Cohen's d all < 0.05 (trivial effect). B&H: 12.20% annualized, 0.61 Sharpe; best strategy MA 10 50 only 8.14%. TA timing is statistically indistinguishable from random.
  2. Cash drag is the main cause: Strategies are in cash 39%-69% of the time. In a 12.2% annualized market, more cash time = worse returns. Returns roughly equal 'holding ratio x market return,' proving timing adds no value. In a long-term bull market, 'not being there' is the biggest cost.
  3. Costs add insult to injury: After costs, best strategy drops from 8.14% to 6.72%. High-frequency strategies (MA 5,20 avg ~15 trades/year) suffer 3.3 pp annual cost drag. Even marginal timing ability (which does not exist) would be wiped out by costs.
  4. Robust across sub-periods: Whether split equally (2004-2013/2014-2025) or by structural break (2004-2016/2017-2025), all strategies fail to beat B&H in every sub-period. The "best" strategy varies between periods, ruling out period-specific bias and aligning with AMH's prediction of increasing efficiency over time.
  5. MA(10,50) relatively best but still insufficient: Its 61% holding ratio makes it closest to B&H, but still trails by 4.06 pp and is not significant. Its 'advantage' is fully explained by higher time-in-market, not timing skill.

一句話總結：對於投資 0050 的一般投資人而言，技術分析（標準教科書版本）不僅無法幫你賺更多，反而因為頻繁空倉和交易成本，讓你賺得更少。最佳策略就是最簡單的那個——買進持有。

One-line summary: For 0050 investors, standard textbook TA not only fails to earn more, but causes you to earn less due to frequent cash periods and transaction costs. The best strategy is the simplest — buy and hold.

5.2 研究限制總結5.2 Summary of Limitations

引用結論時請注意以下限制：

  1. 標的限制：僅測試 0050 一檔 ETF。0050 追蹤台灣 50 指數，是高度分散化、高流動性的大盤標的。技術分析在個股（尤其是資訊不對稱的中小型股）、商品、外匯、加密貨幣等市場的表現可能不同。本研究的結論適用範圍為「台灣大盤 ETF」，不應過度推廣。
  2. 參數限制：僅使用業界標準參數（如 MA 50/200、KD 9/3/3），不做最佳化。最佳化後的參數在樣本內可能表現更好，但面臨嚴重的過擬合風險——且即使最佳化後通過了統計檢定，也無法保證在未來的樣本外期間同樣有效。使用標準參數是對技術分析「最公平」的測試方式。
  3. 策略類型限制：僅測試純機械式策略（依照固定規則進出場），不包含主觀判斷、多指標複合策略、或結合基本面/籌碼面的綜合策略。技術指標作為「工具箱中的一項輔助工具」可能有價值，但作為「唯一交易規則」則無效。
  4. 時期偏差風險：2004-2025 年涵蓋了一個主要的長期多頭趨勢。在持續盤整或空頭主導的市場中，某些技術策略（特別是震盪指標如 KD、RSI）的相對表現可能不同。子期間分析雖然減輕了此顧慮，但無法完全排除。
  5. 執行假設：T+1 收盤價執行、無滑價。實務上的滑價和流動性問題（尤其在開盤跳空時）可能使實際報酬略差於回測結果。
  6. 空倉假設：空倉期間報酬 = 0%，未計入貨幣市場基金利息（約 1-1.5% 年化）。即使加入此利息，也僅增加約 0.3-0.7%，遠不足以改變「落後 B&H」的結論。
  7. 未考慮放空：本研究假設空倉時持有現金，不做空。如果允許放空（在賣出信號時做空而非持有現金），某些策略在特定崩盤期間可能表現不同，但長期而言，在上升趨勢中放空的期望報酬為負。

When citing conclusions, note the following limitations:

  1. Target limitation: Only 0050 ETF tested — a highly diversified, liquid market ETF. TA may perform differently on individual stocks (especially small caps with information asymmetry), commodities, forex, or crypto. Conclusions apply to "Taiwan market ETFs," not all markets.
  2. Parameter limitation: Only industry-standard parameters (MA 50/200, KD 9/3/3) without optimization. Optimized parameters may improve in-sample performance but face severe overfitting risk — and even optimized strategies passing in-sample tests may not work out-of-sample. Standard parameters provide the "fairest" test of TA.
  3. Strategy type limitation: Only pure mechanical strategies (fixed entry/exit rules), excluding subjective judgment, composite indicators, or fundamental/institutional analysis combinations. TA as "one tool in the toolbox" may have auxiliary value; as "the only trading rule" it is ineffective.
  4. Period bias risk: 2004-2025 spans a predominantly bullish long-term trend. In range-bound or bearish markets, some strategies (especially oscillators like KD, RSI) may perform differently. Sub-period analysis mitigates but cannot fully eliminate this concern.
  5. Execution assumptions: T+1 close execution, no slippage. Real-world slippage and liquidity issues (especially gap openings) may worsen actual returns vs backtest results.
  6. Cash assumption: 0% during cash periods, excluding money market interest (~1-1.5% annualized). Even with this interest, only ~0.3-0.7% improvement — far from enough to change the "trailing B&H" conclusion.
  7. No shorting: Cash during non-invested periods, no short selling. If shorting were allowed (shorting on sell signals instead of holding cash), some strategies might differ during specific crashes, but long-term shorting in an uptrend has negative expected returns.

5.3 未來研究方向5.3 Future Research

  1. 個股與跨市場測試：在台積電 (2330)、鴻海 (2317) 等個股，或美國 S&P 500、日經 225、加密貨幣等不同市場上測試相同策略。研究問題：技術分析在資訊效率較低的小型股或新興市場是否有效？如果 0050（高效率市場）無效但小型股有效，可以支持 AMH 的「效率因市場而異」預測。
  2. 複合策略：測試 MA + KD 雙重確認、RSI + MACD 組合等多指標複合策略。研究問題：多指標交叉確認是否能有效過濾假訊號？這需要解決更嚴重的多重比較問題（策略組合數量成倍增加）。
  3. 機器學習方法：使用隨機森林、LSTM、Transformer 等方法取代固定規則，測試能否捕捉非線性的技術形態。研究問題：ML 模型是否能學到人眼無法察覺的模式？但需特別注意過擬合和前視偏差——金融領域的 ML 應用失敗率極高，成功案例往往難以在樣本外重現。
  4. 條件式策略（市場狀態依賴）：分析技術分析在不同市場環境（波動率高/低、VIX 水平、趨勢/盤整）下的條件式績效。研究問題：「只在高波動時期使用技術分析、低波動時期 Buy & Hold」是否可行？如果波動率本身可以預測，這種混合策略可能有價值。
  5. 部位調整策略：從「全倉買/全倉賣」擴展為根據訊號強度調整持倉比例（如 RSI 越低持倉越多、越高持倉越少）。研究問題：連續性的部位調整是否比二元進出更有效？
  6. 高頻數據分析：使用分鐘級或 tick 級數據，測試日內技術策略。研究問題：技術分析在更短時間尺度上是否有效？注意：高頻策略的交易成本和基礎設施要求遠高於日線策略。
  7. 台灣市場效率的時間演變：使用 rolling-window 方法，追蹤台灣市場效率從 1990 年代至今的演變軌跡。研究問題：市場效率是否如 AMH 預測的那樣隨時間提高？效率的「拐點」在何時？
  1. Individual stock & cross-market: Test on TSMC (2330), Hon Hai (2317), S&P 500, Nikkei 225, or crypto markets. Research question: Is TA effective in less informationally efficient small-caps or emerging markets? If ineffective on 0050 (efficient) but effective on small-caps, it supports AMH's "efficiency varies by market."
  2. Composite strategies: Test MA+KD dual confirmation, RSI+MACD combinations. Research question: Does multi-indicator confirmation effectively filter false signals? This faces even more severe multiple comparison problems (exponentially more strategy combinations).
  3. Machine learning: Use random forests, LSTM, Transformers to replace fixed rules. Research question: Can ML models detect patterns invisible to the human eye? Beware of overfitting — ML applications in finance have very high failure rates; successes rarely reproduce out-of-sample.
  4. Conditional strategies (regime-dependent): Analyze TA performance across market regimes (high/low volatility, VIX levels, trending/ranging). Research question: Is "use TA only in high-volatility, B&H in low-volatility" viable? If volatility itself is predictable, such hybrid strategies may have value.
  5. Position sizing: Extend from all-in/all-out to signal-strength-based allocation (e.g., more invested as RSI drops, less as it rises). Research question: Is continuous position sizing more effective than binary entry/exit?
  6. High-frequency data: Use minute-level or tick-level data for intraday TA strategies. Research question: Is TA effective at shorter timescales? Note: HFT strategies have much higher cost and infrastructure requirements than daily strategies.
  7. Taiwan market efficiency evolution: Use rolling-window methods to track Taiwan market efficiency from the 1990s to present. Research question: Does efficiency increase over time as AMH predicts? When is the "tipping point"?
