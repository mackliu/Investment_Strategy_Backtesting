V-004：技術分析能預測股價？V-004: Can Technical Analysis Predict Stock Prices?

討論Discussion

4.1 主要發現4.1 Key Findings

  1. 技術分析無法打敗 Buy & Hold：所有 8 個策略的年化報酬均低於 B&H（12.20%）。最佳策略 MA 10 50 的超額報酬為 -4.06%，統計上不顯著。
  2. 空倉是主因：技術分析策略有 30%-70% 的時間空倉，而 0050 長期趨勢向上（年化 12%+），空倉 = 錯失上漲。策略的報酬損失大致等於空倉比例 × 市場報酬。
  3. 交易成本雪上加霜：高頻策略（年交易 16-20 次）的成本拖累高達 3-4 個百分點。這意味著即使有微弱的擇時能力，也會被成本抹去。
  4. 無一通過統計檢定：Permutation Test + Holm-Bonferroni 校正後，所有策略的 p 值 = 1.000。Bootstrap CI 多數包含 0 或全為負值。
  1. Technical analysis cannot beat Buy & Hold: All 8 strategies' annualized returns are below B&H (12.20%). Best strategy MA 10 50 has excess return of -4.06%, not statistically significant.
  2. Cash drag is the main cause: Strategies are in cash 30-70% of the time, while 0050's long-term trend is upward (12%+ annualized). Being in cash = missing gains. Return loss roughly equals cash ratio × market return.
  3. Transaction costs add insult to injury: High-frequency strategies (16-20 trades/year) suffer 3-4 pp cost drag, wiping out any marginal timing ability.
  4. None pass statistical tests: After Permutation + Holm-Bonferroni, all p-values = 1.000. Bootstrap CIs mostly include 0 or are entirely negative.

4.2 為什麼技術分析「感覺有用」？4.2 Why Does Technical Analysis "Feel" Effective?

既然數據顯示技術分析無效，為什麼仍然有這麼多人深信不疑？行為金融學提供了幾個令人信服的解釋：

確認偏誤（Confirmation Bias）：人腦天生傾向記住「正確」的訊號，忽略「錯誤」的訊號。當黃金交叉出現後市場確實上漲，投資人會記得這次「印證」；但當黃金交叉出現後市場下跌時，投資人傾向歸因於「其他因素」而非指標失靈。長此以往，投資人心中只保留了成功的案例，形成「技術分析很準」的錯誤印象——這就像你只記得那些「夢到的事情成真了」的時候，卻忘了絕大多數的夢完全沒有預測力。

倖存者偏差（Survivorship Bias）：你在網路上看到的「技術分析達人」都是成功者——那些用技術分析虧錢的人不會拍影片教學，也不會出書分享。這讓技術分析看起來比實際上有效得多。就像你在報紙上只看到中樂透的人，卻看不到千萬個沒中的人，所以會覺得中樂透「好像不難」。

敘事偏誤（Narrative Bias）：技術分析提供了一套完整的「故事框架」——黃金交叉、死亡交叉、支撐位、壓力位，每個詞都暗示因果關係。人腦酷愛因果敘事（「因為出現黃金交叉，所以股價上漲」），但相關不等於因果，而且我們的數據顯示，連相關都不存在。

自我實現的短暫效應：在極短的時間尺度上，當足夠多的交易者相信同一個指標並同時下單時，可能確實會短暫地推動價格。但這個效應極其短暫，且已被高頻交易商充分套利，散戶投資人幾乎不可能從中獲利。

If data shows TA is ineffective, why do so many believe in it? Behavioral finance offers compelling explanations:

Confirmation Bias: The brain naturally remembers "correct" signals and ignores "wrong" ones. When a golden cross is followed by a rally, investors remember the "validation"; when it fails, they attribute it to "other factors." Over time, only success cases are retained, creating the false impression that TA "works well" - like only remembering dreams that "came true" while forgetting the vast majority that had no predictive power.

Survivorship Bias: "TA experts" visible online are all survivors - those who lost money using TA do not make tutorial videos or publish books. This makes TA appear far more effective than it actually is - like only seeing lottery winners in newspapers, never the millions who did not win, making it seem "not that hard."

Narrative Bias: TA provides a complete "story framework" - golden cross, death cross, support, resistance - each term implying causation. The brain loves causal narratives ("because golden cross appeared, stock rose"), but correlation does not equal causation, and our data shows not even correlation exists.

Brief self-fulfilling effect: On very short timescales, when enough traders believe the same indicator and trade simultaneously, prices may briefly move. But this effect is extremely transient and already fully arbitraged by HFT firms - retail investors are virtually unable to profit from it.

4.3 為什麼 MA(10,50) 相對最佳？4.3 Why Is MA(10,50) Relatively Best?

在 8 個策略中，MA(10,50) 的年化報酬 8.14% 最接近 B&H（12.20%），雖然仍然落後 4.06 個百分點且統計不顯著，但為什麼它相對較好？

持倉比例的「甜蜜點」：MA(10,50) 的持倉比為 61%，在所有策略中最高（或接近最高）。它的均線間距適中——不像 MA(5,20) 那樣頻繁交叉產生過多假訊號，也不像 MA(50,200) 那樣反應遲鈍，錯過整個中期趨勢。10 日和 50 日的組合剛好在「過度敏感」和「過度遲鈍」之間取得平衡。

但「相對最佳」不等於「有效」：重要的是，MA(10,50) 仍然無法通過 Permutation Test——它的「優勢」完全可以用較高的持倉比來解釋（在場內時間多 = 報酬自然較高），而非歸功於擇時能力。這就好比一個學生在考場坐了最久（做最多題），成績自然比較好，但不代表他比較聰明。

Among 8 strategies, MA(10,50) achieves 8.14% annualized, closest to B&H (12.20%) — still trailing by 4.06 pp and statistically insignificant. But why is it relatively best?

Holding ratio "sweet spot": MA(10,50) has a 61% holding ratio, highest (or near-highest) among all strategies. Its MA gap is moderate — not as noisy as MA(5,20) (too many false signals) or as sluggish as MA(50,200) (missing entire mid-term trends). The 10/50 combination balances "over-sensitivity" and "over-sluggishness."

But "relatively best" is not "effective": Critically, MA(10,50) still fails the Permutation Test. Its "advantage" is entirely explained by higher holding ratio (more time invested = naturally higher returns), not timing ability. Like a student who stays longest in the exam room (attempts more questions) scoring highest — it does not mean they are smarter.

4.4 交易成本是隱形殺手4.4 Transaction Costs: The Hidden Killer

成本分析揭示了一個殘酷的現實：即使技術分析有微弱的擇時能力（本研究未發現），交易成本也足以抹去任何潛在優勢。

以 MA(5,20) 為例：年均約 15 次訊號，每次來回成本 0.385%，一年下來光成本就吃掉約 5.9% 的報酬。即使策略在不含成本下有 2% 的超額報酬（實際上連 1% 都不到），成本也會直接吞噬殆盡，反而變成負超額報酬。

反觀 Buy & Hold：20 年只交易 2 次（買入 + 最終賣出），總成本 0.385% x 1 次 = 0.385%（20 年合計！），年化不到 0.02%。這就是被動投資的成本優勢——巴菲特所說的「不做事也是一種投資智慧」。

頻率與成本的致命迴圈：短期策略（MA 5/20、MACD）訊號最多、成本最高、報酬最差；長期策略（MA 50/200）訊號最少、成本最低，但空倉時間過長。無論哪種頻率，技術分析都找不到「划算的」頻率——因為問題的根源不在頻率，而在於訊號本身缺乏預測力。

Cost analysis reveals a harsh reality: even if TA had marginal timing ability (not found in this study), transaction costs would wipe out any potential advantage.

Take MA(5,20): averaging ~15 signals/year, at 0.385% round-trip cost each, annual cost alone consumes ~5.9% of returns. Even if the strategy had 2% excess before costs (it does not even have 1%), costs would devour it entirely, turning it into negative excess returns.

Compare B&H: only 2 trades in 20 years (buy + final sell), total cost 0.385% x 1 = 0.385% (over 20 years!), annualized under 0.02%. This is the cost advantage of passive investing — Buffett's wisdom that "doing nothing is also investment intelligence."

Fatal frequency-cost loop: Short-term strategies (MA 5/20, MACD) have the most signals, highest costs, worst returns; long-term strategies (MA 50/200) have fewest signals, lowest costs, but too much cash time. No frequency works — because the root problem is not frequency but that signals lack predictive power.

4.5 適應性市場假說的視角4.5 The Adaptive Markets Perspective

Lo (2004) 的 AMH 架構為我們的結果提供了一個更細緻的解讀。AMH 不認為市場永遠有效或永遠無效，而是視效率為一個動態的概念——市場效率取決於參與者的組成、競爭程度和環境變化。

技術分析的「生態位」可能已經消失：在早期（1990 年代以前），台灣市場以散戶為主，資訊傳播緩慢，技術分析可能確實提供了某些有價值的信號。但隨著外資持股提高至 40% 以上、程式交易普及、資訊近乎即時傳播，曾經有效的模式已被充分套利。子期間分析的結果也佐證了這一點：2017 年後市場效率更高，技術策略表現更差。

AMH 的啟示：不要用「技術分析永遠有效」或「永遠無效」的二元框架思考。而是理解：在當前市場環境下，標準技術分析策略對台灣大盤 ETF 缺乏預測力。未來若市場結構再次發生重大變化（例如一種全新的投資群體出現），某些策略可能再次短暫有效——但在效率恢復後又會失去效力。投資人不應依賴任何「一招打天下」的策略，而應隨市場環境調整。

Lo (2004)'s AMH framework provides a nuanced reading of our results. AMH does not view markets as permanently efficient or inefficient — efficiency is dynamic, depending on participant composition, competition, and environmental changes.

TA's "ecological niche" may have vanished: In earlier eras (pre-1990s), Taiwan markets were retail-dominated, information spread slowly, and TA may have provided valuable signals. But with foreign ownership above 40%, algorithmic trading prevalence, and near-instant information dissemination, previously effective patterns have been fully arbitraged. Sub-period analysis supports this: post-2017 market efficiency is higher, TA strategies perform worse.

AMH takeaway: Do not think in binary terms of "TA always works" or "never works." Understand: in the current market environment, standard TA strategies lack predictive power on Taiwan market ETFs. If market structure changes dramatically in the future (e.g., entirely new investor groups emerge), some strategies may briefly become effective again — but will lose efficacy once efficiency recovers. Investors should not rely on any "one-size-fits-all" strategy but adapt to the market environment.

4.7 公平性考量4.7 Fairness Considerations

本研究的結論有幾個重要的邊界條件，讀者在引用時應注意：

  • 僅測試標準參數：經過最佳化的參數在訓練期間可能表現更好，但這引入了過擬合風險——就像考試前拿到考古題，在那張考卷上一定能考高分，但遇到新題目就不一定了。嚴格的最佳化測試需要「樣本外」驗證，這不在本研究範圍內。
  • 僅測試指數 ETF：0050 是高度分散化的大盤指數，效率較高。技術分析在個股（尤其是資訊不對稱的中小型股）、商品、外匯等市場的表現可能不同。本研究的結論不應推廣至所有市場。
  • 僅測試純機械策略：實際交易者可能結合基本面、市場情緒、籌碼面等其他因素做綜合判斷。技術指標作為「工具箱中的一項工具」可能有輔助價值，但作為「唯一的交易規則」則無效。
  • 歷史不等於未來：過去 21 年（2004-2025）涵蓋了一個主要的長期多頭趨勢。在持續盤整或空頭主導的市場中，某些技術策略（如趨勢跟隨的 MA 交叉）的相對表現可能不同。
  • 空倉假設：空倉期間報酬假設為 0%，實務上可存入貨幣市場基金（約 1-1.5%），但這只會讓技術策略的報酬增加 0.3-0.7%，遠不足以改變結論。
  • 二元持倉限制：本研究使用全倉買入/全倉賣出的簡單模型。更精細的部位管理（如根據訊號強度調整持倉比例）可能改善結果，但也增加了自由度和過擬合風險。
  • Only standard parameters tested: Optimized parameters may perform better in-sample, but introduce overfitting — like getting exam answers beforehand: great on that test, not on new ones. Proper optimization requires out-of-sample validation, beyond this study's scope.
  • Only index ETF tested: 0050 is highly diversified and efficient. TA may work differently on individual stocks (especially small caps with information asymmetry), commodities, or forex.
  • Only pure mechanical strategies: Actual traders combine fundamentals, sentiment, and positioning. TA as "one tool in the toolbox" may have auxiliary value, but as "the only trading rule" it is ineffective.
  • Past is not future: The 2004-2025 period covers a predominantly bullish trend. In range-bound or bearish markets, some strategies (e.g., trend-following MA) may perform relatively differently.
  • Cash assumption: 0% during cash periods; money market funds would yield ~1-1.5%, adding only 0.3-0.7% — not nearly enough to change conclusions.
  • Binary position limitation: All-in/all-out simplification. More nuanced position sizing (e.g., based on signal strength) might improve results but also increases degrees of freedom and overfitting risk.

4.8 投資啟示4.8 Investment Implications

對保守型投資人：如果你的投資標的是 0050 這類大盤 ETF，最簡單有效的策略就是「買進持有」。不需要研究均線、KD、MACD，不需要每天看盤擔心黃金交叉或死亡交叉，不需要支付頻繁交易的手續費和交易稅。省下研究技術分析的時間，可以拿來陪家人、學習新技能、或做更有生產力的事——你的報酬不會因此減少，反而可能更高。

對技術分析愛好者：本研究不否定技術分析在所有市場上的價值。但數據清楚顯示，在台灣大盤 ETF 上，標準技術分析策略無法產生可靠的超額報酬。如果你仍想使用技術分析，建議：(1) 結合基本面和籌碼面做綜合判斷，而非純粹依賴技術指標；(2) 嚴格控制交易頻率，避免被成本侵蝕；(3) 將技術分析視為「輔助參考」而非「決策依據」。

對積極型投資人：如果你追求超越市場的報酬，技術分析可能不是答案——至少在標準教科書形式上不是。你可能需要更創新的方法（如機器學習、另類數據），或者接受一個殘酷的現實：在高度效率的市場中，持續打敗市場是極其困難的。多數對沖基金的長期表現也未能超越被動指數投資。

For conservative investors: If your target is a market ETF like 0050, the simplest and most effective strategy is buy-and-hold. No need to study MA, KD, or MACD, no need to watch the market daily, no frequent trading costs. Time saved from TA study can be spent with family or learning new skills — your returns will not suffer, and may even improve.

For TA enthusiasts: This study does not deny TA's value everywhere, but data shows standard TA cannot produce reliable excess returns on Taiwan market ETFs. If you still want to use TA: (1) combine with fundamentals and positioning for comprehensive judgment; (2) strictly control trading frequency to avoid cost erosion; (3) treat TA as "supplementary reference" not "decision basis."

For aggressive investors: If seeking above-market returns, TA may not be the answer — at least not in standard textbook form. You may need more innovative methods (ML, alternative data), or accept a harsh reality: consistently beating an efficient market is extremely difficult. Most hedge funds also fail to outperform passive indexing long-term.
