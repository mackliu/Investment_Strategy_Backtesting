緒論Introduction

1.1 研究背景1.1 Background

「均線黃金交叉就買、死亡交叉就賣」「KD 值低於 20 就是超賣訊號」「MACD 柱狀體由負轉正是買點」——這些技術分析規則是台灣投資社群中最廣泛流傳的交易策略。無數的財經 YouTube 頻道和投資課程以此作為核心教學內容，暗示投資人只要遵循這些規則就能穩定獲利。

然而，技術分析的有效性在學術界一直備受爭議。效率市場假說（EMH）的弱式效率主張歷史價格已反映所有資訊，基於價格模式的交易策略不應產生超額報酬。儘管行為金融學指出動量效應和均值回歸可能使某些策略短期有效，但長期而言，扣除交易成本後是否仍有效，是一個需要數據回答的問題。

本研究的目的不是要全面否定技術分析的所有可能用途，而是針對最常見的「標準教科書」版本進行科學驗證。我們希望幫助投資人建立獨立思考的能力，用數據判斷投資論點的可靠性。

"Buy when the golden cross appears, sell at the death cross." "KD below 20 means oversold—time to buy." "MACD histogram turning positive is a buy signal." These technical analysis rules are among the most widely circulated trading strategies in Taiwan's investment community.

However, the effectiveness of technical analysis has long been debated in academia. The weak-form Efficient Market Hypothesis (EMH) posits that historical prices already reflect all information, and price-pattern-based strategies should not generate excess returns. While behavioral finance suggests momentum and mean reversion may make some strategies temporarily effective, whether they remain profitable after transaction costs is an empirical question.

This study does not aim to completely dismiss all possible uses of technical analysis, but rather to scientifically test the most common "textbook" versions. We hope to help investors develop independent thinking abilities and evaluate investment claims with data.

1.2 文獻回顧1.2 Literature Review

Fama (1970) 將市場效率分為三種形式：弱式（歷史價格）、半強式（所有公開資訊）、強式（含內線資訊）。弱式效率意味著技術分析無法產生超額報酬，因為所有歷史價格資訊已被充分反映在現價中。用生活化的比喻來說：如果地上有一張千元鈔票，在一個「效率」的地方，它會立刻被撿走——你不可能靠「觀察地面模式」來預測下一張鈔票會出現在哪裡。

Brock, Lakonishok & LeBaron (1992) 的經典研究是技術分析支持者最常引用的論文之一。他們發現 1897-1986 年間，美國道瓊指數上的 MA 交叉和交易量突破策略確實有統計顯著的預測力。然而，Sullivan, Timmermann & White (1999) 指出這些結果可能受到「數據窺探」（data snooping）的影響——當你同時測試上百種策略，總有幾個「恰好」表現好，但這不代表它們真的有效。使用 White Reality Check 校正後，多數策略的顯著性消失了。這就像射了 100 箭之後才畫靶心——當然能「命中紅心」，但這不代表你射得準。

Lo, Mamaysky & Wang (2000) 使用非參數核迴歸方法重新檢驗技術形態（頭肩頂、雙底等），發現某些形態確實包含增量資訊，但經濟上的實際價值（扣除成本後）非常有限。

Park & Irwin (2007) 的綜述研究彙整了 95 篇技術分析實證文獻，發現約 56% 的研究結論為正面，但其中多數存在數據窺探、交易成本忽略、或存活偏差等方法論問題。整體而言，「乾淨」的研究（proper controls）較少支持技術分析的有效性。

台灣市場：早期研究（如 Bessembinder & Chan 1998）發現亞洲新興市場效率較低，技術分析可能有殘餘效果。但近年隨著電子交易普及、外資佔比提高（台股外資持股超過 40%）和資訊透明度提升，台灣市場效率已大幅改善。Hsu, Taylor & Wang (2016) 的跨市場研究也發現，多數技術策略在已開發市場上扣除成本後已無顯著超額報酬。

Fama (1970) classified market efficiency into three forms: weak (historical prices), semi-strong (all public info), strong (including insider info). Weak-form efficiency implies technical analysis cannot produce excess returns. Analogy: if there is a $1,000 bill on the ground in an "efficient" place, it gets picked up immediately — you cannot predict where the next one will appear by "observing ground patterns."

Brock, Lakonishok & LeBaron (1992), the classic study most cited by TA proponents, found statistically significant MA and trading-range-break strategies on the DJIA (1897-1986). However, Sullivan, Timmermann & White (1999) showed these results may be data snooping artifacts — when testing hundreds of strategies, some "happen" to perform well by chance. After White Reality Check correction, most lose significance. Like shooting 100 arrows then drawing the bullseye — of course you "hit" it, but it does not mean you are a good archer.

Lo, Mamaysky & Wang (2000) used non-parametric kernel regression to re-examine technical patterns (head-and-shoulders, double bottoms, etc.), finding some contain incremental information but with very limited economic value after costs.

Park & Irwin (2007) surveyed 95 empirical TA studies: ~56% found positive results, but most had data snooping, transaction cost omission, or survivorship bias issues. Studies with proper controls are less supportive of TA effectiveness.

Taiwan market: Early research (Bessembinder & Chan 1998) found Asian emerging markets less efficient. But with electronic trading, rising foreign ownership (>40%), and improved transparency, Taiwan market efficiency has significantly improved. Hsu, Taylor & Wang (2016) found most TA strategies lack significant after-cost excess returns in developed markets.

1.3 研究假說1.3 Research Hypotheses

H0（虛無假說）：技術分析策略的報酬不優於 Buy & Hold（超額報酬 ≤ 0）

H1（對立假說）：至少有一個技術分析策略的報酬顯著優於 Buy & Hold

檢定方法：Permutation Test（10,000 次隨機排列）+ Holm-Bonferroni 多重比較校正

H0 (null): Technical analysis strategy returns are not superior to Buy & Hold (excess return ≤ 0)

H1 (alternative): At least one technical strategy significantly outperforms Buy & Hold

Test method: Permutation Test (10,000 permutations) + Holm-Bonferroni multiple comparison correction

假說邏輯：如果技術分析真的有效，那麼至少應該有一個策略的報酬顯著高於「什麼都不做」的 Buy & Hold。我們刻意將門檻設得很低——不要求所有策略都有效，只要有「一個」就好。如果連這個最低標準都達不到，就更難主張技術分析對 0050 有預測力。使用 Holm-Bonferroni 校正的原因是：當你同時測試 8 個策略時，純粹靠運氣也有約 33% 的機率至少有一個「看起來顯著」。多重比較校正就是為了避免這種假陽性。

Hypothesis logic: If TA truly works, at least one strategy should significantly outperform "doing nothing" (Buy & Hold). We deliberately set a low bar — not requiring all strategies to work, just one. If even this minimum standard is unmet, it is harder to claim TA has predictive power on 0050. Holm-Bonferroni correction is needed because testing 8 strategies simultaneously gives ~33% chance of at least one appearing significant by pure luck.

1.4 適應性市場假說的觀點1.4 The Adaptive Markets Hypothesis Perspective

Lo (2004) 提出了「適應性市場假說」（Adaptive Markets Hypothesis, AMH），試圖調和 EMH 和行為金融之間的矛盾。AMH 主張市場效率不是恆定的，而是隨時間波動——在某些時期、某些市場中，技術分析可能有效；但當越多人使用相同策略，套利機會就會消失。

這個觀點對本研究有重要啟示：即使我們發現技術分析在 2004-2025 年間對 0050 無效，也不能排除它在 (1) 更早期的、效率較低的台灣市場中可能有效，或 (2) 在某些特定的高波動時期有短暫的有效性。AMH 暗示投資人應持續測試和調整策略，而非盲目信奉任何「永遠有效」或「永遠無效」的結論。本研究的結果反映的是目前市場環境下的實證發現，而非普遍定律。

Lo (2004) proposed the Adaptive Markets Hypothesis (AMH), attempting to reconcile EMH and behavioral finance. AMH argues market efficiency fluctuates over time — in certain periods and markets, technical analysis may work; but as more participants use the same strategies, arbitrage opportunities disappear.

This has important implications for our study: even if we find TA ineffective on 0050 during 2004-2025, we cannot rule out that it (1) may have worked in earlier, less efficient Taiwan markets, or (2) has brief periods of effectiveness during high-volatility regimes. AMH suggests investors should continuously test and adapt strategies, rather than blindly adhering to any 'always works' or 'never works' conclusion. Our results reflect empirical findings under current market conditions, not universal laws.

1.5 研究貢獻1.5 Research Contribution

本研究的主要貢獻在於：(1) 以台灣數據驗證國際文獻發現——多數技術分析研究以美國數據為主，台灣市場的實證相對匱乏，本研究以 0050 為標的填補此空白；(2) 嚴謹的統計檢定——使用 Permutation Test + 多重比較校正，而非僅比較報酬高低。多數網路上的「技術分析回測」僅呈現報酬曲線，缺乏統計檢定，無法區分「真正有效」和「純屬巧合」；(3) 完整的成本考量——同時呈現含/不含成本的結果，讓讀者了解「理論上可能有效」和「實際上可獲利」之間的差距。許多策略回測忽略交易成本，導致過度樂觀的結論；(4) 子期間穩健性分析——確認結論不是某個特定時期的偶然現象；(5) 親民的表達方式——以生活化比喻解釋統計概念，讓非專業讀者也能理解，促進金融素養。

Key contributions: (1) Taiwan-data validation of international findings — most TA studies use US data; this study fills the gap with 0050 as the target; (2) Rigorous statistics — Permutation Test + multiple comparison correction. Most online "TA backtests" only show return curves without statistical tests, unable to distinguish "truly effective" from "pure coincidence"; (3) Complete cost accounting — showing both with/without cost results. Many backtests ignore costs, leading to overoptimistic conclusions; (4) Sub-period robustness — confirming conclusions are not artifacts of a specific period; (5) Accessible communication — everyday analogies for statistical concepts, promoting financial literacy.
