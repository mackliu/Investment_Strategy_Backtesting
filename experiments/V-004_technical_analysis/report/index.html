V-004：技術分析能預測股價？V-004: Can Technical Analysis Predict Stock Prices?

研究問題：技術分析（均線、KD、MACD 等指標）能否預測股價走勢？依據技術訊號操作，能否打敗單純的買進持有策略？

Research Question: Can technical analysis indicators (moving averages, KD, MACD, etc.) predict stock price movements? Can trading based on technical signals beat a simple buy-and-hold strategy?

摘要Abstract

「均線黃金交叉就買」「KD 低於 20 超賣」「MACD 翻正就是買點」——這些技術分析口訣是台灣投資社群中最耳熟能詳的交易規則。無數的財經 YouTube 頻道和投資課程以此作為核心教學內容，讓許多投資新手誤以為只要學會看技術指標，就能穩定獲利。但事實是否真是如此？讓數據來回答。

本研究以 0050（元大台灣50 ETF）為標的，測試 8 個常見技術分析策略（MA 交叉 x 4、KD、MACD、RSI、布林通道）在 2004-2025 年間（約 21 年）的績效表現。

核心發現：所有 8 個策略均無法打敗買進持有。Buy & Hold 年化報酬 12.20%，最佳策略 MA 10 50 僅 8.14%（不含成本），最差策略 KD 9 3 3 僅 1.72%。Permutation Test 顯示 0/8 個策略在多重比較校正後達到統計顯著（即無一顯著）。

計入交易成本後，所有策略的績效進一步惡化。交易頻率最高的策略（如 MA(5,20) 年均 16 次交易）受成本拖累最嚴重。本研究的結論是：對於 0050 這類市場指數 ETF，技術分析策略不具有統計上可靠的預測力。用白話來說：與其花時間看線圖、等黃金交叉，不如直接買進持有——省時、省力、還賺更多。

'Buy when golden cross appears.' 'KD below 20 means oversold.' 'MACD turning positive is a buy signal.' — These are among the most popular trading rules in Taiwan's investment community. Countless YouTube channels and courses teach them as core content. But do they actually work? Let the data answer.

This study tests 8 common technical analysis strategies (4 MA crossovers, KD, MACD, RSI, Bollinger Bands) on 0050 (Yuanta Taiwan 50 ETF) over 2004-2025 (~21 years).

Key finding: All 8 strategies fail to beat Buy & Hold. B&H annualized return is 12.20%, best strategy MA 10 50 achieves only 8.14% (before costs), worst KD 9 3 3 only 1.72%. Permutation tests show 0/8 strategies are significant after multiple comparison correction (i.e., none).

After transaction costs, all strategies deteriorate further. High-frequency strategies (e.g., MA(5,20) averaging 16 trades/year) suffer the most. The conclusion: for market-index ETFs like 0050, technical analysis strategies lack statistically reliable predictive power. In plain language: rather than spending time on chart patterns and waiting for golden crosses, just buy and hold — saves time, effort, and earns more.

上圖呈現所有策略與買進持有的累積報酬走勢。深色粗線為 Buy & Hold，所有技術分析策略均落後於買進持有。技術分析策略因頻繁空倉（等待訊號期間持有現金）而錯失了市場的長期上漲趨勢。這就像一個總是在等「完美時機」才出門的人，反而因為猶豫不決而錯過了最多的好天氣。

The chart shows cumulative returns of all strategies vs Buy & Hold. The dark thick line is B&H; all technical strategies lag behind. These strategies miss long-term market gains due to frequent cash positions (holding cash while waiting for signals). Like someone always waiting for the 'perfect moment' to go outside, missing the most good weather due to indecision.

五大關鍵發現Five Key Findings

  1. 0/8 達到統計顯著：Permutation Test + Holm-Bonferroni 多重比較校正後，所有策略的調整後 p 值 = 1.000。原始 p 值範圍 0.1478 ~ 0.7912，無一低於 0.05 門檻。技術指標的訊號品質在統計上與擲銅板無異。
  2. 空倉是報酬殺手：持倉比例從 31%（KD）到 61%（MA 10 50）不等。在年化 12.2% 的市場中，空倉一半時間理論上只能獲得約一半的報酬。實際數據吻合此推算，證明技術擇時未帶來額外價值。
  3. 交易成本雪上加霜：含成本後，最佳策略報酬從 8.14% 降至 6.72%。高頻交易的 MA(5,20) 每年約產生 15 次訊號，年化成本拖累約 3.3 個百分點。
  4. MA(10,50) 相對最佳但仍不足：持倉比 61%、年化 8.14%，是 8 策略中最接近 B&H 者——但仍落後 4.06 個百分點，且統計不顯著。
  5. 結論跨時期穩健：無論等分（2004-2013 / 2014-2025）或結構斷點切分（2004-2016 / 2017-2025），所有策略在每個子期間均無法打敗 B&H。「最佳」策略在不同時期不一致，排除了特定期間偏差。
  1. 0/8 statistically significant: After Permutation Test + Holm-Bonferroni correction, all adjusted p-values = 1.000. Raw p-values range 0.1478 ~ 0.7912, none below 0.05. TA signal quality is statistically indistinguishable from coin flips.
  2. Cash drag kills returns: Holding ratios range from 31% (KD) to 61% (MA 10 50). In a 12.2% annualized market, being in cash half the time theoretically captures only half the return. Actual data matches this estimate, proving TA timing adds no extra value.
  3. Transaction costs add insult to injury: After costs, best strategy drops from 8.14% to 6.72%. MA(5,20) generates ~15 signals/year, costing ~3.3 pp annually.
  4. MA(10,50) relatively best but still insufficient: 61% holding ratio, 8.14% annualized — closest to B&H among 8 strategies but still trails by 4.06 pp, and statistically insignificant.
  5. Robust across periods: Whether split equally (2004-2013/2014-2025) or by structural break (2004-2016/2017-2025), all strategies fail to beat B&H in every sub-period. The 'best' strategy varies between periods, ruling out period-specific artifacts.
