V-004：技術分析能預測股價？V-004: Can Technical Analysis Predict Stock Prices?

研究問題：技術分析（均線、KD、MACD 等指標）能否預測股價走勢？依據技術訊號操作，能否打敗單純的買進持有策略？

Research Question: Can technical analysis indicators (moving averages, KD, MACD, etc.) predict stock price movements? Can trading based on technical signals beat a simple buy-and-hold strategy?

摘要Abstract

本研究以 0050（元大台灣50 ETF）為標的，測試 8 個常見技術分析策略（MA 交叉 × 4、KD、MACD、RSI、布林通道）在 2004-2025 年間（約 21 年）的績效表現。

核心發現：所有 8 個策略均無法打敗買進持有。Buy & Hold 年化報酬 12.20%，最佳策略 MA 10 50 僅 8.14%（不含成本），最差策略 KD 9 3 3 僅 1.72%。Permutation Test 顯示 0/8 個策略在多重比較校正後達到統計顯著（即無一顯著）。

計入交易成本後，所有策略的績效進一步惡化。交易頻率最高的策略（如 MA(5,20) 年均 16 次交易）受成本拖累最嚴重。本研究的結論是：對於 0050 這類市場指數 ETF，技術分析策略不具有統計上可靠的預測力。

This study tests 8 common technical analysis strategies (4 MA crossovers, KD, MACD, RSI, Bollinger Bands) on 0050 (Yuanta Taiwan 50 ETF) over 2004-2025 (~21 years).

Key finding: All 8 strategies fail to beat Buy & Hold. B&H annualized return is 12.20%, best strategy MA 10 50 achieves only 8.14% (before costs), worst KD 9 3 3 only 1.72%. Permutation tests show 0/8 strategies are significant after multiple comparison correction (i.e., none).

After transaction costs, all strategies deteriorate further. High-frequency strategies (e.g., MA(5,20) averaging 16 trades/year) suffer the most. The conclusion: for market-index ETFs like 0050, technical analysis strategies lack statistically reliable predictive power.
