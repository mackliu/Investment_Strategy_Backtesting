V-002：定期定額必勝論驗證V-002: Does Dollar-Cost Averaging Always Win?

研究問題：每月定期定額投資台灣 ETF（0050/0056），長期下來是否一定賺錢？與銀行定存相比，績效差異有多大？0050 與 0056 哪個更適合定期定額？

Research Question: Does monthly dollar-cost averaging (DCA) into Taiwan ETFs (0050/0056) always yield positive returns over the long term? How does it compare to bank deposits? Which ETF is more suitable for DCA?

摘要Abstract

本研究以每月定額 10,000 元投入 0050 或 0056 的定期定額策略，使用 2003-2025 年的完整歷史數據進行滾動回測。共計模擬 2,384 組不同起始月份與投資期間的組合，以 XIRR（擴展內部報酬率）計算年化報酬，並與同期銀行一年期定儲機動利率做嚴格配對比較。

核心發現：

  • 長期 DCA 幾近「必勝」：0050 持有 7 年以上的正報酬率達 100%，平均年化 XIRR 約 10.8%。0056 同樣在 7 年以上達到 100% 勝率。
  • DCA 大幅優於定存：所有投資期間的 DCA-定存差值均為統計顯著（Holm-Bonferroni 校正後 p < 0.001），平均超額報酬達 8-11 個百分點。
  • 短期仍有風險：持有 1 年的最差 XIRR 為 -61.9%，勝率僅 76%——約有 24% 的機率虧損。
  • 0050 長期優於 0056：在 3 年以上的 DCA 情境下，0050 平均超額報酬約 3 個百分點，統計顯著。

結論：「定期定額一定賺」的論述在長期（7 年以上）大致成立，但「一定」二字過於絕對。更精確的表述是：「歷史上，長期定期定額台灣 ETF 有非常高的獲利機率，且顯著優於定存。」

This study backtests a monthly DCA strategy investing 10,000 TWD into 0050 or 0056, using complete historical data from 2003-2025. A total of 2,384 scenario combinations (varying start month and investment period) were simulated. Returns are annualized via XIRR (Extended Internal Rate of Return) and compared against the prevailing 1-year time savings floating rate.

Key Findings:

  • Long-term DCA is near-certain to profit: 0050 achieves 100% win rate at 7+ years, mean XIRR ~10.8%. 0056 also reaches 100% at 7+ years.
  • DCA vastly outperforms deposits: All DCA-deposit differences are statistically significant (Holm-Bonferroni adjusted p < 0.001), with 8-11 pp average excess returns.
  • Short-term risk exists: 1Y worst-case XIRR is -61.9%, win rate only 76% — about 24% chance of loss.
  • 0050 beats 0056 long-term: For 3Y+ DCA, 0050 averages ~3 pp higher XIRR, statistically significant.

Conclusion: The claim 'DCA always profits' is largely supported at 7+ year horizons, but the word 'always' overstates. A more accurate statement: 'Historically, long-term DCA into Taiwan ETFs has had a very high probability of profit and significantly outperforms deposits.'

研究方法概要Methodology Overview

本研究採用滾動窗口回測法，對每一個可能的起始月份進行 DCA 模擬，投資期間涵蓋 1、2、3、5、7、10、15 年。統計推論使用 Newey-West HAC t 檢定（處理重疊樣本的自相關）、Cohen's d 效果量、Block Bootstrap 信賴區間，並以 Holm-Bonferroni 方法校正多重比較。穩健性分析包含不同交易成本情境與不含息報酬。

This study employs a rolling-window backtest, simulating DCA for every possible starting month across investment periods of 1, 2, 3, 5, 7, 10, and 15 years. Statistical inference uses Newey-West HAC t-tests (for overlapping sample autocorrelation), Cohen's d effect sizes, Block Bootstrap confidence intervals, and Holm-Bonferroni correction for multiple comparisons. Robustness checks include alternative cost scenarios and price-only returns.
