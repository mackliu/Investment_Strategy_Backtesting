V-002：定期定額必勝論驗證V-002: Does Dollar-Cost Averaging Always Win?

1. 緒論1. Introduction

1.1 研究背景1.1 Background

「定期定額」（Dollar-Cost Averaging, DCA）是台灣投資社群中最常被推薦給小資族的投資方式。許多理財頻道和投資社群經常強調：只要每月固定投入 ETF，長期下來一定賺錢。這個論述的吸引力在於其簡單性——不需要擇時進場、不需要大筆資金、只需要紀律和時間。在各大投資論壇（PTT Stock 板、Dcard 理財板）和 YouTube 理財頻道上，「定期定額 0050/0056」幾乎已成為標準建議。

然而，這個廣為流傳的論述存在幾個隱憂。首先，「一定賺」三個字暗示了零風險的保證，這在金融市場上從來不存在。其次，常見的舉例都是特定時間段的回報——通常是從 2008 年金融海嘯後的低點開始計算，刻意放大了報酬率。第三，定期定額 ETF 的機會成本（相對於銀行定存的「安全」選擇）很少被量化討論。

本研究以科學的態度回應這些問題：不預設結論，以完整歷史數據涵蓋所有可能的起始時點，採用嚴謹的統計方法量化 DCA 的績效、風險、以及相對於定存的超額報酬。這不是為了「打臉」任何人，而是為了讓投資人在充分了解風險的情況下做出更好的決策。

Dollar-cost averaging (DCA) is the most commonly recommended investment approach for young salary earners in Taiwan's investing community. Financial influencers and advisors frequently emphasize: just invest a fixed amount monthly into ETFs, and you will always profit in the long run. The appeal lies in its simplicity — no market timing needed, no large lump sum required, just discipline and time. On major investment forums (PTT Stock, Dcard Finance) and YouTube finance channels, 'DCA into 0050/0056' has become near-standard advice.

However, this widely circulated narrative has several concerns. First, 'always profit' implies a zero-risk guarantee, which never exists in financial markets. Second, most influencer examples cherry-pick specific time periods — typically starting from post-2008 crisis lows, inflating return figures. Third, the opportunity cost of DCA ETFs (relative to the 'safe' alternative of bank deposits) is rarely quantified.

This study addresses these concerns scientifically: without presupposing conclusions, using complete historical data covering all possible starting points, and employing rigorous statistical methods to quantify DCA performance, risk, and excess returns over deposits. The goal is not to 'debunk' anyone, but to enable investors to make better decisions with full awareness of the risks.

1.2 文獻回顧1.2 Literature Review

學術界對 DCA 的研究由來已久。Constantinides (1979) 從理論上指出，在效率市場假說下，DCA 並非最優策略——一次性投入（lump-sum）的期望報酬通常高於 DCA。Rozeff (1994) 和 Brennan, Li & Torous (2005) 的實證研究也大致支持這個結論：在美國市場中，約 65-70% 的情境下一次性投入優於 DCA。

然而，這些研究存在一個關鍵前提：投資人「已經擁有一筆資金」。對於每月領薪水、沒有初始本金的投資人而言，「有錢就投入」本身就是一種 DCA——他們沒有一次性投入的選擇。Hayley (2012) 將這種情境稱為「被動 DCA」（involuntary DCA），並指出在此情境下，DCA 的關鍵問題不是「是否優於一次性投入」，而是「是否值得承擔股市風險，而非放在銀行定存」。

本研究正是從這個角度出發：一位月薪族每月可投入 10,000 元，應該選擇 DCA 投入 ETF 還是放銀行定存？台灣本土的 DCA 研究相對稀少，且多以特定回測期間為主，缺乏滾動窗口分析和嚴格的統計推論。

Academic research on DCA has a long history. Constantinides (1979) theoretically showed that under efficient market hypothesis, DCA is not optimal — lump-sum investing typically yields higher expected returns. Rozeff (1994) and Brennan, Li & Torous (2005) empirically confirmed this: in US markets, lump-sum beats DCA about 65-70% of the time.

However, these studies assume the investor 'already has a lump sum.' For salaried workers receiving monthly pay with no initial capital, 'investing as money arrives' is inherently a form of DCA — they have no lump-sum option. Hayley (2012) termed this 'involuntary DCA' and argued the relevant question is not 'DCA vs lump-sum,' but 'whether the stock market risk is worth taking compared to bank deposits.'

This study adopts precisely this perspective: a salaried worker with 10,000 TWD monthly — should they DCA into ETFs or deposit in a bank? Taiwan-specific DCA studies are relatively scarce and mostly use fixed backtest periods, lacking rolling-window analysis and rigorous statistical inference.

1.3 研究假說1.3 Hypotheses

本研究建立三層階層式假說檢定，從最基本的問題逐步深入：

  • Q1 — 絕對績效：DCA 投資 0050/0056 的年化報酬（XIRR）是否顯著大於零？這回答了最根本的問題：「定期定額到底有沒有賺錢？」
  • Q2 — 相對績效：DCA ETF 的報酬是否顯著優於同期銀行定存？這回答了機會成本的問題：「承擔股市風險是否值得？」
  • Q3 — 標的比較：0050（市值型）與 0056（高股息型）在 DCA 情境下的報酬是否有顯著差異？這回答了標的選擇的問題：「該買 0050 還是 0056？」

三層設計的邏輯是：如果 Q1 不成立（DCA 連正報酬都無法達到），則 Q2 和 Q3 的討論便無意義。同理，如果 Q2 不成立（DCA 沒有比定存好），則討論 0050 vs 0056 的意義也有限。這種階層式設計確保了研究結論的邏輯一致性。

This study establishes a three-layer hierarchical hypothesis testing framework, progressing from basic to deeper questions:

  • Q1 — Absolute Performance: Is the XIRR of DCA into 0050/0056 significantly greater than zero? This answers the most fundamental question: 'Does DCA actually make money?'
  • Q2 — Relative Performance: Does DCA ETF return significantly exceed concurrent bank deposit returns? This addresses opportunity cost: 'Is the stock market risk worth taking?'
  • Q3 — Asset Comparison: Is there a significant difference between 0050 (market-cap) and 0056 (high-dividend) DCA returns? This guides asset selection: 'Should I buy 0050 or 0056?'

The three-layer logic: if Q1 fails (DCA cannot even achieve positive returns), then Q2 and Q3 become moot. Similarly, if Q2 fails (DCA is not better than deposits), then the 0050-vs-0056 comparison has limited practical relevance. This hierarchical design ensures logical consistency in research conclusions.

1.4 研究貢獻1.4 Research Contribution

本研究相較於既有文獻和網路上的回測分析，有以下差異：

  • 完整覆蓋：使用 0050 自 2003 年上市以來的所有可用數據，不挑選特定起始時點。
  • 滾動窗口：計算每一個可能的起始月份，而非僅計算一條從頭到尾的績效曲線。
  • 嚴格對照組：以同期實際銀行定儲利率（非固定利率假設）作為比較基準。
  • 統計推論：使用 Newey-West HAC 處理重疊樣本問題，報告效果量和信賴區間——不只看 p 值。
  • 多重比較校正：使用 Holm-Bonferroni 方法控制族系錯誤率。
  • 穩健性分析：測試不同交易成本和不含息報酬對結論的影響。

Compared to existing literature and online backtest analyses, this study offers:

  • Complete coverage: Uses all available data since 0050's inception in 2003, without cherry-picking start dates.
  • Rolling window: Computes every possible starting month, not just a single start-to-end performance curve.
  • Rigorous benchmark: Uses contemporaneous actual bank deposit rates (not fixed-rate assumptions) as comparison.
  • Statistical inference: Employs Newey-West HAC for overlapping samples, reports effect sizes and CIs — not just p-values.
  • Multiple comparison correction: Holm-Bonferroni to control family-wise error rate.
  • Robustness checks: Tests alternative cost scenarios and price-only returns.
