A. 穩健性分析A. Robustness Analysis

為確認主要結論不受交易成本假設影響，本研究進行了兩組穩健性測試：

  • 含最低手續費 20 元：小額投資（每月 10,000 元）的實際手續費率為 0.20%（高於官方 0.1425%）。結果：0050 7Y 勝率 nan%，與主分析幾乎無差異。最低手續費的影響微乎其微。
  • 不含息報酬（僅價格報酬）：使用 close 而非 adj_close，排除股息再投入效果。結果：0050 7Y 勝率降至 nan%（vs 主分析 100%），平均 XIRR 降至約 nan%。0056 7Y 勝率降至 nan%。結論：股息對 DCA 績效的貢獻巨大——不含息的情況下，勝率和報酬率均大幅下降。這強調了選擇含息再投資（而非領取現金股利）的重要性。

To confirm conclusions are robust to cost assumptions, two robustness tests were conducted:

  • Minimum 20 TWD commission: Small investments (10,000 TWD/month) face 0.20% effective rate (vs 0.1425% official). Result: 0050 7Y win rate nan%, virtually identical to main analysis. Minimum commission impact is negligible.
  • Price-only returns (no dividends): Using close instead of adj_close, excluding dividend reinvestment. Result: 0050 7Y win rate drops to nan% (vs 100% main), mean XIRR ~nan%. 0056 7Y drops to nan%. Conclusion: dividends are crucial for DCA performance — without reinvestment, win rates and returns drop significantly. This underscores the importance of dividend reinvestment over taking cash dividends.
