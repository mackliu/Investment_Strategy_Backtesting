2. 研究方法2. Methodology

2.1 數據來源與品質2.1 Data Sources and Quality

本研究使用以下數據：

  • 0050 日線數據：2003-06-30 ~ 2025-11-25（5,517 交易日，270 個月）。含 adj_close（除權息調整收盤價），完整反映配息再投入的效果。
  • 0056 日線數據：2007-12-26 ~ 2025-11-25（4,403 交易日，216 個月）。自上市日起完整收錄。
  • 台灣銀行定存利率：2001-01 ~ 2026-01（301 個月），使用「一年期定期儲蓄存款機動利率」作為主要基準。此利率最貼近一般民眾定存的實際收益。利率範圍 0.84% ~ 5.05%，反映了台灣長期低利率環境的真實狀況。
  • 台灣 CPI：1981-01 ~ 2026-01（541 個月），用於通膨調整參考。

數據品質驗證：0050 和 0056 的 adj_close 資料已與 FinMind、Yahoo Finance 交叉比對。0050 首日 adj_close 異常值（等於 close 而非調整值）已修正。定存利率資料來自台灣央行公開統計，經手動驗證無缺漏。

This study uses the following data:

  • 0050 daily prices: 2003-06-30 to 2025-11-25 (5,517 trading days, 270 months). Includes adj_close (dividend-adjusted close), reflecting reinvested dividends.
  • 0056 daily prices: 2007-12-26 to 2025-11-25 (4,403 trading days, 216 months). Complete since inception.
  • Bank deposit rates: 2001-01 to 2026-01 (301 months), using the '1-year time savings floating rate' as primary benchmark. This rate best represents actual returns for retail depositors. Range: 0.84% to 5.05%, reflecting Taiwan's persistently low-rate environment.
  • Taiwan CPI: 1981-01 to 2026-01 (541 months), for inflation adjustment reference.

Data quality: 0050 and 0056 adj_close data were cross-validated against FinMind and Yahoo Finance. A first-day adj_close anomaly in 0050 (equaling unadjusted close) was corrected. Deposit rate data from Taiwan's central bank statistics were manually verified for completeness.

2.2 DCA 模擬設計2.2 DCA Simulation Design

情境設定：一位月薪族每月提撥 10,000 元，面臨三個選擇：（1）定期定額買 0050、（2）定期定額買 0056、（3）存入銀行定儲。整個投資期間不提領任何資金——ETF 股息透過 adj_close 自動反映再投入效果，定存利息按月複利累積。

模擬流程：

  1. 選定起始月：以每月第一個交易日為扣款日，涵蓋所有可能的起始月份（0050: 270 個月，0056: 205 + 11 個月）
  2. 每月投入：以當日 adj_close 計算可買入股數（允許小數股），扣除買入手續費 0.1425%
  3. 持有 N 年：投資期間 = 1, 2, 3, 5, 7, 10, 15 年（= N x 12 個月的連續扣款）
  4. 結算：最後一個投入月的最後交易日，以 adj_close 計算總市值，扣除賣出手續費 0.1425% + ETF 交易稅 0.1%
  5. 年化報酬：以 XIRR（擴展內部報酬率）計算，精確考慮每筆現金流的時間加權

為什麼用 adj_close？adj_close 已將除權息日的價格調整反映在歷史價格中，等效於「配息自動再投入」。這比手動追蹤每次配息更為精確，且避免了配息時間差的模擬複雜度。

為什麼用 XIRR 而非幾何年化報酬？DCA 的現金流不規則（每月投入一筆、最終領回一筆），XIRR 能精確處理這種多期現金流的年化計算，是 DCA 績效衡量的標準方法。

Scenario: A salaried worker allocates 10,000 TWD monthly to one of three options: (1) DCA into 0050, (2) DCA into 0056, (3) bank time savings. No withdrawals during the investment period — ETF dividends are reflected via adj_close (reinvested), deposit interest compounds monthly.

Simulation process:

  1. Starting month: First trading day of each month as debit date, covering all possible start months
  2. Monthly investment: Calculate shares at adj_close (fractional shares allowed), deduct 0.1425% buy commission
  3. Hold N years: Investment periods = 1, 2, 3, 5, 7, 10, 15 years (= N x 12 consecutive monthly contributions)
  4. Settlement: Last trading day of final contribution month, total value at adj_close minus 0.1425% sell commission + 0.1% ETF transaction tax
  5. Annualized return: XIRR (Extended IRR), precisely accounting for time-weighted cash flows

Why adj_close? adj_close already reflects ex-dividend adjustments in historical prices, equivalent to 'automatic dividend reinvestment.' This is more precise than manually tracking each dividend and avoids simulation complexity around dividend timing.

Why XIRR over geometric annualized return? DCA has irregular cash flows (monthly contributions, one final withdrawal). XIRR precisely handles multi-period cash flow annualization and is the standard method for evaluating DCA performance.

2.3 定存模擬設計2.3 Deposit Simulation Design

定存模擬作為 DCA 的對照組，代表「最保守的替代方案」。每月同樣投入 10,000 元，以當月的一年期定儲機動利率按月複利計算。使用歷史實際利率而非固定利率假設，確保比較的公平性。在台灣 2001-2025 年期間，定存利率經歷了從 5% 以上（2001 年）降至不到 1%（2009-2020 年）再回升至約 1.7%（2025 年）的變化，這個動態過程完整反映在模擬中。

The deposit simulation serves as DCA's control group, representing the 'most conservative alternative.' Same 10,000 TWD monthly, compounded at the contemporaneous 1-year time savings floating rate. Using actual historical rates (not fixed-rate assumptions) ensures fair comparison. Taiwan's deposit rates from 2001-2025 ranged from above 5% (2001) to below 1% (2009-2020) and back to ~1.7% (2025) — this dynamic process is fully captured in the simulation.

2.4 統計方法2.4 Statistical Methods

由於本研究的滾動窗口設計，相鄰起始月的 DCA 路徑高度重疊（例如 2010 年 1 月開始的 5 年 DCA 與 2 月開始的 5 年 DCA，有 59 個月完全重疊）。這種重疊導致嚴重的序列自相關，使得傳統 t 檢定會高估顯著性。為此，本研究採用以下方法：

  • Newey-West HAC t 檢定：自動估計最佳延遲期數，提供異質性與自相關一致的標準誤。這是處理時間序列重疊樣本的黃金標準方法。
  • Cohen's d 效果量：p 值只告訴我們差異是否「存在」，效果量告訴我們差異有「多大」。d < 0.2 為小效果、0.2-0.8 為中等、0.8-1.2 為大效果、> 1.2 為極大效果。
  • 環形區塊拔靴法（10,000 次）：不依賴常態假設的穩健信賴區間。區塊大小設為投資期間的月數，保留序列相關結構。
  • Wilcoxon 符號等級檢定：非參數穩健性驗證，不依賴分佈假設。
  • Holm-Bonferroni 校正：本研究進行多組檢定（14 組 Q1 + 14 組 Q2 + 7 組 Q3），需控制族系錯誤率。Holm-Bonferroni 是 Bonferroni 的改良版，在控制錯誤率的同時保有較高統計功效。

Due to the rolling-window design, adjacent DCA paths overlap heavily (e.g., a 5Y DCA starting January 2010 shares 59 months with one starting February 2010). This overlap creates severe serial autocorrelation, causing traditional t-tests to overstate significance. We therefore employ:

  • Newey-West HAC t-test: Automatically estimates optimal lag length, providing heteroskedasticity and autocorrelation consistent standard errors. This is the gold standard for overlapping time-series samples.
  • Cohen's d effect size: P-values only tell us whether a difference 'exists'; effect sizes tell us 'how large.' d < 0.2 = small, 0.2-0.8 = medium, 0.8-1.2 = large, > 1.2 = very large.
  • Circular Block Bootstrap (10,000 reps): Robust CIs without normality assumptions. Block size equals investment period months to preserve serial dependence.
  • Wilcoxon signed-rank test: Non-parametric robustness check, distribution-free.
  • Holm-Bonferroni correction: With 14 Q1 + 14 Q2 + 7 Q3 tests, family-wise error control is essential. Holm-Bonferroni improves on Bonferroni with higher power while maintaining error control.

2.5 交易成本假設2.5 Transaction Cost Assumptions

主要分析使用「官方公告費率」：買入手續費 0.1425%、賣出手續費 0.1425%、ETF 交易稅 0.1%。多數券商提供手續費 6 折優惠（實際 0.0855%），但本研究使用全額費率以確保保守估計。穩健性分析另測試「含最低手續費 20 元」情境（小額投資人面臨的較高實際費率）。

以每月 10,000 元投入為例：買入手續費 = 10,000 x 0.1425% = 14.25 元，低於最低 20 元門檻。因此含最低手續費情境的實際費率為 20/10,000 = 0.20%，比官方費率高約 0.06 個百分點。

The primary analysis uses 'official published rates': 0.1425% buy commission, 0.1425% sell commission, 0.1% ETF transaction tax. Most brokers offer 40% discounts (effective 0.0855%), but we use full rates for conservative estimates. Robustness analysis additionally tests 'minimum 20 TWD commission' (higher effective rate for small investments).

For 10,000 TWD monthly: buy commission = 10,000 x 0.1425% = 14.25 TWD, below the 20 TWD minimum. The minimum commission scenario thus has an effective rate of 20/10,000 = 0.20%, about 0.06 pp higher.
