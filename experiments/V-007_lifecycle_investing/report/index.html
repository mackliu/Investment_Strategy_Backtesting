V-007：年輕人應該 All-in 股票？V-007: Should Young People Go All-in on Stocks?

研究問題：年輕人應該把所有資金投入股票嗎？長期 100% 股票配置真的是最佳策略嗎？還是該因應不同的人生階段調整配置？

Research Question: Should young people invest 100% in stocks? Is an all-stock allocation truly optimal, or should allocation adapt to different life stages?

摘要

本研究以美國超過 150 年的股票與債券月度實質報酬數據，模擬不同投資期間（10-40 年）與 6 種配置策略的歷史定期定額（DCA）投資生涯。6 種策略涵蓋固定比例（100% 股票、80/20、60/40、40/60）和動態策略（目標日期、反向滑降），共產生超過 56,000 條歷史投資路徑。

核心發現：100% 股票策略在 30 年 DCA 投資後中位數終值為投入成本的 3.05 倍，遠高於 60/40 的 2.56 倍。然而，100% 股票在 10 年期的虧損機率為 11.5%，30 年期最差 5% 情境僅有 1.57 倍，而 60/40 同期最差 5% 為 1.26 倍。序列風險分析顯示，投資末期遭遇崩盤的投資者終值中位數僅 2.83 倍，遠低於初期崩盤者的 5.29 倍（效果量 d = 2.06，極大）。恐慌賣出模擬更證實：在崩盤時出場的投資者終值顯著低於紀律投資者（p < 0.001）。

Abstract

Using 150+ years of US real stock and bond monthly returns, this study simulates DCA investment careers of 10-40 years across 6 allocation strategies — 4 fixed (100% stock, 80/20, 60/40, 40/60) and 2 dynamic (Target Date, Reverse Glide) — generating over 56,000 historical investment paths.

Key finding: 100% stock achieves a median terminal value of 3.05x invested capital after 30 years, vs 2.56x for 60/40. However, 100% stock has 11.5% loss probability at 10 years; the worst 5% at 30 years yields only 1.57x vs 60/40's 1.26x. Sequence risk analysis shows late-career crashes yield only 2.83x median vs 5.29x for early crashes (d = 2.06). Panic selling simulation confirms significantly lower terminal values for those who exit during drawdowns (p < 0.001).

上圖解讀：箱型圖呈現各策略在 10-40 年投資期間的終值分布。紅色虛線為損益平衡點（1.0x，即回本）。隨著投資期間延長，所有策略的中位數（箱內中線）上升，且分布逐漸遠離紅線——但 100% 股票（紅色）的箱體和鬚線最長，代表最大的不確定性。60/40（藍色）的箱體較窄，結果更為集中。

Chart interpretation: Box plots show terminal value distributions across strategies and periods. Red dashed line is the break-even point (1.0x). As horizon extends, all medians (center lines) rise and distributions move away from the red line — but 100% stock (red) has the widest boxes and whiskers, indicating greatest uncertainty. 60/40 (blue) has narrower boxes with more concentrated outcomes.

主要發現Key Findings

  1. 時間是朋友，但非保證：100% 股票的虧損機率從 10 年 11.5% → 20 年 4.4% → 30 年 0.0%。時間確實大幅降低虧損機率，但即使 30 年也未歸零。
  2. 報酬差距隨時間擴大：10 年的策略差異不大（中位數 1.46x），但 40 年後 100% 股票（4.58x）是 60/40（3.41x）的 1.3 倍。複利效應讓小差異長期放大。
  3. 序列風險效果驚人：崩盤發生在職涯末期 vs 初期，終值差異達 1.87 倍，效果量 d = 2.06（極大效果量）。這是本研究最重要的風險警示。
  4. 紀律比配置更重要：恐慌賣出模擬中，60/40 紀律投資者的終值優於 100% 股票恐慌賣出者。選擇你能堅持的配置，而非理論最優。
  5. Time helps but is no guarantee: 100% stock loss probability: 10Y 11.5% -> 20Y 4.4% -> 30Y 0.0%. Time greatly reduces loss probability, but even 30 years never reaches zero.
  6. Return gap widens with time: 10-year strategy differences are modest (median 1.46x), but at 40 years, 100% stock (4.58x) is 1.3x of 60/40 (3.41x). Compounding amplifies small differences over time.
  7. Sequence risk is dramatic: Late-career vs early-career crashes produce 1.87x difference; effect size d = 2.06 (very large). This is the study's most important risk warning.
  8. Discipline matters more than allocation: Panic selling simulation shows disciplined 60/40 investors outperform panicking 100% stock investors. Choose the allocation you can stick with, not the theoretically optimal one.
