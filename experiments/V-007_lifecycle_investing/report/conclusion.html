V-007：年輕人應該 All-in 股票？V-007: Should Young People Go All-in on Stocks?

結論Conclusion

5.1 核心結論5.1 Core Conclusions

本研究以美國 150+ 年歷史數據，模擬 10-40 年的 DCA 投資生涯，比較 6 種配置策略，並以 Block Bootstrap、Kruskal-Wallis、Wilcoxon 等非參數方法進行嚴格的統計檢定。以下是五項核心結論，每項均以具體數據與效果量佐證：

Using 150+ years of US data, simulating 10-40 year DCA careers across 6 strategies, with rigorous nonparametric tests (Block Bootstrap, Kruskal-Wallis, Wilcoxon), we present five core conclusions, each backed by concrete data and effect sizes:

結論一：100% 股票在中位數上優於所有其他策略，且效果隨投資期間放大。30 年中位數 3.05x（vs 60/40 的 2.56x），效果量從 d = 0.37（10 年）遞增至 d = 1.07（40 年），p < 0.001。含義：如果只看中位數，100% 股票是最佳選擇。

Conclusion 1: 100% stock outperforms all strategies in median, with effect amplifying over time. 30Y median 3.05x (vs 60/40 2.56x), effect size from d = 0.37 (10Y) to d = 1.07 (40Y), p < 0.001. Implication: looking only at medians, 100% stock is optimal.

結論二：但尾部風險不容忽視——100% 股票在最差情境下幾乎沒有優勢。30 年最差 5%：100% 股票 1.57x vs 60/40 1.26x，差距僅 0.31x。而 P95-P5 離散度：100% 股票 4.81x，60/40 僅 2.45x。含義：100% 股票的高報酬主要來自好運情境的額外收穫，壞運時幾乎無優勢。

Conclusion 2: Tail risk cannot be ignored — 100% stock has almost no advantage in worst cases. 30Y worst 5%: 100% stock 1.57x vs 60/40 1.26x, gap only 0.31x. P95-P5 spread: 100% stock 4.81x vs 60/40 2.45x. Implication: 100% stock's high returns come from good-luck scenarios; in bad luck, there is almost no advantage.

結論三：序列風險是 100% 股票的最大隱患，效果量極大。崩盤發生時點與終值的相關係數 r = -0.52，初期 vs 末期崩盤的效果量 d = 2.06（極大），中位數差距 5.29x vs 2.83x。含義：接近退休時仍維持 100% 股票，等於對序列風險毫無防備。這是不負責任的配置。

Conclusion 3: Sequence risk is the biggest hidden threat of 100% stock, with very large effect. Crash timing vs terminal value: r = -0.52, early vs late crash effect d = 2.06 (very large), median gap 5.29x vs 2.83x. Implication: maintaining 100% stock near retirement is unprotected against sequence risk — an irresponsible allocation.

結論四：恐慌賣出的代價可能超過配置選擇的影響。恐慌賣出效果量：100% 股票 d = 0.49，60/40 d = 0.72（p < 0.001）。60/40 紀律投資者的長期報酬，優於 100% 股票的恐慌賣出者。含義：選擇你能堅持的配置，比選擇理論最優配置更重要。

Conclusion 4: Panic selling can cost more than allocation choice. Panic effect: 100% stock d = 0.49, 60/40 d = 0.72 (p < 0.001). Disciplined 60/40 outperforms panicking 100% stock long-term. Implication: choosing an allocation you can maintain is more important than choosing the theoretical optimum.

結論五：沒有一種策略在所有情境都最佳。最佳配置取決於：(1) 投資期間長短（短期風險大）；(2) 個人風險承受度（心理因素）；(3) 人力資本特性（職業風險）；(4) 是否能維持紀律（行為因素）。含義：「年輕人應該 All-in 股票」這個通用建議，對部分人正確、對部分人危險。

Conclusion 5: No single strategy dominates all scenarios. Optimal allocation depends on: (1) investment horizon (shorter = riskier); (2) personal risk tolerance (psychological); (3) human capital characteristics (career risk); (4) ability to maintain discipline (behavioral). Implication: "young people should go all-in on stocks" is correct for some but dangerous for others.

5.3 研究限制5.3 Limitations

本研究的結論應在以下限制的脈絡下解讀：

  1. 存活偏差：使用美國數據（20 世紀表現最佳的股市之一），可能高估股票的長期預期報酬。Dimson, Marsh & Staunton (2002) 的全球數據顯示，其他國家的股票溢酬普遍低於美國。
  2. 債券報酬模型：債券報酬由 10 年期殖利率推算（存續期間 7.5 年），而非實際交易報酬。在利率快速變動時期（如 1970-80 年代），模型可能高估或低估實際債券報酬。
  3. 路徑重疊性：歷史滾動路徑高度重疊——相鄰兩條 30 年路徑共享 359 個月的數據。雖然使用了非參數方法和 Block Bootstrap 來處理，有效獨立觀察值仍遠少於路徑總數。
  4. 未考慮交易成本與稅負：年度再平衡需要交易，產生手續費和稅負。台灣的交易稅（ETF 0.1%）和手續費（0.1425%）會略微降低再平衡策略的報酬。
  5. 固定投入假設：假設每月投入固定金額，未考慮收入隨年齡增長。若加入薪資成長，會放大職涯後期投入的比重，可能改變序列風險的影響。
  6. 台灣數據不足：0050 僅有 22 年數據，無法進行 30-40 年的長期生涯模擬，在地驗證僅限於短期投資。
  7. 恐慌模擬簡化：只測試了單一閾值（-20%）和單一等待期（12 個月），現實中投資者的恐慌行為更為複雜且多樣。

Results should be interpreted within these limitations:

  1. Survivorship bias: US data (one of the 20th century's best markets) may overstate expected equity returns. Dimson, Marsh & Staunton (2002) show equity premiums are generally lower elsewhere.
  2. Bond return model: Returns estimated from 10Y yields (duration 7.5), not actual trading. During rapid rate changes (e.g., 1970-80s), the model may over- or under-estimate actual bond returns.
  3. Path overlap: Rolling paths are highly overlapping — adjacent 30Y paths share 359 of 360 months. Despite using nonparametric methods and Block Bootstrap, effective independent observations are far fewer than total paths.
  4. No transaction costs/taxes: Annual rebalancing incurs fees and taxes. Taiwan's transaction tax (ETF 0.1%) and commission (0.1425%) slightly reduce rebalancing returns.
  5. Fixed contribution assumption: Constant monthly investment ignores income growth. Adding salary growth would amplify late-career contributions, potentially altering sequence risk impact.
  6. Insufficient Taiwan data: Only 22 years of 0050 data, insufficient for 30-40Y simulations; local validation limited to short horizons.
  7. Simplified panic model: Only one threshold (-20%) and one wait period (12 months) tested. Real investor panic behavior is far more complex and varied.

5.4 未來研究方向5.4 Future Research Directions

  1. 多國數據驗證：使用 Dimson-Marsh-Staunton 全球數據庫或 MSCI 全球指數，在去除存活偏差後重新檢驗上述結論。特別是日本（失落的 30 年）和新興市場。
  2. 收入成長模型：加入薪資隨年齡增長的 DCA 模擬（例如前 20 年年增 3%，後 20 年持平），觀察是否改變最佳配置建議。收入成長會加大職涯後期投入比重，可能進一步放大序列風險。
  3. 估值調整策略：根據 CAPE 或其他估值指標動態調整股票比重（CAPE 高時降低、低時增加），觀察是否能改善風險調整報酬。這需要進一步的前視偏差檢驗。
  4. 精細行為模型：測試不同恐慌閾值（-10%, -15%, -25%, -30%）、不同等待期（3, 6, 18, 24 個月）、以及「分批退出」（非全部賣出）的行為變異。
  5. 退休後銜接：將本研究的「累積階段」結論與 V-013（提領策略）結合，建立完整的「累積 → 提領」生命週期模擬。
  1. Multi-country validation: Use Dimson-Marsh-Staunton global database or MSCI World, re-examine conclusions after removing survivorship bias. Especially Japan (lost 30 years) and emerging markets.
  2. Income growth model: DCA with age-linked salary growth (e.g., +3%/year for first 20 years, flat thereafter), observing if optimal allocation advice changes. Income growth amplifies late-career contributions, potentially increasing sequence risk.
  3. Valuation-adjusted strategy: Dynamically adjust stock weight based on CAPE (reduce when CAPE high, increase when low), testing if risk-adjusted returns improve. Requires careful look-ahead bias testing.
  4. Refined behavioral model: Test different panic thresholds (-10%, -15%, -25%, -30%), different wait periods (3, 6, 18, 24 months), and partial exits (not all-or-nothing).
  5. Post-retirement linkage: Connect this study's accumulation-phase conclusions with V-013 (withdrawal strategies) for a complete accumulation-to-decumulation lifecycle simulation.

5.5 底線5.5 The Bottom Line

「年輕人應該 All-in 股票」——數據給出了一個有條件的答案。

如果你有穩定收入、投資期間超過 30 年、且能在 -40% 回檔時保持冷靜繼續投入，100% 股票確實是中位數最佳的選擇。但如果你的收入不穩定、投資期間較短、或者不確定自己能否在崩盤時堅持，60/40 或目標日期策略是更務實的選擇——它們犧牲了一些中位數報酬，但大幅降低了尾部風險和行為風險。

記住：投資是一場馬拉松。跑完全程比跑最快的前半段更重要。

"Young people should go all-in on stocks" — the data gives a conditional answer.

If you have stable income, 30+ year horizon, and can stay calm through -40% drawdowns, 100% stock is indeed the median-optimal choice. But if your income is unstable, horizon shorter, or you are unsure about staying disciplined in crashes, 60/40 or Target Date is the more pragmatic choice — sacrificing some median return for dramatically reduced tail and behavioral risk.

Remember: investing is a marathon. Finishing the race matters more than leading the first half.

本研究僅為學術分析，不構成投資建議。投資決策應考慮個人財務狀況、稅務、生活需求等因素。
This study is academic analysis only and does not constitute investment advice. Investment decisions should consider personal finances, taxes, and life circumstances.
