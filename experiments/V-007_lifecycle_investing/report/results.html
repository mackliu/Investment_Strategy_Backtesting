實證結果Results

3.1 終值分布3.1 Terminal Value Distributions

本研究最核心的結果是各策略在不同投資期間的終值分布。「終值」以「投入成本倍數」表示——例如 3.0x 代表每投入 1 元，最終持有 3 元（獲利 200%）。

在 30 年 DCA 投資生涯中，100% 股票策略的終值中位數為 3.05 倍，遠高於 80/20 的 2.86 倍、60/40 的 2.56 倍和 40/60 的 2.08 倍。目標日期策略（90→50）達到 2.62 倍，反向滑降為 2.67 倍。40 年生涯中差距更加擴大：100% 股票 4.58 倍 vs 60/40 3.41 倍。

然而，中位數只是故事的一半。100% 股票在 30 年最差 5% 情境下終值僅 1.57 倍，而最佳 5% 可達 6.38 倍——兩者相差 4.1 倍。60/40 的同期最差 5% 為 1.26 倍，結果更為集中。這意味著 100% 股票是一場「高賠率但方差極大」的賭注。

The study's core result is the terminal value distribution across strategies and periods. "Terminal value" is expressed as a multiple of total invested capital — 3.0x means 1 dollar invested becomes 3 dollars (200% gain).

For 30-year DCA careers, 100% stock achieves a median of 3.05x, well above 80/20's 2.86x, 60/40's 2.56x, and 40/60's 2.08x. Target Date reaches 2.62x, Reverse Glide 2.67x. At 40 years the gap widens further: 100% stock 4.58x vs 60/40 3.41x.

However, medians are only half the story. 100% stock at 30Y worst 5% yields only 1.57x while best 5% reaches 6.38x — a 4.1x spread. 60/40 worst 5% is 1.26x — much more concentrated. 100% stock is a "high-payoff but extremely high-variance" bet.

小提琴圖解讀：每個「小提琴」的寬度代表該終值出現的頻率。100% 股票（紅色）的分布有明顯的長右尾（少數人獲得極高報酬），但左尾也更寬（部分人僅勉強回本或虧損）。40/60（綠色）的分布最窄，代表結果最確定——但代價是中位數也最低。目標日期策略（虛線）的分布介於兩者之間，顯示動態調整能同時兼顧報酬和穩定性。

Violin plot interpretation: Each "violin" width represents the frequency of that terminal value. 100% stock (red) has a pronounced long right tail (few investors get extremely high returns) but also a wider left tail (some barely break even or lose). 40/60 (green) has the narrowest distribution — most certain outcomes but lowest median. Target Date (dashed) falls between, showing dynamic adjustment balances return and stability.

熱力圖摘要：此圖以顏色深淺呈現各策略在各期間的中位數終值和回撤。可以觀察到兩個清晰的模式：(1) 縱向看，投資期間越長，中位數越高（時間的複利效應）；(2) 橫向看，股票比重越高，中位數越高但回撤也越深（風險-報酬取捨）。

Heatmap summary: Color intensity shows median terminal values and drawdowns. Two clear patterns: (1) vertically, longer periods mean higher medians (compounding effect); (2) horizontally, higher stock weight means higher medians but deeper drawdowns (risk-return tradeoff).

3.2 虧損機率3.2 Loss Probability

虧損機率分析：虧損定義為「終值低於投入成本」（倍數 < 1.0x）。隨著投資期間延長，所有策略的虧損機率都顯著下降。

以 100% 股票為例：10 年虧損機率 11.5%，20 年降至 4.4%，30 年再降至 0.0%。60/40 在 10 年期的虧損機率為 11.2%，略低於 100% 股票。這支持了「時間是風險的朋友」這個論點——但需要重要的附加說明。

附加說明：(1) 虧損機率不等於風險的全部——即使不虧損，終值 1.1x（只賺 10%）和 3.0x（賺 200%）的體驗天差地遠；(2) 100% 股票在 30 年期的虧損機率雖低，但並非零——這意味著少數不幸的投資者即使堅持了 30 年，仍然虧損；(3) 這些數據基於美國市場（歷史上表現最佳的市場之一），使用其他國家的數據可能得到更高的虧損機率。

Loss probability: Loss is defined as terminal value below invested capital (< 1.0x). All strategies show declining loss probability with longer horizons.

100% stock: 10Y 11.5%, 20Y 4.4%, 30Y 0.0%. 60/40 at 10Y: 11.2%, slightly lower than 100% stock. This supports "time reduces risk" — with important caveats.

Caveats: (1) Loss probability is not the full picture — ending at 1.1x (10% gain) vs 3.0x (200%) are vastly different experiences; (2) 100% stock 30Y loss probability is low but not zero — some unlucky investors still lose after 30 years; (3) These figures are US-market-based (one of history's best performers); other countries may show higher loss rates.

3.3 策略比較3.3 Strategy Comparison

策略比較互動圖：此圖以長條呈現各策略在每個投資期間的中位數終值。在所有期間中，100% 股票的中位數最高，40/60 最低，其他策略介於之間。值得注意的是目標日期策略——它的中位數略高於 60/40（2.62x vs 2.56x），同時回撤更為溫和，顯示動態調整的優勢。

Strategy comparison: Bars show median terminal values by strategy and period. 100% stock leads in all periods, 40/60 is lowest, others fall between. Notably, Target Date has slightly higher median than 60/40 (2.62x vs 2.56x) with milder drawdowns, demonstrating dynamic adjustment benefits.

風險-報酬散佈圖：橫軸為中位數最大回撤（風險），縱軸為中位數終值（報酬）。理想的策略應在左上方（低風險、高報酬）。目標日期和反向滑降策略在此圖上位於「甜蜜區」——報酬接近 80/20，但回撤接近 60/40。100% 股票明顯在右上方（最高報酬，但最深回撤），說明額外的報酬是以顯著更高的風險為代價。

Risk-return scatter: X-axis is median max drawdown (risk), Y-axis is median terminal value (return). Ideal strategies sit in the upper-left (low risk, high return). Target Date and Reverse Glide occupy a "sweet spot" — returns near 80/20 but drawdowns near 60/40. 100% stock is clearly upper-right (highest return but deepest drawdown), showing extra return comes at significantly higher risk cost.

3.4 序列風險3.4 Sequence Risk

什麼是序列風險？即使兩個投資者在相同的 30 年期間投資，如果一位在職涯初期遇到崩盤、另一位在退休前遇到崩盤，結果可能天差地遠。初期崩盤時累積金額小、影響有限，且後續的定額投入以低價買進，反而受益。末期崩盤時累積金額大，且無後續投入可以攤平成本——損失是「不可逆的」。

What is sequence risk? Even if two investors invest over the same 30-year span, one facing a crash early and another facing it near retirement can have vastly different outcomes. Early crashes affect a small accumulation and subsequent DCA buys at low prices actually help. Late crashes devastate a large accumulation with no subsequent contributions to average down — losses are "irreversible."

散佈圖解讀：每個點代表一條歷史路徑（100% 股票, 30 年）。橫軸是最大回撤發生的生涯時點（0 = 開始, 1 = 結束），縱軸是終值倍數。趨勢線清楚顯示——崩盤越晚發生，終值越低。初期崩盤（綠點，時點 < 0.3）的終值中位數約 5.29 倍；末期崩盤（紅點，時點 > 0.7）僅約 2.83 倍——差距高達 1.87 倍。Kruskal-Wallis 檢定確認三組之間存在極顯著差異（H = 391.4, p < 0.001），效果量 d = 2.06（極大）。

這是本研究最重要的風險警示：接近退休時仍持有 100% 股票，是一種對序列風險毫無防備的配置。目標日期策略透過在末期降低股票比重，正是為了緩解這個風險。

Scatter plot interpretation: Each dot is a historical path (100% stock, 30Y). X-axis is when the worst drawdown occurred (0 = start, 1 = end), Y-axis is terminal value. The trend clearly shows later crashes produce lower terminal values. Early crashes (green, < 0.3) have median ~5.29x; late crashes (red, > 0.7) only ~2.83x — a 1.87x gap. Kruskal-Wallis confirms highly significant group differences (H = 391.4, p < 0.001), effect size d = 2.06 (very large).

This is the study's most important risk warning: maintaining 100% stock near retirement is completely unprotected against sequence risk. Target Date strategies address this by reducing stock weight as retirement approaches.

3.5 恐慌賣出的代價3.5 Cost of Panic Selling

模擬設計：我們比較兩種投資者行為——紀律投資者（始終執行策略，無論市場如何）和恐慌賣出者（回撤超過 20% 時全部出場，12 個月後才重新進場）。兩者使用完全相同的歷史報酬路徑，唯一差異是行為反應。

Simulation design: We compare two investor types — disciplined (stays the course regardless of market conditions) and panic seller (exits when drawdown exceeds 20%, re-enters after 12 months). Both use identical historical return paths; the only difference is behavioral response.

恐慌賣出的代價：上圖以直方圖對比紀律投資者（綠色）和恐慌賣出者（紅色）的終值分布。紅色分布明顯左移——恐慌賣出者的終值中位數顯著低於紀律投資者。Wilcoxon 檢定結果：100% 股票策略中，恐慌賣出的代價（中位數差異）約為投入成本的 0.30x，p < 0.001，效果量 d = 0.49（中等）。60/40 策略中效果更大——d = 0.72（中偏大），因為 60/40 在崩盤時跌幅較小、觸發恐慌的次數較少，但一旦觸發，對低回撤策略的傷害更嚴重。

核心啟示：如果你無法在 -30% 時保持冷靜，100% 股票不是你的策略。選擇一個你能在最壞情況下仍然堅持的配置——60/40 的紀律投資者，長期報酬優於 100% 股票的恐慌賣出者。

Cost of panic selling: The histogram contrasts disciplined (green) and panic sellers (red). Red distribution shifts clearly leftward — panic sellers achieve significantly lower terminal values. Wilcoxon test: for 100% stock, panic cost (median difference) is ~0.30x of invested capital, p < 0.001, effect d = 0.49 (medium). For 60/40, effect is larger — d = 0.72 (medium-large), because 60/40 triggers fewer panics but each trigger damages low-drawdown strategies more severely.

Key insight: If you cannot stay calm at -30%, 100% stock is not your strategy. Choose an allocation you can maintain in the worst scenario — a disciplined 60/40 investor outperforms a panicking 100% stock investor long-term.
