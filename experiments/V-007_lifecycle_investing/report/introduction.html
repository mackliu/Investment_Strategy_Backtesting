緒論Introduction

1.1 背景1.1 Background

「年輕人有時間等回本，所以應該 100% 股票」是投資圈最廣為流傳的建議之一。從 PTT 股版到各大理財 YouTube 頻道，這個論點幾乎已成為共識。其核心邏輯看似無懈可擊：(1) 股票長期報酬率遠超債券——美國股市 150 年的實質年化報酬約 6.9%，債券僅約 2.4%；(2) 投資期間越長，股票的虧損機率越低——20 年以上幾乎不會虧損；(3) 年輕人的人力資本（未來薪資收入）本質上類似債券，因此金融資產應全部投入股票以達到最佳分散。

然而，這個看似完美的邏輯存在幾個致命盲點。首先，「長期不虧」不等於「短期不痛」——即使最終回本，途中 -50% 以上的回撤足以讓多數人恐慌賣出。其次，「時間分散化」的概念本身存在學術爭議——Bodie (1995) 指出，雖然年化波動度隨時間下降，但終值的絕對不確定性（以金額衡量）實際上隨時間增加。最後，「平均」報酬掩蓋了巨大的路徑差異——退休前遭遇崩盤的投資者，結果可能與退休前遇到牛市的投資者天差地遠。

本研究的核心問題是：如果我們不看平均值，而是看「所有可能的歷史結果」，100% 股票策略在不同情境下的真實面貌是什麼？它真的適合所有年輕人嗎？還是存在某些被忽略的風險，需要更細膩的策略？

"Young people have time to recover, so they should invest 100% in stocks" is one of the most widespread investment advice. The logic seems airtight: (1) stocks vastly outperform bonds long-term — US stocks returned ~6.9% real annually over 150 years vs ~2.4% for bonds; (2) longer horizons reduce stock loss probability — nearly zero beyond 20 years; (3) young people's human capital (future wages) is bond-like, so financial assets should be all-stock.

However, this logic has critical blind spots. First, "no loss long-term" does not mean "no pain short-term" — a -50% drawdown during the journey is enough to trigger panic selling for most people. Second, "time diversification" is academically contested — Bodie (1995) shows that while annualized volatility declines with time, absolute terminal value uncertainty actually increases. Finally, "average" returns mask enormous path dependence — an investor facing a crash before retirement may have vastly different outcomes than one facing a bull market.

The core question: if we examine not averages but "all possible historical outcomes," what is the true picture of 100% stock strategy across scenarios? Is it suitable for all young people, or are there overlooked risks requiring more nuanced strategies?

1.2 文獻回顧1.2 Literature Review

支持高股票配置的陣營：

Jeremy Siegel (2014) 的經典著作《散戶投資正典》（Stocks for the Long Run）是「年輕人全股票」的理論基石。他以 1802 年以來的美國數據證明，股票在任何 20 年以上的投資期間中，實質報酬均為正值。更重要的是，他發現股票的「長期風險」（以 20-30 年滾動報酬的標準差衡量）實際上低於債券——這是支持時間分散化最有力的證據。

Dimson, Marsh & Staunton (2002) 以 16 個國家、101 年的跨國數據支持了 Siegel 的結論：在幾乎所有國家，股票的長期實質報酬都顯著高於債券。他們的數據也顯示股票溢酬（equity risk premium）約為 3-5% 年化——這是承擔股票波動所獲得的「風險補償」。

反對「時間分散化」的陣營：

Zvi Bodie (1995) 的重要論文指出「時間分散化」是一種危險的謬誤。他的核心論點是：用選擇權定價的方式衡量，為投資組合購買「不虧損保險」的成本，隨投資期間增加而上升，而非下降。換言之，市場認為長期持有股票的風險更大，而非更小。年化波動度下降只是數學上的錯覺——真正重要的是終值的分布，而終值的方差隨時間擴大。

Pastor & Stambaugh (2012) 進一步指出，當考慮「參數不確定性」（我們對未來報酬率和波動度的估計存在誤差）後，股票的長期風險可能比短期更高。他們的研究為 Bodie 的理論提供了實證支持。

生命週期投資理論：

Ayres & Nalebuff (2010) 提出了反直覺的建議：年輕人不僅應該 100% 股票，甚至應該用槓桿加碼。他們的邏輯是——年輕時金融資本太少（可能只有總財富的 5-10%），即使 100% 股票也無法在人生尺度上實現最佳的時間分散化。這個理論在學術界引起了激烈辯論，但在實務上幾乎無人採用。

更務實的生命週期理論（如 Target Date Fund 的設計哲學）建議：年輕時以高股票比例開始，隨年齡增加逐漸降低——通常是「年齡 = 債券比例」的經驗法則（30 歲 → 30% 債券）。這兼顧了年輕時承擔風險的能力和退休前降低波動的需求。

行為金融學的警示：

Benartzi & Thaler (1995) 的損失趨避（Loss Aversion）研究揭示了理論與實踐的鴻溝：人類對虧損的痛感約為獲利快感的 2.5 倍。即使理論上 100% 股票長期最優，多數投資人在面對 -20% 以上的虧損時會恐慌賣出，「鎖定」虧損後卻錯過後續反彈。選擇配置時，「自己能否堅持」比「理論是否最優」更為關鍵。

Supporting high equity allocation:

Jeremy Siegel (2014) in "Stocks for the Long Run" showed that US stocks have positive real returns for every 20+ year period since 1802. Moreover, stocks' "long-run risk" (measured by 20-30 year rolling return SD) is actually lower than bonds — the strongest evidence for time diversification.

Dimson, Marsh & Staunton (2002) confirmed this across 16 countries over 101 years: stocks outperform bonds in virtually all countries, with equity risk premium of 3-5% annualized.

Against "time diversification":

Zvi Bodie (1995) argues time diversification is a dangerous fallacy. Using option pricing, the cost of insuring a portfolio against loss increases with horizon, not decreases. The market prices longer stock holding as riskier, not safer. Declining annualized volatility is a mathematical illusion — terminal value variance expands with time.

Pastor & Stambaugh (2012) show that accounting for parameter uncertainty, long-run stock risk may exceed short-run risk, providing empirical support for Bodie.

Lifecycle investing theory:

Ayres & Nalebuff (2010) suggest young people should even use leverage, since their financial capital is too small for proper time diversification. More pragmatic lifecycle theory (Target Date Fund philosophy) recommends starting high-stock and gradually reducing — the "age = bond percentage" rule of thumb.

Behavioral finance warning:

Benartzi & Thaler (1995) show loss aversion is ~2.5x stronger than gain pleasure. Despite theoretical optimality of 100% stock, most investors panic sell at -20%+ drawdowns, "locking in" losses and missing subsequent recovery. The ability to maintain discipline matters more than theoretical optimization.

Q1 的意義：這是最基本的問題——如果 100% 股票在所有投資期間都「統計上」優於其他配置，那「年輕人應該全股票」的建議在報酬面就有數據支持。但我們不僅看中位數，也看尾部風險。

Q2 的意義：生命週期策略是否能在不大幅犧牲報酬的前提下降低風險？如果答案為是，那「隨年齡調整配置」就是一個優於「固定 100% 股票」的策略。

Q3 的意義：序列風險是「年輕人全股票」最大的隱藏威脅。如果投資末期的崩盤對終值影響極大，那麼接近退休時仍持有 100% 股票就是不負責任的配置。

Q4 的意義：如果恐慌賣出的損失大於次優配置的機會成本，那「選擇你能堅持的配置」就比「選擇理論最優配置」更為重要。

Q1 significance: The most basic question — if 100% stock is statistically superior across all periods, the "young people should go all-in" advice has data support. We examine both medians and tail risks.

Q2 significance: Can lifecycle strategies reduce risk without significantly sacrificing return? If yes, "age-adjusted allocation" is superior to "fixed 100% stock."

Q3 significance: Sequence risk is the hidden threat of all-stock allocation. If late-career crashes devastate terminal values, maintaining 100% stock near retirement is irresponsible.

Q4 significance: If panic selling losses exceed suboptimal allocation opportunity costs, "choose what you can stick with" is more important than "choose the theoretical optimum."

1.4 研究貢獻1.4 Research Contributions

  • 完整歷史路徑分析：不使用 Monte Carlo 隨機模擬，而是以所有可能的歷史起點進行滾動回測，保留了報酬序列的真實結構（自相關、波動群聚、厚尾）。
  • 六種策略全面比較：涵蓋固定比例和動態策略（目標日期、反向滑降），提供投資人更完整的決策參考。
  • 序列風險量化：以崩盤時點為變數，直接量化序列風險的效果大小。
  • 行為模擬：首次在長期投資模擬中加入「恐慌賣出」行為的影響分析。
  • 台灣在地驗證：以 0050 ETF 檢驗相同結論在台灣市場是否成立。
  • Complete historical path analysis: Uses all possible historical starting points instead of Monte Carlo, preserving real return structure (autocorrelation, volatility clustering, fat tails).
  • Six-strategy comprehensive comparison: Covers fixed and dynamic strategies (Target Date, Reverse Glide) for more complete decision-making.
  • Sequence risk quantification: Directly measures effect size using crash timing as variable.
  • Behavioral simulation: First-of-its-kind panic selling impact analysis in long-term DCA simulation.
  • Taiwan local validation: Tests conclusions with 0050 ETF in Taiwan market.
