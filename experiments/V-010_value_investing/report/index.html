V-010: 低 PE/PB 選股必勝V-010: Low PE/PB Always Wins

研究問題：「低 PE/PB 的價值股長期必勝成長股」這個信條是否成立？價值溢酬是否正在消失？價值陷阱有多常見？

Research Question: Does the belief that "low PE/PB value stocks always beat growth" hold? Is the value premium disappearing? How common are value traps?

「買便宜的好股票」是投資界流傳最久的信條之一。從 Benjamin Graham 到 Warren Buffett，無數投資大師都宣揚低 PE（本益比）、低 PB（股價淨值比）的選股策略。學術界同樣支持：Fama & French (1992) 發現低 PB 股票每年平均比高 PB 股票多賺 4-5%。

但這個「常識」正面臨前所未有的挑戰。過去十年，全球科技股領漲，價值股全面落後。本研究以台灣市值前 30 大上市公司（2010-2025）與美國 Fama-French 因子（1926-2025）進行雙市場分析，從 PE、PB、殖利率三個價值指標建構 quintile 投組，並以嚴謹的統計方法檢驗價值溢酬是否真的「必勝」。

"Buy cheap good stocks" is one of the longest-standing beliefs in investing. From Benjamin Graham to Warren Buffett, countless investment legends have championed low PE and low PB stock selection strategies. Academia agreed: Fama & French (1992) found low PB stocks outperform high PB by 4-5% annually.

But this "common wisdom" faces unprecedented challenges. Over the past decade, global tech stocks led while value stocks lagged. This dual-market study uses Taiwan top-30 stocks (2010-2025) and US Fama-French factors (1926-2025), constructing quintile portfolios from PE, PB, and yield to rigorously test whether value truly "always wins."

上圖展示 PE quintile Q1（最便宜）與 Q5（最貴）的累積報酬比較。兩者的差距反映了價值溢酬的大小。如果曲線持續分離，代表便宜股票確實跑贏昂貴股票；如果曲線交織或 Q5 反超，則價值溢酬可能已不存在。

The chart compares cumulative returns of PE quintile Q1 (cheapest) vs Q5 (most expensive). The gap reflects the value premium magnitude. If the curves continuously diverge, cheap stocks indeed outperform expensive ones; if they intertwine or Q5 overtakes, the value premium may no longer exist.

主要發現Key Findings

  • 價值陷阱普遍：平均 51% 的月份，最便宜的股票反而跑輸市場平均——便宜不等於被低估，有些股票便宜是有原因的。
  • 價值溢酬顯著衰減：2015 年後，價值因子的報酬大幅縮水，台灣與美國市場同步出現此現象。
  • 複合因子略勝單一因子：結合 PE/PB/殖利率的複合排名比單一指標更穩健，但差距並不懸殊。
  • 美國 HML 同步衰減：Fama-French HML 自 2010 年代起表現不佳，全球價值因子面臨結構性挑戰。
  • Value traps are common: On average, 51% of months the cheapest stocks underperform the market — cheap does not mean undervalued; some stocks are cheap for good reason.
  • Value premium decays: Post-2015, value factor returns significantly diminished, a phenomenon observed simultaneously in Taiwan and the US.
  • Composite beats single: Combined PE/PB/yield ranking is more robust than any single metric, though the margin is not dramatic.
  • US HML also decays: Fama-French HML underperforms since the 2010s, suggesting structural challenges for global value factors.
