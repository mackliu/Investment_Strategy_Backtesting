V-010：低 PE/PB 選股必勝V-010: Low PE/PB Always Wins

討論Discussion

4.1 核心發現4.1 Core Findings

發現一：價值溢酬存在但不穩定。台灣低 PE/PB 股票在部分時期確實勝過高 PE/PB 股票，但差異不如文獻中描述的那麼穩定。這與 Asness et al. (2013) 跨市場研究的發現一致：價值因子在不同市場的表現差異很大。用生活化的比喻來說，價值投資就像釣魚——在某些魚塘、某些季節特別有效，但不是到哪裡都能大豐收。

發現二：價值溢酬顯著衰減。無論台灣還是美國市場，價值因子在 2015 年後的表現都明顯弱於歷史平均。正如 Fama & French (2021) 指出的，HML 從歷史的 4.3%/年降至不到 1%。可能原因包括：(1) 因子套利的普及——當太多人使用同一策略，原本的超額報酬就會被「套利」掉，就像當所有人都知道某家餐廳好吃而排長龍時，排隊的「成本」就抵消了「好吃」的「收益」；(2) 科技股主導的市場結構變化——NVIDIA、台積電等高 PE 公司的獲利持續超預期，使得「高 PE = 高估」的等式不再成立；(3) 傳統 PB 無法反映無形資產密集型企業的真實價值——微軟的品牌、Google 的數據、台積電的製程技術，這些都不會反映在帳面價值上，導致 PB 排序將優質科技公司錯誤歸類為「昂貴」。

發現三：價值陷阱普遍存在。平均有 51% 的月份，最便宜的股票反而落後市場。大量低 PE/PB 股票實際上是基本面惡化的公司——便宜是有原因的。這就像在清倉拍賣時看到的「超低價」商品，很多時候便宜是因為品質有問題，而非真正的好康。這支持了 Arnott et al. (2021) 和 Novy-Marx (2013) 的觀點：需要更精細的價值衡量方式，尤其是結合品質因子來過濾「假便宜」。

Finding 1: Value premium exists but is unstable. TW low PE/PB stocks outperform in some periods, but not as consistently as classic literature suggests. Consistent with Asness et al. (2013): value factor varies significantly across markets. In everyday terms, value investing is like fishing — it works great at certain ponds in certain seasons, but not everywhere all the time.

Finding 2: Value premium significantly decayed. Both TW and US markets show weaker value factor performance post-2015. As Fama & French (2021) noted, HML dropped from 4.3%/yr historically to under 1%. Possible causes: (1) factor arbitrage crowding — when too many people use the same strategy, excess returns get "arbitraged away," like a great restaurant losing its advantage when everyone queues up; (2) tech stock structural dominance — NVIDIA, TSMC with high PE yet consistently beating earnings expectations, breaking the "high PE = overvalued" equation; (3) traditional PB fails to capture intangible assets — Microsoft's brand, Google's data, TSMC's process technology are not reflected in book value, causing PB sorting to misclassify quality tech companies as "expensive."

Finding 3: Value traps are pervasive. In 51% of months, the cheapest stocks actually underperform the market. Many low PE/PB stocks have deteriorating fundamentals — cheap for a reason. It is like 'clearance sale' items that are cheap because of quality issues, not genuine bargains. This supports Arnott et al. (2021) and Novy-Marx (2013): more sophisticated value measures are needed, especially combining quality factors to filter "fake cheap" stocks.

4.2 注意事項4.2 Important Caveats

  • 標的池有限：僅 30 檔大型股，無法完整代表全市場。中小型股的價值溢酬可能與大型股不同（Israel & Moskowitz 2013 指出大型股的價值效應較弱）。
  • 存續偏差（Survivorship Bias）：使用當前市值前 30，未考慮歷史退市公司。已退市的公司可能正是那些「價值陷阱」——因為基本面惡化而被市場淘汰的公司不在我們的分析中。
  • 交易成本未計：季度再平衡涉及賣出舊持股、買入新排名的股票，每次約 0.4-0.7% 的交易成本。如果價值溢酬本身只有 1-2%，一年四次再平衡的交易成本就可能吞噬大部分甚至全部的超額報酬。
  • FinMind 數據品質：免費版 API 可能存在部分日期遺漏或數據延遲的問題。我們無法交叉驗證每一筆 PE/PB 數據的準確性，這可能導致部分排序結果有偏差。
  • 前視偏差風險：我們使用「當前」市值前 30 大的名單，但回測起始日（2010 年）的前 30 大公司可能與現在不同。嚴格的無偏差設計應使用每個時點的「當時」前 30 大。
  • 期間偏差：2010-2025 恰好是全球科技股大牛市，這段期間成長股的優異表現可能誇大了價值溢酬衰減的程度。如果未來科技股泡沫破裂，價值因子可能重新崛起。
  • 因子定義敏感性：不同的價值指標（PE vs PB vs 殖利率 vs 複合）和不同的分組方式（五等分 vs 三等分 vs 十等分）可能產生不同的結論。本研究的結論僅適用於所選的特定定義。
  • Limited universe: Only 30 large caps, not representative of full market. Small-cap value premium may differ (Israel & Moskowitz 2013 noted weaker large-cap value effect).
  • Survivorship bias: Using current top 30, not accounting for historical delistings. Delisted companies may have been the very "value traps" — companies eliminated due to deteriorating fundamentals are absent from our analysis.
  • No transaction costs: Quarterly rebalancing involves selling old holdings and buying new ranking stocks, costing ~0.4-0.7% each time. If the value premium is only 1-2%, four annual rebalances may consume most or all excess returns.
  • FinMind data quality: Free API may have date gaps or delays. We cannot cross-verify every PE/PB data point, potentially causing some ranking inaccuracies.
  • Look-ahead bias risk: We use the "current" top 30 by market cap, but the top 30 at the backtest start (2010) may differ from today. A strictly unbiased design should use the "then-current" top 30 at each point in time.
  • Period bias: 2010-2025 coincides with a massive global tech bull market. Growth stocks' superior performance during this period may exaggerate the degree of value premium decay. If a tech bubble bursts, value may resurge.
  • Factor definition sensitivity: Different value metrics (PE vs PB vs yield vs composite) and grouping methods (quintile vs tercile vs decile) may yield different conclusions. Our results apply only to the specific definitions chosen.

4.3 投資啟示4.3 Investment Implications

對保守型投資人：不建議單純依賴 PE/PB 選股。如果你是偏好低風險、穩定收益的投資人，數據顯示單純買「便宜股」的策略在近十年表現不佳，而且價值陷阱的風險很高。建議考慮指數化投資（如 0050），避免主動選股的陷阱。如果堅持要做價值投資，至少要搭配品質指標（ROE > 15%、營收持續成長等）來篩選。

對穩健型投資人：可以將價值因子作為多因子策略的一個組成部分，而非唯一依據。例如結合價值（低 PE）+ 品質（高 ROE）+ 動能（近 6 個月上漲）三因子，能有效降低價值陷阱的風險。此外，季度再平衡的頻率可以考慮降為半年或年度，以減少交易成本。

對積極型投資人：如果你相信價值因子是週期性的而非永久消亡，那麼目前可能正是價值投資的「低谷」——歷史上每次成長股泡沫破裂後（如 2000 年科技泡沫），價值因子都會強勁反彈。但這是一場豪賭：你需要有足夠的耐心和資金撐過可能長達 5-10 年的低迷期。美國 FF HML 的歷史顯示，價值因子的「冬眠期」可以非常漫長。

For conservative investors: Do not rely solely on PE/PB stock selection. If you prefer low risk and stable returns, data shows that simply buying "cheap stocks" has performed poorly in the past decade, with high value trap risk. Consider index investing (e.g., 0050) to avoid active selection pitfalls. If you insist on value investing, at minimum combine with quality metrics (ROE > 15%, sustained revenue growth, etc.).

For moderate investors: Consider value factor as one component of a multi-factor strategy, not the sole criterion. For example, combining value (low PE) + quality (high ROE) + momentum (6-month uptrend) can effectively reduce value trap risk. Also consider reducing rebalancing frequency from quarterly to semi-annual or annual to cut transaction costs.

For aggressive investors: If you believe the value factor is cyclical rather than permanently dead, the current period may be a 'trough' — historically, after every growth stock bubble burst (e.g., 2000 tech bubble), value factors rebounded strongly. But this is a bold bet: you need sufficient patience and capital to survive potentially 5-10 years of malaise. US FF HML history shows that value factor 'hibernation periods' can be extremely prolonged.
