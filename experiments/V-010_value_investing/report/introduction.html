V-010：低 PE/PB 選股必勝V-010: Low PE/PB Always Wins

緒論Introduction

1.1 背景1.1 Background

「買低 PE/PB 股票」可能是股票市場最古老的投資信條之一。自 Benjamin Graham 在 1934 年出版的《證券分析》提出價值投資理念以來，「買便宜的好公司」一直是投資人的核心策略。用日常生活來比喻，這就像是在百貨公司週年慶時搶購打折商品——同樣品質的東西，用更低的價格買入，長期下來理應獲得更高的報酬。

學術研究也長期支持這個觀點：Fama & French (1992, 1993) 的三因子模型將「HML（High Minus Low，價值減成長）」列為系統性風險因子，實證顯示低 PB 股票組合每年平均比高 PB 組合多賺約 4-5%。在台灣，價值投資同樣受到推崇——許多投資書籍和理財節目鼓勵投資人關注「低本益比」、「低股價淨值比」的個股，宣稱這是散戶「穩穩賺」的不二法門。

然而，近年來出現了令人不安的轉變。全球科技巨頭（蘋果、微軟、NVIDIA 等）的 PE 動輒 30-50 倍，卻持續大幅上漲；反觀低 PE 的傳統產業股卻表現平庸。Fama & French (2021) 自己也承認，價值溢酬從歷史平均的 4.3% 大幅下降至近十年的 0.6%。這引發了激烈辯論：價值投資真的「死了」嗎？還是正在經歷暫時的低潮？本研究正是要以數據回答這個問題。

"Buy low PE/PB stocks" may be the oldest investment creed. Since Benjamin Graham published Security Analysis in 1934, "buy cheap quality companies" has been a core strategy. Think of it like shopping during a big sale — same quality goods at a lower price should yield better long-term returns.

Academic research long supported this: Fama & French (1992, 1993) three-factor model established HML (High Minus Low) as a systematic risk factor, showing low PB stocks outperform high PB by ~4-5% annually. In Taiwan, value investing is equally revered — investment books and financial TV shows encourage investors to focus on "low PE" and "low PB" stocks as a reliable path to steady returns.

However, a concerning shift has occurred. Global tech giants (Apple, Microsoft, NVIDIA) trade at PE ratios of 30-50x yet keep rising; while low PE traditional industry stocks languish. Fama & French (2021) acknowledged the value premium dropped from historical 4.3% to 0.6% in the recent decade. Is value investing "dead"? Or just in a temporary slump? This study seeks to answer that question with data.

1.2 文獻回顧1.2 Literature Review

價值因子奠基：Fama & French (1992) 在 Journal of Finance 發現，PB（股價淨值比）是解釋股票截面報酬最有力的變數之一，開創了價值因子研究的先河。簡單說，他們把所有股票按「股價相對帳面價值的貴便宜」排序，發現最便宜那組長期表現最好。1993 年的三因子模型（市場、規模、價值）成為資產定價的標準框架，至今仍是金融學教科書的核心內容。

行為解釋：Lakonishok, Shleifer & Vishny (1994) 認為價值溢酬來自投資人的行為偏差：市場過度外推近期表現，導致成長股被高估、價值股被低估。就像人們看到某家餐廳排長龍就認定它一定很好吃，但其實可能只是行銷做得好——市場對「熱門股」的追捧也是同樣道理。

獲利能力因子：Novy-Marx (2013) 在 Journal of Financial Economics 發現，毛利率比 PB 更能預測股票報酬。這意味著「便宜」固然重要，但「品質好且便宜」才是真正的贏家。後來 Fama & French (2015) 將獲利能力因子納入五因子模型，承認光看價值是不夠的。

價值溢酬衰減：Fama & French (2021) 在 Review of Asset Pricing Studies 承認近年價值溢酬大幅下降。Arnott et al. (2021) 在 Financial Analysts Journal 則辯護說，如果調整 PB 的計算方式（納入無形資產如專利、品牌價值），價值因子其實仍然有效——問題不在於價值投資「死了」，而是傳統的衡量方式已經過時。

跨市場證據：Asness, Moskowitz & Pedersen (2013) 在 Journal of Finance 發現價值因子在多個國家和資產類別中存在，但 Israel & Moskowitz (2013) 指出大型股的價值溢酬明顯較弱。這對台灣市場尤其重要，因為本研究使用的是市值前 30 大公司（大型股），價值效應可能較不明顯。

「魔法公式」：Greenblatt (2006) 在暢銷書《超越大盤的獲利公式》中提出將 PE 與資本報酬率結合的選股策略，宣稱能長期打敗市場。這個概念啟發了我們使用複合因子（PE + PB + 殖利率）的研究設計。

衡量方式演進：Goncalves & Leonard (2023) 在 JFE 探討了價值指標的演進，認為傳統 PB 可能不再是最佳的價值衡量方式。在數位經濟時代，企業的核心資產往往是無形的（軟體、數據、品牌），這些都不會反映在帳面價值上。

Value factor foundation: Fama & French (1992, Journal of Finance) found PB is one of the most powerful cross-sectional return predictors. In simple terms, they sorted all stocks by "how cheap the stock price is relative to book value" and found the cheapest group performed best long-term. The 1993 three-factor model (market, size, value) became the standard asset pricing framework, still a core topic in finance textbooks today.

Behavioral explanation: LSV (1994) attributed value premium to behavioral biases: markets extrapolate recent performance, overvaluing growth and undervaluing value stocks. It is like assuming a restaurant with a long line must be great — the market's obsession with 'hot stocks' works the same way.

Profitability factor: Novy-Marx (2013, JFE) found gross profitability predicts returns better than PB. This means "cheap" matters, but "high quality AND cheap" is the real winner. Fama & French (2015) later added profitability to their five-factor model, acknowledging value alone is insufficient.

Premium decay: Fama & French (2021) acknowledged significant recent decline. Arnott et al. (2021) argued adjusting for intangible assets (patents, brand value) restores the value factor — the problem is not that value investing "died" but that traditional metrics are outdated.

Cross-market: Asness et al. (2013) found value across countries, but Israel & Moskowitz (2013) noted large-cap value premium is weaker. This is especially relevant for Taiwan since our study uses top-30 companies by market cap (large caps), where the value effect may be less pronounced.

"Magic Formula": Greenblatt (2006) proposed combining PE with return on capital in his bestseller, claiming this strategy beats the market long-term. This concept inspired our composite factor (PE + PB + yield) research design.

Measurement evolution: Goncalves & Leonard (2023) explored how value metrics evolved, suggesting traditional PB may no longer be optimal. In the digital economy, core corporate assets are often intangible (software, data, brand) and do not appear on the balance sheet.

1.3 研究假說1.3 Hypotheses

編號 假說 方法
H1 台灣低 PE quintile 年化報酬 > 高 PE quintile t-test on HML
H2 價值溢酬近 10 年顯著衰減 Structural break test
H3 價值陷阱比例 > 20% Proportion test
H4 複合因子 Sharpe > 單一因子 Sharpe comparison

H1 是最核心的問題：買便宜股票（低 PE）是否真的比買貴股票（高 PE）賺更多？我們用 HML（Q1 減 Q5）的平均報酬來檢驗。如果 HML 顯著大於零，代表價值溢酬存在。

H2 檢驗價值溢酬是否正在消失。我們用結構性斷點檢定（structural break test）尋找 HML 時序中的轉折點——如果存在顯著斷點，代表價值溢酬的性質在某個時間點發生了根本變化。

H3 量化「價值陷阱」的嚴重程度。所謂價值陷阱，是指那些 PE/PB 看似便宜、但實際上基本面惡化的股票——它們便宜是因為市場正確地反映了其糟糕的前景。

H4 探討是否可以透過結合多個指標來改善選股效果。直覺上，PE 低且 PB 低且殖利率高的股票，比僅看單一指標更可能是真正被低估的好公司。

# Hypothesis Method
H1 TW low PE quintile return > high PE quintile t-test on HML
H2 Value premium declined significantly in recent decade Structural break
H3 Value trap rate > 20% Proportion test
H4 Composite factor Sharpe > single factor Sharpe comparison

H1 is the core question: do cheap stocks (low PE) really earn more than expensive stocks (high PE)? We test this using the average return of HML (Q1 minus Q5). If HML is significantly positive, the value premium exists.

H2 tests whether the value premium is disappearing. We use structural break tests to find turning points in the HML time series — a significant break means the nature of the value premium fundamentally changed at some point.

H3 quantifies the severity of "value traps." A value trap is a stock that appears cheap by PE/PB but actually has deteriorating fundamentals — it is cheap because the market correctly reflects its poor prospects.

H4 explores whether combining multiple metrics improves stock selection. Intuitively, stocks with low PE AND low PB AND high yield are more likely to be genuinely undervalued than those identified by any single metric alone.

1.4 研究貢獻1.4 Research Contribution

本研究的貢獻在於：(1) 同時分析台灣與美國市場，提供跨市場比較；(2) 系統性量化價值陷阱的發生頻率，這在台灣的實證文獻中較少被討論；(3) 比較單一因子與複合因子的選股效果，為投資人提供實用的策略參考；(4) 使用結構性斷點檢定來辨識價值溢酬衰減的確切時間點，而非僅靠目測。

This study contributes by: (1) analyzing Taiwan and US markets simultaneously for cross-market comparison; (2) systematically quantifying value trap frequency, rarely discussed in Taiwan empirical literature; (3) comparing single vs composite factor stock selection, providing practical strategy insights; (4) using structural break tests to identify exact timing of value premium decay, rather than relying on visual inspection.
