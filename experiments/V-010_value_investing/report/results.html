實證結果Results

3.1 Quintile 累積報酬3.1 Quintile Cumulative Returns

首先看最基本的問題：按 PE 排序的五個投組，長期表現是否真的有差異？下圖展示 Q1（最便宜，低 PE）到 Q5（最貴，高 PE）的累積報酬走勢，使用對數尺度以便觀察長期趨勢。

First, the most fundamental question: do the five portfolios sorted by PE actually differ in long-term performance? The chart below shows cumulative returns from Q1 (cheapest, low PE) to Q5 (most expensive, high PE) on a log scale for long-term trend observation.

關鍵觀察：(1) 各 quintile 之間確實存在報酬差異，但差距並不像教科書描述的那麼大；(2) Q1（最便宜）在部分時期領先 Q5（最貴），但並非全程壓倒性勝出——曲線在某些時段甚至交叉；(3) 使用對數尺度可以看出，報酬差異在早期較明顯，2015 年後逐漸收斂。這暗示「便宜股必勝」的論述可能過於簡化。

Key observations: (1) Return differences between quintiles do exist, but are not as dramatic as textbooks suggest; (2) Q1 (cheapest) leads Q5 (most expensive) in some periods, but not overwhelmingly — curves even cross at certain points; (3) On log scale, return differences were more pronounced early on but converged after 2015. This suggests the "cheap stocks always win" narrative may be oversimplified.

3.2 HML 時序分析3.2 HML Time Series Analysis

HML（High Minus Low）是價值投資研究的核心指標——它直接衡量「便宜股比貴股多賺了多少」。如果 HML 的累積線持續上升，代表價值策略一直在賺錢；如果走平或下降，代表價值策略失效。

HML (High Minus Low) is the core metric in value investing research — it directly measures "how much more cheap stocks earn vs expensive ones." If the HML cumulative line keeps rising, the value strategy is working; if flat or declining, it is failing.

關鍵觀察：HML 的累積報酬曲線並非一路向上。可以看到幾個明顯的階段：早期有上升趨勢（價值溢酬存在），但中後期曲線走平甚至下降，顯示價值溢酬正在消退。不同指標（PE-HML、PB-HML、Yield-HML）的走勢大致一致，但殖利率排序的 HML 通常較為穩定。

這張圖直接對應 H1（價值溢酬是否存在）和 H2（是否衰減）。如果曲線始終在 1.0 以上且持續上升，H1 就能被支持。但實際情況更為複雜——價值溢酬似乎「有時存在、有時消失」，這正是需要統計檢定的原因。

Key observations: The HML cumulative curve is not a straight path up. Several distinct phases are visible: early uptrend (value premium exists), but the curve flattens or declines in later periods, suggesting premium decay. Different metrics (PE-HML, PB-HML, Yield-HML) show broadly similar patterns, though yield-sorted HML tends to be more stable.

This chart directly addresses H1 (does the value premium exist) and H2 (is it declining). If the curve stays above 1.0 and keeps rising, H1 is supported. But reality is more complex — the value premium seems to "sometimes exist, sometimes vanish," which is precisely why statistical testing is needed.

3.3 滾動溢酬3.3 Rolling Premium

為了更清楚觀察價值溢酬的時變特性，下圖展示滾動 5 年的年化價值溢酬。這張圖的橫軸是時間，縱軸是過去 5 年的年化 HML 報酬（%）。正值代表「過去五年便宜股勝過貴股」，負值代表反過來。

To more clearly observe the time-varying nature of the value premium, the chart below shows the rolling 5-year annualized value premium. The x-axis is time, y-axis is the trailing 5-year annualized HML return (%). Positive means "cheap stocks beat expensive over the past five years," negative means the opposite.

關鍵觀察：(1) 滾動溢酬在零軸上下劇烈擺盪，說明價值溢酬極不穩定——在某些 5 年期間，便宜股大幅勝過；在其他時期，貴股反而表現更好。(2) 2015 年後的多數時段，滾動溢酬落在零附近或負值區域，顯示近年來價值策略表現疲弱。(3) 這種高度波動意味著，即使價值溢酬的長期平均為正，投資人仍需承受漫長的「落後期」——如果你在 2015 年開始執行價值策略，可能已經等了近 10 年還沒看到報酬。

這對 H2（溢酬是否衰減）提供了視覺證據：滾動溢酬確實呈現下降趨勢。但我們需要結構性斷點檢定來確認這是「趨勢性衰減」還是「隨機波動」。

Key observations: (1) The rolling premium oscillates dramatically around zero, indicating extreme instability — in some 5-year windows, cheap stocks vastly outperform; in others, expensive stocks do better. (2) For most post-2015 periods, the rolling premium hovers near zero or in negative territory, showing recent weakness. (3) This high volatility means that even if the long-term average premium is positive, investors must endure lengthy underperformance periods — if you started a value strategy in 2015, you may have waited nearly 10 years without payoff.

This provides visual evidence for H2 (premium decay): the rolling premium does show a declining trend. But we need structural break tests to confirm whether this is "trend decay" or "random fluctuation."

3.4 價值陷阱分析3.4 Value Trap Analysis

所謂「價值陷阱」，是指那些看起來便宜（低 PE/PB）但實際上表現很差的股票。就像在二手市場看到一台「便宜」的車，買了才發現引擎有問題——表面的低價其實反映了隱藏的缺陷。下圖量化了各指標的價值陷阱發生率。

"Value traps" are stocks that appear cheap (low PE/PB) but actually perform poorly. Like finding a 'cheap' car at a used lot only to discover engine problems — the low price actually reflects hidden defects. The chart below quantifies value trap rates for each metric.

關鍵觀察：價值陷阱分析顯示，平均有 51% 的月份，最便宜的股票反而落後市場。這個數字遠非微不足道——如果你一年有 {avg_trap*100:.0f}% 的時間在虧錢，長期報酬就會被嚴重拖累。「嚴重陷阱率」（深紅色部分）更令人擔憂：在相當比例的月份中，最便宜的股票不僅落後市場，而且輸幅超過 5%。

這直接對應 H3 的檢驗：價值陷阱比例是否超過 20%。如果超過，投資人就不能「閉著眼睛買便宜股」，必須搭配品質篩選（例如 Novy-Marx 的獲利能力因子）來過濾掉那些「便宜但品質差」的陷阱股。

Key observations: Value trap analysis shows that in 51% of months, the cheapest stocks actually underperform the market. This is far from trivial — if you are losing money {avg_trap*100:.0f}% of the time, long-term returns suffer significantly. The "severe trap rate" (dark red) is even more concerning: in a substantial proportion of months, the cheapest stocks not only underperform but by more than 5%.

This directly addresses H3: whether the value trap rate exceeds 20%. If so, investors cannot "blindly buy cheap stocks" and must combine quality screening (e.g., Novy-Marx profitability factor) to filter out "cheap but poor quality" trap stocks.

3.5 風險指標比較3.5 Risk Metrics Comparison

選股不能只看報酬，還要考慮風險。下圖比較各 quintile 的三個關鍵風險指標：Sharpe Ratio（風險調整後報酬）、最大回檔（歷史上最大的虧損幅度）和年化波動率（報酬的不穩定程度）。

Stock selection cannot focus only on returns — risk must also be considered. The chart below compares three key risk metrics across quintiles: Sharpe Ratio (risk-adjusted return), maximum drawdown (largest historical loss), and annualized volatility (return instability).

關鍵觀察：理想情況下，Q1（最便宜）應該有最高的 Sharpe Ratio、最小的最大回檔、最低的波動率。但實際結果可能並不完全符合這個預期——便宜股的波動率有時反而更高，因為它們往往是陷入困境的公司。這說明價值溢酬（如果存在的話）部分可能是對「承擔更高風險」的補償，而非無風險的免費午餐。

Key observations: Ideally, Q1 (cheapest) should have the highest Sharpe, smallest drawdown, and lowest volatility. But actual results may not fully match this expectation — cheap stocks sometimes have higher volatility because they tend to be troubled companies. This suggests the value premium (if it exists) may partly be compensation for "bearing higher risk" rather than a risk-free lunch.

3.6 複合因子效果3.6 Composite Factor Performance

Greenblatt (2006) 的「魔法公式」啟發了一個重要問題：結合多個價值指標，是否比單一指標更有效？下圖比較各種因子排序方式的 Sharpe Ratio。

Greenblatt (2006) "Magic Formula" inspired an important question: does combining multiple value metrics outperform any single metric? The chart below compares Sharpe Ratios across different factor sorting methods.

關鍵觀察：複合因子（結合 PE、PB、殖利率三個指標）的 Sharpe Ratio 通常略高於單一因子，但差距並不懸殊。這表示結合多個指標確實有幫助，但不是「魔法」——它稍微改善了風險調整後報酬，但無法從根本上解決價值溢酬衰減的問題。對一般投資人而言，使用複合因子意味著更複雜的選股流程和更頻繁的調倉，邊際收益是否足以覆蓋這些額外成本和精力，值得深思。

Key observations: The composite factor (combining PE, PB, and yield) typically shows a slightly higher Sharpe Ratio than single factors, but the margin is not dramatic. Combining metrics does help, but it is not "magic" — it modestly improves risk-adjusted returns but cannot fundamentally solve the value premium decay problem. For ordinary investors, using composite factors means more complex stock selection and more frequent rebalancing — whether the marginal benefit justifies these additional costs and effort deserves careful consideration.

3.7 美國 HML 歷史3.7 US HML History

為了將台灣結果放在全球脈絡中理解，我們觀察美國 Fama-French HML 因子近百年的表現。這是全世界最長的價值因子時序數據——如果連美國都出現價值溢酬衰減，那這就不僅是台灣的問題。

To contextualize Taiwan results globally, we examine the nearly century-long US Fama-French HML factor performance. This is the longest value factor time series in the world — if even the US shows value premium decay, this is not just a Taiwan issue.

關鍵觀察：Fama-French HML 的累積報酬在近百年間呈現明顯的「兩段式」走勢。2010 年之前（紅色虛線左側），HML 持續累積正報酬，對數尺度下呈現穩定的上升趨勢——這就是教科書上描述的「價值溢酬」。但 2010 年之後，HML 曲線明顯走平甚至下降，顯示價值因子在全球最大的股票市場也面臨困境。

可能的原因包括：(1) 量化基金的普及使因子套利變得過度擁擠；(2) 科技股（高 PE）的結構性主導地位改變了市場生態；(3) 傳統 PB 無法捕捉無形資產密集型企業（如 FAANG）的真正價值。

Key observations: Fama-French HML cumulative returns show a clear "two-phase" pattern over nearly a century. Pre-2010 (left of red dashed line), HML steadily accumulated positive returns with a stable uptrend on log scale — this is the textbook "value premium." But post-2010, the HML curve clearly flattened or declined, showing the value factor faces challenges even in the world's largest stock market.

Possible explanations: (1) proliferation of quant funds made factor arbitrage overcrowded; (2) structural dominance of tech stocks (high PE) changed market ecology; (3) traditional PB fails to capture the true value of intangible-asset-heavy companies (e.g., FAANG).

3.8 台灣 vs 美國 HML 比較3.8 Taiwan vs US HML Comparison

下圖將台灣 HML 與美國 FF HML 疊在一起比較，兩者均以共同起始日為 1.0 進行標準化。觀察兩條曲線是同步移動還是各走各的，有助於判斷價值溢酬是全球性現象還是各市場獨立。

The chart below overlays Taiwan HML with US FF HML, both normalized to 1.0 at the common start date. Observing whether the curves move in sync or independently helps determine if the value premium is global or market-specific.

關鍵觀察：台灣與美國的 HML 走勢有一定的相似性（尤其是近年來的衰減趨勢），但並非完全同步。這表示價值溢酬的衰減可能有全球性驅動因素（如科技股崛起、量化套利普及），但各市場也有其獨特的結構性因素（台灣的半導體產業集中度、市場規模差異等）。

Key observations: Taiwan and US HML trends show some similarity (especially the recent decay trend), but are not perfectly synchronized. This suggests value premium decay may have global drivers (tech stock rise, quant arbitrage), but each market also has unique structural factors (Taiwan semiconductor concentration, market size differences, etc.).

3.10 年度 HML 分佈3.10 Annual HML Distribution

年度 HML 報酬的分佈圖顯示價值溢酬在不同年份的變異程度。如果分佈集中在正值區域，代表價值策略在大多數年份都能獲利；如果分佈橫跨正負兩側，代表價值策略的結果高度不確定。

The annual HML return distribution shows the variability of value premium across years. If the distribution clusters in positive territory, value strategy profits in most years; if it spans both positive and negative, results are highly uncertain.

關鍵觀察：年度 HML 分佈的零軸（黑色垂直線）將圖分為兩半。右半部（正值）代表價值股勝過成長股的年份，左半部代表成長股勝出的年份。分佈的中心是否明顯偏右，以及左尾有多長，是評估價值策略風險的重要依據。如果大量年份落在零軸左側，投資人必須有心理準備可能連續多年落後大盤。

Key observations: The zero line (black vertical) divides the distribution into two halves. Right half (positive) represents years when value beat growth; left half means growth won. Whether the center is clearly right-shifted, and how long the left tail extends, are crucial for assessing value strategy risk. If many years fall on the left side, investors must be prepared for potentially consecutive years of underperformance.
