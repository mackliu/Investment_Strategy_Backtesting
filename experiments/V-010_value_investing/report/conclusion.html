結論Conclusion

核心結論Core Conclusions

本研究以台灣（2010-2025）與美國（1926-2025）雙市場數據，系統性地檢驗「低 PE/PB 必勝」的投資信條。透過 quintile 分組分析、HML 時序檢驗、結構性斷點檢定與價值陷阱量化，我們得出以下結論：

  1. 價值溢酬存在但衰減中：台灣市場的 HML 在部分時期為正值，顯示歷史上確實存在價值溢酬。然而，2015 年後溢酬顯著減弱，與美國 FF HML 的趨勢一致。全球化的因子套利和科技股崛起可能是主要原因。
  2. 價值陷阱是主要風險：平均 51% 的月份中，最便宜的股票反而跑輸市場。低 PE/PB 不等於被低估——投資人必須搭配品質篩選（ROE、營收成長）來過濾「假便宜」。
  3. 複合因子較為穩健：結合 PE、PB 和殖利率三個指標的複合排名，其 Sharpe Ratio 略高於單一因子，但改善幅度有限——不是萬靈丹。
  4. 全球趨勢一致：台灣與美國的價值因子同步衰減，這不是台灣市場的特例，而是全球性的結構變化。

This study systematically tested the "low PE/PB always wins" investment belief using dual-market data from Taiwan (2010-2025) and the US (1926-2025). Through quintile portfolio analysis, HML time series testing, structural break tests, and value trap quantification, we reach the following conclusions:

  1. Value premium exists but is decaying: Taiwan HML is positive in some periods, confirming historical existence. However, post-2015 the premium weakened significantly, consistent with US FF HML trends. Global factor arbitrage crowding and tech stock dominance are likely causes.
  2. Value traps are the main risk: In 51% of months, the cheapest stocks actually underperform. Low PE/PB does not mean undervalued — investors must combine with quality screening (ROE, revenue growth) to filter "false cheap."
  3. Composite is more robust: Combining PE, PB, and yield produces a slightly higher Sharpe Ratio than single factors, but the improvement is modest — not a silver bullet.
  4. Global trend is consistent: Taiwan and US value factors decay in parallel. This is not a Taiwan anomaly but a global structural change.

總結：「低 PE/PB 必勝」過於簡化。價值投資的核心思想（買入被低估的資產）仍然合理，但簡單的 PE/PB 排序已不足以捕捉價值溢酬。現代價值投資需要更精細的指標、更嚴格的品質篩選、以及更長的持有耐心。投資人與其死守「低 PE/PB」的教條，不如擁抱更全面的分析框架。

Summary: "Low PE/PB always wins" is oversimplified. The core idea of value investing (buying undervalued assets) remains sound, but simple PE/PB sorting is insufficient. Modern value investing requires more sophisticated metrics, stricter quality screening, and longer holding patience. Investors are better served by embracing a comprehensive analytical framework than rigidly following the "low PE/PB" dogma.

研究限制總結Summary of Limitations

本研究受限於以下因素，讀者在引用結論時應注意：

  • 台灣數據期間僅 15 年（2010-2025），統計檢定力有限，不如美國近百年數據可靠
  • 30 檔大型股的標的池無法代表全市場，中小型股的價值效應可能不同
  • 存續偏差和前視偏差可能高估了策略表現
  • 未扣除交易成本的理論溢酬可能與實際可獲得的報酬有落差
  • 2010-2025 的全球科技股牛市可能誇大了價值溢酬衰減的程度

This study is subject to the following limitations that readers should note when citing conclusions:

  • Taiwan data spans only 15 years (2010-2025), limiting statistical power compared to nearly a century of US data
  • The 30 large-cap universe cannot represent the full market; small-cap value effects may differ
  • Survivorship and look-ahead biases may overstate strategy performance
  • Theoretical premiums excluding transaction costs may differ from actually achievable returns
  • The 2010-2025 global tech bull market may exaggerate the degree of value premium decay

未來研究方向Future Research Directions

  • 擴大標的池：將分析範圍擴展至全台上市櫃公司（排除流動性不足者），特別是中小型股，檢驗價值效應是否在中小型股中仍然有效。
  • 品質因子結合：將 Novy-Marx (2013) 的獲利能力因子、Greenblatt (2006) 的「魔法公式」等品質篩選條件納入，測試是否能有效降低價值陷阱風險。
  • 條件式價值投資：探討在特定市場條件下（如景氣循環谷底、市場恐慌期）才啟動價值策略是否能提高勝率——也就是「只在對的時候釣魚」。
  • 納入無形資產調整：按照 Arnott et al. (2021) 的建議，將研發支出、品牌價值等無形資產加回帳面價值，重新計算調整後的 PB 並檢驗其預測效果。
  • Expand universe: Extend analysis to all TWSE/OTC-listed companies (excluding illiquid ones), especially small/mid caps, to test whether value effects still work in smaller stocks.
  • Quality factor integration: Incorporate Novy-Marx (2013) profitability factor and Greenblatt (2006) 'Magic Formula' quality screens to test whether they effectively reduce value trap risk.
  • Conditional value investing: Explore whether activating value strategies only under specific market conditions (e.g., business cycle troughs, market panic) improves win rates — i.e., 'only fish when conditions are right.'
  • Intangible asset adjustment: Following Arnott et al. (2021), add R&D spending and brand value back to book value, recalculate adjusted PB, and test its predictive power.
