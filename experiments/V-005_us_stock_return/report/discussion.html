4. 討論4. Discussion

4.1 主要發現4.1 Key Findings

本研究的核心發現可歸納為四點：

  1. 「10%」基本正確但有誤導性：全期名目含息年化 9.2% 接近 10%， 但這個數字隱含了股息再投入（貢獻約 2%）和通膨（侵蝕約 2.3%）。 不含息的名目年化僅 4.6%。
  2. 實質購買力增長約 7% 而非 10%：扣除通膨後的真正購買力增長率為 6.9%。 用「10%」做退休規劃會高估約 30-40% 的實際購買力。
  3. 10% 不是「保證」而是「平均」：10 年滾動窗口中僅 39% 達到 10%， 最差的 10 年甚至是負報酬。入場時機對結果有決定性影響。
  4. 美國例外論：美股的亮眼表現在全球並非普遍現象，以美國推論全球股市存在嚴重的存活偏差。

Our core findings can be summarized in four points:

  1. "10%" is roughly correct but misleading: Full-period nominal total at 9.2% is close to 10%, but this includes dividend reinvestment (~2%) and is before inflation (which erodes ~2.3%). Price-only nominal is just 4.6%.
  2. Real purchasing power grows ~7%, not 10%: After inflation, actual purchasing power grows at 6.9%. Using "10%" for retirement planning overestimates real wealth by ~30-40%.
  3. 10% is an average, not a guarantee: Only 39% of 10-year rolling windows achieve 10%; the worst 10-year period shows negative returns. Entry timing has decisive impact.
  4. American exceptionalism: US stock performance is not universal globally; using the US to generalize about "stock markets" involves serious survivorship bias.

4.2 「10%」為何如此流行？4.2 Why Is '10%' So Popular?

這個數字的流行有幾個原因：

  • 簡潔好記：10% 是個整數，容易記憶和傳播
  • 選擇性起始年：以 1926 年（S&P 500 正式編制）為起點，剛好避開了 1871-1925 年報酬較低的時期
  • 混淆名目與實質：大多數人引用的是名目報酬，忽略通膨的影響
  • 後見之明偏差：回顧歷史時，美國的成功看似理所當然，但站在 1945 年的角度，很難預測美國會成為全球霸主

The popularity of this figure has several explanations:

  • Round number: 10% is a clean integer, easy to remember and spread
  • Selective starting year: Using 1926 (S&P 500 inception) conveniently excludes the lower-return period of 1871-1925
  • Nominal-real confusion: Most citations use nominal returns, ignoring inflation
  • Hindsight bias: In retrospect, US success seems inevitable, but from a 1945 perspective, predicting US global dominance was far from obvious

4.3 注意事項與研究限制4.3 Caveats and Limitations

  • Shiller 數據為月平均價格，非月末收盤價，可能與實際投資報酬有微小差異
  • 早期數據（1871-1926）的品質不如現代，S&P 500 指數在 1957 年才正式擴展至 500 檔成分股
  • 國際比較中，日經 225 與 FTSE 100 為價格指數（不含股息），與 S&P 500 含息報酬的比較不完全公平
  • 匯率分析僅涵蓋 2004 年後約 20 年，可能無法完全反映長期匯率趨勢
  • 本研究未考慮交易成本、稅務（如海外所得稅）和基金管理費等實務因素
  • Shiller data uses monthly average prices, not month-end closes, potentially differing slightly from actual investment returns
  • Early data quality (1871-1926) is inferior to modern data; the S&P 500 only expanded to 500 constituents in 1957
  • International comparisons use price indices (excluding dividends) for Nikkei and FTSE, making the comparison with S&P 500 total return not entirely fair
  • FX analysis covers only ~20 years since 2004, potentially not reflecting long-term currency trends
  • This study does not account for transaction costs, taxes (e.g., overseas income tax), or fund management fees

4.4 投資啟示4.4 Investment Implications

保守型投資人：以 7% 的實質報酬做退休規劃，而非 10%。 長期持有美股確實有正報酬，但要有心理準備面對 10 年甚至更長的低回報期。

積極型投資人：「美股長期上漲」的論點在歷史上基本成立，但不代表任何 10 年都能獲得 10%。 台灣投資人還需額外考慮匯率風險，建議分散至不同國家市場以降低單一市場的集中風險。

Conservative investors: Use 7% real returns for retirement planning, not 10%. Long-term US equity investing does yield positive returns, but be prepared for decade-long periods of below-average performance.

Aggressive investors: The thesis that "US stocks rise long-term" is historically valid, but it doesn't mean every 10-year period delivers 10%. Taiwanese investors should also factor in currency risk and consider diversifying across markets to reduce concentration risk.
