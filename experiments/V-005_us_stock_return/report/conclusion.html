5. 結論5. Conclusion

5.1 核心結論5.1 Core Conclusions

  1. 部分正確：S&P 500 全期名目含息年化 9.2%，接近但未達 10%。 僅在戰後（1950+）起算時才超過 10%（11.3%）
  2. 嚴重高估實質報酬：扣除通膨後的實質年化為 6.9%， 「10%」高估了約 3.1 個百分點的實際購買力增長
  3. 並非穩定保證：10 年滾動窗口達到 10% 的機率僅 39%， 30 年也僅 47%
  4. 存活偏差顯著：美國是全球表現最佳的市場之一，以此推論「股市」長期報酬有嚴重偏差
  5. 台灣投資人需考慮匯率：匯率風險每年約侵蝕 0.5-1% 的報酬
  1. Partially correct: S&P 500 full-period nominal total return is 9.2%, close to but below 10%. Only starting post-war (1950+) does it exceed 10% (11.3%)
  2. Significantly overstates real returns: Real annualized return is 6.9%; "10%" overestimates actual purchasing power growth by ~3.1 percentage points
  3. Not a stable guarantee: Only 39% of 10-year and 47% of 30-year rolling windows achieve 10%
  4. Significant survivorship bias: The US is one of the best-performing markets globally; generalizing from it to "stock markets" involves serious bias
  5. FX risk for Taiwanese investors: Currency risk erodes approximately 0.5-1%/year of returns

5.2 研究限制5.2 Limitations

  • Shiller 數據至 2023 年 9 月，未涵蓋最新市場表現
  • 國際比較受限於指數類型差異（價格指數 vs 含息指數）
  • 匯率分析期間僅約 20 年，統計推論力有限
  • 未考慮交易成本、稅務和基金管理費
  • Shiller data ends September 2023, not covering the latest market performance
  • International comparisons limited by index type differences (price vs total return)
  • FX analysis covers only ~20 years, limiting statistical inference power
  • Does not account for transaction costs, taxes, or fund management fees

5.3 未來研究方向5.3 Future Research

  • 納入更多國家的含息指數進行公平比較（MSCI 系列指數）
  • 考慮 CAPE 估值水準對未來報酬的預測能力
  • 加入匯率避險成本分析（遠期外匯或貨幣避險 ETF）
  • 分析美股集中度風險（近年科技股佔比過高的影響）
  • Include more countries' total return indices for fair comparison (MSCI series)
  • Analyze CAPE valuation level's predictive power for future returns
  • Add currency hedging cost analysis (forwards or hedged ETFs)
  • Examine US market concentration risk (impact of tech sector dominance)
