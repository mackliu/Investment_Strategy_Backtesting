V-005：「美股長期年化報酬 10%」V-005: US Stock 10% Annual Return

研究問題：S&P 500 過去 150 年的長期年化報酬真的有 10% 嗎？ 扣除通膨後呢？台灣投資人要面對多大的匯率風險？這個數字是否因為「存活偏差」被高估了？
Research Question: Does the S&P 500 truly deliver 10% annualized returns over 150+ years? What about after inflation? How much FX risk do Taiwanese investors face? Is this figure inflated by survivorship bias?

摘要Abstract

本研究使用 Robert Shiller 的長期數據集（1871-2023，共 1829 個月）， 驗證「美股長期年化報酬 10%」這一廣為流傳的投資論點。

主要發現：

  • 全期名目含息年化報酬為 9.2%，表面上接近 10%， 但 10 年滾動報酬僅有 39% 的機率達到 10%
  • 扣除通膨後的實質年化報酬僅 6.9%， 「10%」嚴重高估了實際購買力增長
  • 不同年代差異巨大：1950 年代名目含息高達 19%， 2000 年代僅 -1%
  • 存活偏差明顯：同期日經 225 年化僅 2.6%（不含息）

This study uses Robert Shiller's long-term dataset (1871-2023, 1829 months) to verify the widely cited claim that US stocks deliver 10% annualized returns.

Key findings:

  • Full-period nominal total annualized return is 9.2%, seemingly close to 10%, but only 39% of 10-year rolling periods actually achieve 10%
  • Real (inflation-adjusted) annualized return is only 6.9%, meaning "10%" significantly overstates actual purchasing power growth
  • Huge variation across decades: 1950s nominal total reached 19%, while 2000s was only -1%
  • Clear survivorship bias: Nikkei 225 returned only 2.6% annually (price only) over the same period
