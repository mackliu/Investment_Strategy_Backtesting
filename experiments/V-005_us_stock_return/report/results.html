3.2 滾動年化報酬3.2 Rolling Annualized Returns

滾動分析揭示了「10%」這個數字的不穩定性。10 年滾動名目含息報酬的均值為 9.3%，但最差時為 -4.0%，最佳時為 21.2%。即使持有 30 年，仍有 53% 的機率無法達到 10%。

Rolling analysis reveals the instability of the '10%' figure. The 10-year rolling nominal total return averages 9.3%, but ranges from -4.0% to 21.2%. Even with 30-year holding, there's still a 53% chance of falling below 10%.

箱型圖清楚顯示：隨著持有期拉長，報酬離散度顯著收窄，但中位數始終低於 10% 基準線。

The boxplot clearly shows: as holding periods lengthen, return dispersion narrows significantly, but the median consistently stays below the 10% benchmark.

3.4 年代別差異3.4 Decade Variation

不同年代的報酬差異巨大。1920s、1950s、1980s、1990s、2010s 是「黃金年代」，名目含息均超過 13%。但 1930s（大蕭條）和 2000s（網路泡沫+金融海嘯）則幾乎歸零或為負。投資人的入場時機極為關鍵。

Returns vary enormously across decades. The 1920s, 1950s, 1980s, 1990s, and 2010s were 'golden decades' with nominal total returns exceeding 13%. But the 1930s (Great Depression) and 2000s (dot-com + GFC) were near-zero or negative. Entry timing matters enormously.

3.5 通膨侵蝕效果3.5 Inflation Erosion

通膨是隱形的「報酬稅」。全期名目含息年化 9.2% 扣除通膨後僅剩 6.9%，差距約 2.3% / 年。換言之，「10%」中有約 2-3% 只是補償購買力下降。

Inflation is an invisible 'return tax'. Full-period nominal total 9.2% minus inflation leaves only 6.9%, a gap of ~2.3%/year. In other words, roughly 2-3% of the '10%' merely compensates for purchasing power erosion.

Newey-West HAC 檢定結果：5 年以上滾動窗口的名目含息報酬均值均未顯著超過 10%。10 年以上窗口甚至在 10% 以下（t 值為負，p 值接近 1）。實質報酬在 10 年以上窗口也未能顯著超過 7%。結論：無法拒絕「美股長期報酬 <= 10%」的虛無假說。

Newey-West HAC tests: Nominal total return means for 5+ year rolling windows are not significantly above 10%. For 10+ year windows, they are actually below 10% (negative t-statistics, p-values near 1). Real returns for 10+ year windows also fail to exceed 7%. Conclusion: We cannot reject H0 that US long-term returns are ≤ 10%.

3.7 國際比較與存活偏差3.7 International Comparison & Survivorship Bias

1988 年以來的共同期間比較中，S&P 500 含息年化 10.8% 大幅領先日經 225 的 2.6%（不含息）和 FTSE 100 的 4.8%（不含息）。即使與同樣含息的 DAX（8.8%）相比，美國仍然遙遙領先。這說明「10%」並非股市的普遍規律，而是美國市場的特殊現象。

In the common period since 1988, S&P 500 total return at 10.8% far exceeds Nikkei 225's 2.6% (price only) and FTSE 100's 4.8% (price only). Even compared to total-return DAX (8.8%), the US leads significantly. This demonstrates '10%' is not a universal stock market law but a US-specific phenomenon.

3.8 台灣投資人匯率風險3.8 FX Risk for Taiwan Investors

2004 年以來，台幣計價報酬與美元計價報酬的差距取決於匯率走勢。短期（1 年）匯率波動可能貢獻正報酬，但中長期（5-10 年）匯率拖累約 0.5-1%/年。匯率風險是台灣投資人投資美股時不可忽視的因素。

Since 2004, the gap between TWD-denominated and USD-denominated returns depends on exchange rate movements. Short-term (1-year) FX fluctuations may add returns, but medium-to-long term (5-10 years) FX drag is approximately 0.5-1%/year. Currency risk is a significant factor for Taiwanese investors.
