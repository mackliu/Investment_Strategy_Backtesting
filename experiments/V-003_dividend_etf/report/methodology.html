研究方法Methodology

2.1 數據來源與品質2.1 Data Sources and Quality

本研究使用以下數據：

  • 0050 日線數據：5517 個交易日（2003-06-30 ~ 2025-11-25），含收盤價與還原價。數據來源為本地資料庫（經 V-001 完整驗證），並以 yfinance 交叉驗證。
  • 0056 日線數據：4403 個交易日（2007-12-26 ~ 2025-11-25），含收盤價與還原價。
  • 除權息明細：FinMind API（0050: 21 配息年, 0056: 16 配息年）
  • 台灣 CPI：主計處月度/年度資料（1981~2026）

所有數據經過缺漏值檢查、日報酬率異常值篩查（>10%）、交易日連續性檢查。0050 於 2025 年 6 月進行約 4:1 股票分割，本研究已針對此事件進行拆股調整（split_adj_close），確保跨期間比較的一致性。

This study uses the following data:

  • 0050 daily prices: 5517 trading days (2003-06-30 ~ 2025-11-25), including close and adjusted close. Source: local database (fully validated in V-001), cross-validated with yfinance.
  • 0056 daily prices: 4403 trading days (2007-12-26 ~ 2025-11-25), including close and adjusted close.
  • Dividend records: FinMind API (0050: 21 dividend years, 0056: 16 dividend years)
  • Taiwan CPI: DGBAS monthly/annual data (1981-2026)

All data underwent missing value checks, daily return outlier screening (>10%), and trading day continuity checks. 0050 underwent an approximately 4:1 stock split in June 2025; this study applies split adjustment (split_adj_close) to ensure consistent cross-period comparisons.

2.2 分析維度2.2 Analysis Dimensions

本研究從以下七個維度分析股息品質，涵蓋「收益性」、「穩定性」、「機制」與「實際應用」四大面向：

  1. 年度殖利率：年度配息 / 年初股價——最基本的收益性指標，直接回答「每投入 100 元能拿回多少現金」。
  2. 股息成長率：年度配息的 YoY 變化——衡量配息能否跟上通膨。若殖利率高但逐年下降，長期購買力將侵蝕。
  3. 股息穩定性：變異係數（CV）= 標準差 / 平均值——退休族需要穩定的現金流。CV 越低代表配息金額越可預測。
  4. 除息日效果：股價跌幅 vs 配息金額的比率（drop ratio）——直接檢驗「左手換右手」假說。若 ratio = 1.0，表示配息等於股價下跌。
  5. 填息分析：除息後股價回復到除息前水準的天數——雖然「填息」不代表真正賺到配息（總報酬才是重點），但填息速度反映市場對該 ETF 基本面的信心。
  6. 總報酬分解：價格報酬 vs 股息報酬的貢獻比——揭示報酬的結構組成。若一檔 ETF 的報酬幾乎全來自配息而非價格成長，其「成長」本質上是在回收自己的資本。
  7. 實質殖利率：名目殖利率 - 通膨率——對退休族最關鍵的指標，因為退休開支會隨通膨上升。實質殖利率為負代表配息的購買力正在縮水。

This study analyzes dividend quality across seven dimensions, covering four aspects: "profitability," "stability," "mechanism," and "practical application":

  1. Annual dividend yield: Annual dividends / Year-start price — the most basic profitability metric, directly answering "how much cash do you get back per NT$100 invested."
  2. Dividend growth rate: YoY change in annual dividends — measures whether dividends keep pace with inflation. High yield but declining year-over-year means purchasing power erodes over time.
  3. Dividend stability: Coefficient of variation (CV) = std / mean — retirees need predictable cash flow. Lower CV means more predictable dividend amounts.
  4. Ex-dividend day effect: Price drop / dividend ratio (drop ratio) — directly tests the "left hand to right hand" hypothesis. If ratio = 1.0, dividends exactly equal the price drop.
  5. Fill-gap analysis: Days for price to recover to pre-ex-dividend level — while "filling the gap" does not mean truly earning the dividend (total return is what matters), fill speed reflects market confidence in the ETF's fundamentals.
  6. Total return decomposition: Price return vs dividend return contribution — reveals return structure. If an ETF's return comes almost entirely from dividends rather than price growth, its "growth" is essentially recovering its own capital.
  7. Real dividend yield: Nominal yield - inflation rate — the most critical metric for retirees, since retirement expenses rise with inflation. Negative real yield means dividend purchasing power is shrinking.

2.3 統計方法2.3 Statistical Methods

  • 配對 t 檢定 + Wilcoxon：比較 0050 vs 0056 殖利率差異。為什麼配對？因為同一年份的市場環境相同（如 2020 年疫情衝擊同時影響兩檔 ETF），年份間的觀測具相關性。配對設計移除了年份效應，使比較更精確。Wilcoxon 符號秩檢定作為無母數替代方案，不假設常態分佈。
  • 單樣本 t 檢定：檢驗 drop ratio 是否等於 1.0。為什麼？若 drop ratio 顯著不等於 1.0，表示除息日有額外的市場效應（如稅盾效應或投資人情緒），配息並非「完美的左手換右手」。
  • Fisher's exact test：比較填息率（二元變數：填 or 不填）。為什麼用 Fisher's 而非卡方？因為樣本數較小（各約 15-20 筆除息事件），Fisher's exact test 在小樣本下更可靠。
  • Mann-Whitney U：比較填息天數。為什麼不用 t 檢定？填息天數呈明顯右偏（大部分在 30 天內填息，但少數需要數百天），違反常態假設。Mann-Whitney U 是無母數方法，對偏態分佈仍然有效。
  • Levene's test：比較股息變異程度。為什麼？退休族不僅關心平均殖利率，更關心配息的穩定性。Levene's 檢定比較兩組的變異數是否相等，對非常態數據具穩健性。
  • Bootstrap 信賴區間：10,000 次重抽樣，提供穩健的區間估計。為什麼？本研究的樣本量較小（約 15-17 年），傳統大樣本近似可能不準確。Bootstrap 不依賴分佈假設，適合小樣本情境。
  • Cohen's d：效果量指標（0.2 小、0.5 中、0.8 大、1.2 極大）。為什麼？殖利率差異即使在統計上顯著，如果效果量很小，投資人在實務上可能感受不到差異。只有當效果量夠大，結論才有經濟實質意義。
  • Paired t-test + Wilcoxon: Compare 0050 vs 0056 yields. Why paired? Same-year market conditions (e.g., 2020 COVID impact) affect both ETFs, creating correlated observations. Paired design removes the year effect for more precise comparison. Wilcoxon signed-rank test serves as a non-parametric alternative without normality assumption.
  • One-sample t-test: Test whether drop ratio equals 1.0. Why? If drop ratio significantly differs from 1.0, it suggests additional market effects on ex-dividend days (e.g., tax shield or investor sentiment), meaning dividends are not "perfectly left hand to right hand."
  • Fisher's exact test: Compare fill rates (binary: filled or not). Why Fisher's instead of chi-square? Small sample sizes (~15-20 ex-dividend events each) make Fisher's exact test more reliable than large-sample chi-square approximation.
  • Mann-Whitney U: Compare fill-gap days. Why not t-test? Fill-gap days are clearly right-skewed (most fill within 30 days, but some take hundreds), violating normality. Mann-Whitney U is non-parametric and valid for skewed distributions.
  • Levene's test: Compare dividend variability. Why? Retirees care about stability, not just average yield. Levene's test compares variance equality between groups and is robust to non-normality.
  • Bootstrap CI: 10,000 resamples for robust interval estimation. Why? With small sample sizes (~15-17 years), traditional large-sample approximations may be inaccurate. Bootstrap is distribution-free, suitable for small samples.
  • Cohen's d: Effect size metric (0.2 small, 0.5 medium, 0.8 large, 1.2 very large). Why? A yield difference, even if statistically significant, may be imperceptible in practice if the effect size is tiny. Only sufficiently large effect sizes carry economic substance.

2.4 退休模擬設計2.4 Retirement Simulation Design

投入 1,000,000 元，比較兩種策略：

  • 策略 A（0056 領息）：持有 0056，每年領取股息作為現金流，不賣出任何持股
  • 策略 B（0050 賣股）：持有 0050，每年賣出等同 0056 年均配息金額的股票

比較指標：累計現金收入 + 期末剩餘市值。為測試穩健性，對多個起始年份（2008~2015）各模擬 10 年。

Starting with NT$1,000,000, we compare two strategies:

  • Strategy A (0056 dividends): Hold 0056, collect annual dividends as cash flow, never sell shares
  • Strategy B (0050 sell): Hold 0050, sell shares equivalent to 0056's average annual dividend

Comparison metric: cumulative cash received + remaining portfolio value. For robustness, we simulate 10 years from multiple start years (2008-2015).
