實證結果Results

3.1 殖利率比較3.1 Dividend Yield Comparison

0056 的平均殖利率（6.91%）顯著高於 0050（3.43%），差異約 3.48 個百分點。配對 t 檢定結果高度顯著（p<0.001），效果量 Cohen's d = 1.34（屬極大效果），Bootstrap 95% CI 不包含 0。0056 的殖利率優勢在每個分析年度都成立。

0056's mean yield (6.91%) is significantly higher than 0050's (3.43%), a difference of ~3.48 percentage points. The paired t-test is highly significant (p<0.001), with Cohen's d = 1.34 (very large effect). The Bootstrap 95% CI excludes 0. 0056's yield advantage holds in every analysis year.

從年度配息金額來看，0050 平均年配 2.62 元，0056 平均年配 1.82 元。但 0050 的股價較高，因此殖利率較低。值得注意的是 0056 於 2024 年起改為季配息，配息次數增加但每次金額降低。

In absolute terms, 0050 averages NT$2.62 per share annually, while 0056 averages NT$1.82. However, 0050's higher share price results in a lower yield. Note that 0056 switched to quarterly dividends starting 2024, increasing frequency but decreasing per-distribution amounts.

3.2 除息日效果（左手換右手？）3.2 Ex-Dividend Day Effect (Left Hand to Right Hand?)

除息日股價跌幅與配息金額之比（drop ratio）在 0050 為 0.74、0056 為 1.00。兩者在統計上均不顯著偏離 1.0（0050 p=0.11, 0056 p=0.97）。這表示除息日的股價調整大致等於配息金額，即「你領到的股息幾乎等於股價的下跌」。

這個結果支持「配息是左手換右手」的觀點：配息並非「額外」收入，而是從持有的資產中提取出來的。對退休族而言，領息和賣股在經濟上是等價的。

The ex-dividend day price drop / dividend ratio (drop ratio) is 0.74 for 0050 and 1.00 for 0056. Neither is statistically significantly different from 1.0 (0050 p=0.11, 0056 p=0.97). This means the price adjustment on ex-dividend day approximately equals the dividend amount - 'what you receive in dividends is roughly what you lose in share price.'

This supports the "left hand to right hand" view: dividends are not "extra" income but rather extracted from your existing assets. For retirees, collecting dividends and selling shares are economically equivalent.

3.3 填息分析3.3 Fill-Gap Analysis

0050 的填息率為 96.7%（29/30），中位數填息天數 5 天；0056 的填息率為 87.0%（20/23），中位數填息天數 34 天。

填息率在 Fisher's exact test 中未達顯著差異（p=0.31），但填息速度有顯著差異：0050 填息顯著快於 0056（Mann-Whitney U p=0.003）。0050 中位數僅需 5 天即可填息，而 0056 需要 34 天。

填息只是回到除息前的價格水準，並不代表「賺到了配息」。真正重要的是含息總報酬（adj_close），而非是否填息。

0050's fill rate is 96.7% (29/30), median fill days 5; 0056's fill rate is 87.0% (20/23), median fill days 34.

Fill rates show no significant difference (Fisher's p=0.31), but fill speed differs significantly: 0050 fills gaps much faster than 0056 (Mann-Whitney U p=0.003). 0050 takes a median of 5 days to fill, while 0056 takes 34 days.

Filling the gap merely means returning to the pre-ex-dividend price level; it does not mean you "earned" the dividend. What truly matters is total return (adj_close), not whether the gap was filled.

3.4 總報酬分解3.4 Total Return Decomposition

在 2008~2025 共同期間，0050 總報酬 610.8%，其中價格報酬 305.3%、股息報酬 305.5%（股息貢獻 50%）。0056 總報酬 284.2%，其中價格報酬 102.6%、股息報酬 181.5%（股息貢獻 64%）。

這說明 0050 的報酬主要來自價格成長（市值型 ETF 的本質），而 0056 高度依賴配息。當一檔 ETF 的報酬主要來自配息時，它實質上是在「自己提領自己的資產」。

During 2008-2025, 0050's total return was 610.8%, split into 305.3% price return and 305.5% dividend return (dividend contribution: 50%). 0056's total return was 284.2%, with 102.6% price and 181.5% dividend (64% contribution).

This illustrates that 0050's returns come primarily from price appreciation (the essence of a market-cap ETF), while 0056 relies heavily on dividends. When an ETF's returns primarily come from dividends, it is essentially "withdrawing from its own assets."

3.5 實質殖利率3.5 Real Dividend Yield

扣除通膨後，0050 實質殖利率平均 2.22%，0056 為 5.73%。兩者均維持正值，表示配息至少能跟上通膨。

After inflation adjustment, 0050's real yield averages 2.22%, 0056's 5.73%. Both remain positive, indicating dividends at least keep pace with inflation.

3.6 退休模擬3.6 Retirement Simulation

投入 100 萬元，從 2008 年開始模擬 15 年：

  • 策略 A（0056 領息）：累計配息 751,456 元 + 期末市值 986,408 元 = 1,737,864 元
  • 策略 B（0050 賣股）：累計賣出 751,456 元 + 期末市值 589,627 元 = 1,341,083 元

在此基礎情境中，0056 領息策略的合計價值較高。但多起始年份穩健性分析（2008~2015 各 10 年）中，0050 賣股策略勝出率為 38%，差異在統計上不顯著。

With NT$1M starting from 2008, simulated for 15 years:

  • Strategy A (0056 dividends): Cumulative dividends NT$751,456 + final portfolio NT$986,408 = NT$1,737,864
  • Strategy B (0050 sell): Cumulative sales NT$751,456 + final portfolio NT$589,627 = NT$1,341,083

In this base scenario, 0056 dividend strategy has higher total value. However, in multi-start robustness analysis (2008-2015, 10 years each), 0050 sell strategy wins in 38% of scenarios, and the difference is not statistically significant.

3.7 稅後報酬3.7 After-Tax Returns

在三種稅務情境下的年化報酬比較：

  • 免稅：0050 11.54% vs 0056 5.24%
  • 退休族（5% 合併、8.5% 抵減、NHI 2.11%）：0050 11.48% vs 0056 5.18%
  • 高所得（28% 分離、NHI 2.11%）：0050 10.66% vs 0056 4.38%

由於配息金額越高，稅負越重，0056 在高稅率情境下的劣勢更加明顯。高配息策略對高所得者尤其不利。

Annualized returns across three tax scenarios:

  • Tax-free: 0050 11.54% vs 0056 5.24%
  • Retiree (5% combined, 8.5% credit, NHI 2.11%): 0050 11.48% vs 0056 5.18%
  • High income (28% separate, NHI 2.11%): 0050 10.66% vs 0056 4.38%

Since higher dividends incur higher taxes, 0056's disadvantage becomes more pronounced under high tax rates. High-dividend strategies are particularly unfavorable for high-income earners.

3.8 子期間分析：2017 年前後的差異3.8 Sub-Period Analysis: Pre vs Post 2017

2017 年後台積電權重飆升，台股結構性轉變。將分析期間以 2017 年為界拆分：

  • 2008-2016（科技股暴漲前）：0050 年化 5.21% vs 0056 年化 3.40%，差距僅 1.81 個百分點
  • 2017-2025（科技股暴漲後）：0050 年化 18.41% vs 0056 年化 12.45%，差距擴大至 5.96 個百分點

這顯示 0050 的報酬優勢高度依賴 2017 年後的科技股行情。在 2008-2016 兩者表現相近的環境下，退休模擬中 0056 領息策略實際上勝出（合計 1,270,680 vs 1,074,719）。這表明「0050 一定比 0056 好」的結論不適用於所有市場制度。

The surge in TSMC's weight after 2017 structurally transformed Taiwan's stock market. Splitting the analysis at 2017:

  • 2008-2016 (pre tech boom): 0050 annualized 5.21% vs 0056 3.40%, gap only 1.81 pp
  • 2017-2025 (post tech boom): 0050 annualized 18.41% vs 0056 12.45%, gap widened to 5.96 pp

This shows 0050's return advantage is highly dependent on post-2017 tech sector performance. In the 2008-2016 environment when both performed similarly, 0056 dividend strategy actually wins in retirement simulation (1,270,680 vs 1,074,719). The conclusion that "0050 always beats 0056" does not hold across all market regimes.

3.9 「左手換右手」的長期驗證3.9 "Left Hand to Right Hand" Long-Term Verification

除息日 drop ratio ~= 1.0 表明短期內配息確實等於股價下跌。但如果配息「真的」是左手換右手（如龐氏結構），那 0056 的淨值應持續歸零。實際情況如何？

從 2008 年開始追蹤：

  • 初始價格：25.75 元
  • 累計配息：29.05 元/股（已超過初始股價的 113%）
  • 如果是龐氏（本金侵蝕）：淨值應歸零（25.75 - 29.05 = 0.00）
  • 0056 實際收盤：35.15 元（成長 36.5%）
  • 含息總價值：64.20 元（成長 149%）

結論：0056 在 17 年間累計配出的金額已超過初始股價，但股價不僅沒有歸零，反而成長了。這證明 ETF 配的是「企業盈餘」而非「投資人的本金」。除息日的短期 drop ratio ~= 1.0 是機械性效果，不等於「配息沒有價值」。真正的左手換右手是本金逐漸耗盡（如圖中灰色虛線），與 0056 實際走勢截然不同。

The ex-dividend day drop ratio ~= 1.0 shows that short-term, dividends do equal price drops. But if dividends were truly 'left hand to right hand' (Ponzi structure), 0056's NAV should decline toward zero. What actually happened?

Tracking from 2008:

  • Initial price: 25.75
  • Cumulative dividends: 29.05 per share (113% of initial price)
  • If Ponzi (principal erosion): NAV should be 0.00
  • 0056 actual close: 35.15 (grew 36.5%)
  • Close + dividends: 64.20 (grew 149%)

Conclusion: Over 17 years, 0056 distributed more than its initial price in cumulative dividends, yet the price did not decline to zero — it actually grew. This proves ETF dividends come from corporate earnings, not investor principal. The short-term drop ratio ~= 1.0 is a mechanical effect and does NOT mean "dividends have no value." True "left hand to right hand" means principal gradually depletes to zero (gray dashed line in chart), which is fundamentally different from 0056's actual trajectory.
