V-003：高股息 ETF 是退休首選？V-003: Are High-Dividend ETFs the Best Choice for Retirement?

研究問題：高股息 ETF（如 0056）真的是退休族的最佳選擇嗎？靠配息能穩定生活嗎？與市值型 ETF（0050）賣股策略相比，哪個更有利？

Research Question: Are high-dividend ETFs (e.g., 0056) truly the best choice for retirees? Can dividends alone sustain living expenses? How does this compare to selling shares of a market-cap ETF (0050)?

摘要Abstract

本研究以 0050（元大台灣50）與 0056（元大高股息）為標的，從七個維度分析高股息 ETF 的股息品質與退休適用性。分析期間為 2008~2025 年（共同上市期間約 17.9 年）。

核心發現：0056 殖利率顯著高於 0050（平均 6.9% vs 3.4%，p<0.001，Cohen's d=1.34），但總報酬遠低於 0050（284% vs 611%）。0050 的股息貢獻僅佔總報酬 50%，而 0056 高達 64%，反映出 0056 的報酬主要來自配息而非價格成長。除息日股價跌幅與配息金額之比（drop ratio）在兩檔 ETF 中均接近 1.0（0050: 0.74, 0056: 1.00），統計上不顯著偏離 1.0，支持「配息為左手換右手」的觀點。

退休模擬顯示：投入 100 萬元，0056 領息策略 15 年後合計約 1,737,864 元，0050 賣股策略合計約 1,341,083 元。多起始年份穩健性分析中，0050 賣股策略在 38% 的情境下勝出。

This study analyzes 0050 (Yuanta Taiwan 50) and 0056 (Yuanta High Dividend) across seven dividend quality dimensions, covering 2008-2025 (~17.9 years of common listing).

Key findings: 0056's dividend yield is significantly higher than 0050's (mean 6.9% vs 3.4%, p<0.001, Cohen's d=1.34), but total return is substantially lower (284% vs 611%). Dividends contribute only 50% of 0050's total return but 64% of 0056's, reflecting 0056's return primarily comes from dividends rather than price appreciation. The ex-dividend day price drop ratio is near 1.0 for both ETFs (0050: 0.74, 0056: 1.00), not significantly different from 1.0, supporting the 'left hand to right hand' view of dividends.

Retirement simulation: Starting with NT$1M, 0056 dividend strategy yields approximately NT$1,737,864 after 15 years, while 0050 sell strategy yields NT$1,341,083. In multi-start robustness analysis, 0050 wins in 38% of scenarios.

上圖呈現 0050 與 0056 自共同上市以來的含息累積報酬走勢（以 100 為基準）。0050 含息成長至約 711，0056 成長至約 384。儘管 0056 殖利率較高，但總報酬遠不及 0050，這是因為 0050 的價格成長遠超 0056。

The chart shows cumulative total returns (base=100) since common listing. 0050 grew to ~711, 0056 to ~384. Despite 0056's higher yield, its total return is far lower than 0050's, because 0050's price appreciation greatly exceeds 0056's.
