V-012：股債 60/40 永遠有效？V-012: Does the 60/40 Portfolio Always Work?

研究方法Methodology

2.1 數據來源與品質2.1 Data Sources & Quality

本研究的核心數據來源是 Robert Shiller 教授維護的 CAPE 數據集，涵蓋 1871 年至今的美國股市月報酬率、10 年期國庫券殖利率、CPI 通膨指數等。這是全球歷史最悠久、最常被引用的金融數據集之一，被學術界廣泛用於長期資產配置研究（Shiller, 2000; Siegel, 2014）。本研究使用 1926 年起共 1170 個月（97.5 年）的數據進行核心分析，涵蓋大蕭條、二戰、冷戰、石油危機、網路泡沫、金融海嘯、COVID-19 及 2022 升息衝擊等所有重大經濟事件。

Shiller 數據的一項限制是：股票報酬來自 S&P 綜合指數（非可直接投資的標的），且早期交易成本較高、流動性較低，可能高估實際可獲得的報酬。然而，這些偏誤在各配置間大致對稱，不影響配置之間的比較結論。此外，黃金數據來自 GLD ETF（2004 年上市），樣本期間僅約 20 年，擴展配置的結論需謹慎解讀。

Our core data comes from Robert Shiller's CAPE dataset, covering US stock returns, 10-year Treasury yields, and CPI since 1871. This is one of the world's longest and most-cited financial datasets, widely used in long-term asset allocation research (Shiller, 2000; Siegel, 2014). We use 1170 months (97.5 years) from 1926 for core analysis, spanning the Great Depression, WWII, Cold War, oil crises, dot-com bubble, GFC, COVID-19, and 2022 rate shock.

One limitation: Shiller stock returns are from the S&P Composite (not a directly investable instrument), and early-period transaction costs were higher with lower liquidity, potentially overstating achievable returns. However, these biases are roughly symmetric across allocations, preserving relative comparisons. Gold data (GLD ETF, listed 2004) covers only ~20 years; extended allocation conclusions require caution.

2.2 債券報酬模型2.2 Bond Return Model

由於早期沒有債券指數，本研究使用 Shiller 數據中的 GS10（10 年期國庫券殖利率）推算債券月報酬。假設存續期間（duration）為 7.5 年：

月報酬 = 前月殖利率/12 - 7.5 x (當月殖利率 - 前月殖利率)

此方法將債券報酬分為兩部分：(1) 票息收入（前月殖利率/12）提供穩定的正報酬；(2) 價格變動（存續期間 x 殖利率變動）反映利率風險。當利率下降時，債券價格上漲（正的價格報酬）；利率上升時反之。此方法為學術界廣泛使用的債券報酬近似法（Campbell & Viceira, 2002），實證上與 Bloomberg 債券指數在重疊期間的相關係數超過 0.95。

為什麼選擇 7.5 年存續期間？美國 10 年期國庫券的修正存續期間（modified duration）約在 7-8 年之間。7.5 年是常用的折衷值，介於新發行券（~9 年）和已流通券（~6 年）之間。穩健性分析中，我們也測試了 5 年和 10 年的假設，結論不變。

Since early bond indices do not exist, we estimate bond monthly returns from Shiller's GS10 (10-year Treasury yield) assuming duration = 7.5 years:

Monthly return = prev_yield/12 - 7.5 x (yield - prev_yield)

This decomposes bond returns into: (1) coupon income (prev_yield/12) providing steady positive returns; (2) price change (duration x yield change) reflecting interest rate risk. When rates fall, bond prices rise (positive price return); when rates rise, prices fall. This is a widely-used academic approximation (Campbell & Viceira, 2002), with empirical correlation exceeding 0.95 against Bloomberg indices in overlapping periods.

Why 7.5 years duration? The modified duration of the US 10-year Treasury is approximately 7-8 years. 7.5 years is a common compromise between newly issued (~9 years) and seasoned (~6 years) bonds. Robustness checks with 5-year and 10-year assumptions yield similar conclusions.

2.3 投資組合建構2.3 Portfolio Construction

核心配置（5 種）：以 Shiller 全期間數據（1926 年起）進行回測。從 100% 股票到 100% 債券，每 20% 為一級距，涵蓋 100/0、80/20、60/40、40/60、0/100 五種配置。此外，效率前緣分析以每 5% 為一級距，計算 21 個配置的風險-報酬特性。

擴展配置（2 種）：使用 GLD 上市後的 ETF 時代數據。(1) 永久投資組合（Permanent Portfolio）：25% 股票 + 25% 長債 + 25% 黃金 + 25% 現金，由 Harry Browne 提出，設計為「任何經濟環境都能存活」的配置。(2) 四資產配置：60% 股票 + 20% 債券 + 10% 黃金 + 10% 現金，是保留 60/40 核心精神但加入黃金的「後 60/40」方案。

再平衡機制：比較三種再平衡策略的影響——(1) 年度再平衡：每年 1 月回到目標配置；(2) 季度再平衡：每季回到目標配置；(3) 不再平衡（Buy & Hold Drift）：初始投入後任其漂移。再平衡時不考慮交易成本（因月度數據中單次再平衡的成本影響極小）。

Core Allocations (5): Backtested using full Shiller data from 1926. From 100% stock to 100% bond in 20% steps: 100/0, 80/20, 60/40, 40/60, 0/100. The efficient frontier analysis uses 5% steps across 21 allocations.

Extended Allocations (2): Uses ETF-era data post-GLD listing. (1) Permanent Portfolio: 25% stock + 25% long bond + 25% gold + 25% cash, proposed by Harry Browne to survive any economic environment. (2) Four-Asset: 60% stock + 20% bond + 10% gold + 10% cash, a "post-60/40" allocation preserving the 60/40 spirit with added gold.

Rebalancing: We compare three rebalancing strategies: (1) Annual: rebalance to target every January; (2) Quarterly: rebalance every quarter; (3) No rebalancing (Buy & Hold Drift): initial allocation drifts freely. Transaction costs are excluded (impact is minimal at monthly frequency).

2.4 利率環境劃分2.4 Interest Rate Regimes

本研究將 1926 年以來的歷史劃分為四個利率環境，依據美國 10 年期國庫券殖利率走勢和總經特徵。這種分層分析的目的是揭示 60/40 在不同環境下的真實表現差異——整體平均數字可能掩蓋了環境間的巨大落差。

We divide post-1926 history into four interest rate regimes based on 10-year Treasury yield trends and macroeconomic characteristics. This stratified analysis reveals 60/40's true performance variation — overall averages may mask enormous differences across regimes.

環境期間特徵對 60/40 的影響
戰後復甦1926-1964利率波動小，經濟復甦，戰後重建股債相關性低，分散效果普通
高通膨1965-1981石油危機，利率從 4% 飆升至 15%股債齊跌，60/40 保護失效
債券牛市1982-2020利率從 15% 持續下降至 0%，38 年大趨勢黃金時代，股債負相關，分散效果最強
升息週期2022-2023聯準會從 0% 升至 5%+ 對抗通膨股債齊跌重現，60/40 面臨壓力測試
RegimePeriodCharacteristicsImpact on 60/40
Post-War1926-1964Low rate volatility, post-war recoveryLow correlation, moderate diversification

為什麼這樣劃分？利率環境是決定股債相關性的關鍵因素。在利率上升期，債券價格下跌，同時升息也壓縮股票估值（提高折現率），導致股債正相關，削弱分散效果。反之，在利率下降期，債券價格上漲，經濟衰退又推動「逃向品質」（flight to quality），使股債呈現負相關，強化分散效果。理解這個機制是正確運用 60/40 的關鍵。

Why this classification? The interest rate environment is the key driver of stock-bond correlation. During rising rates, bond prices fall while higher discount rates also compress equity valuations, leading to positive stock-bond correlation and weakened diversification. During falling rates, bond prices rise and recessions trigger "flight to quality," creating negative correlation and strengthening diversification. Understanding this mechanism is key to properly applying 60/40.

2.5 統計方法2.5 Statistical Methods

由於金融時間序列具有自相關、厚尾、波動群聚等特性，傳統的 t 檢定和常態分布假設往往不適用。本研究選用的統計方法針對這些問題進行了修正：

Financial time series exhibit autocorrelation, fat tails, and volatility clustering, making traditional t-tests and normality assumptions inappropriate. Our statistical methods specifically address these issues:

方法用於假說為何選用
Block BootstrapH1（環境差異）、H3（60/40 vs 股票）保留月度自相關結構，無需常態假設。Block size = 12 個月，重抽 10,000 次。
Fisher z-transformH2（相關性結構變化）專門用於比較兩個相關係數是否顯著不同，適用於不同樣本量。
Paired t-testH4（再平衡效果）再平衡與否使用完全相同的月報酬序列，為天然的配對設計。
效果量（Cohen's d）所有假說p 值受樣本量影響；效果量直接衡量差異的「實際大小」，不受樣本量影響。
MethodHypothesisWhy
Block BootstrapH1 (regime difference), H3 (60/40 vs stock)Preserves monthly autocorrelation, no normality assumption. Block size = 12 months, 10,000 iterations.
Fisher z-transformH2 (correlation structural change)Specifically designed to compare two correlation coefficients, handles different sample sizes.
Paired t-testH4 (rebalancing effect)Rebalanced and non-rebalanced portfolios use identical monthly returns — a natural paired design.
Effect size (Cohen's d)All hypothesesp-values depend on sample size; effect sizes measure practical significance regardless of N.

Block Bootstrap 的 block size 選擇：我們使用 12 個月（1 年）的 block size，以捕捉金融數據中的月度動量效應和季節性模式。較短的 block（如 3 個月）可能無法保留完整的自相關結構；較長的 block（如 24 個月）則會大幅減少有效重抽次數。12 個月是文獻中最常用的折衷值（Politis & Romano, 1994）。

顯著性水準：所有檢定使用 alpha = 0.05 的雙尾檢定。在四個假說的情境下，我們意識到多重比較問題，但由於每個假說檢驗的是不同面向的獨立問題（而非同一問題的多次比較），我們維持個別 alpha = 0.05 而未進行 Bonferroni 校正。

Block size selection: We use a 12-month (1-year) block size to capture monthly momentum effects and seasonal patterns. Shorter blocks (e.g., 3 months) may miss autocorrelation structure; longer blocks (e.g., 24 months) drastically reduce effective resamples. 12 months is the most common compromise in the literature (Politis & Romano, 1994).

Significance level: All tests use two-tailed alpha = 0.05. While we test four hypotheses, each examines a different aspect of 60/40 performance (not multiple comparisons of the same question), so we maintain individual alpha = 0.05 without Bonferroni correction.
