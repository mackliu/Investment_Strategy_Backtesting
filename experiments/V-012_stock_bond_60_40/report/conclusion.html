V-012：股債 60/40 永遠有效？V-012: Does the 60/40 Portfolio Always Work?

結論Conclusion

5.1 核心結論5.1 Key Conclusions

本研究以美國近百年的 Shiller 數據，系統性檢驗了 60/40 股債配置的真實面貌。以下五個核心結論，每個都以統計檢定和實際數據為支撐：

Using nearly a century of US Shiller data, we systematically examine the true picture of 60/40. The following five core conclusions are each supported by statistical tests and empirical evidence:

  1. 60/40 並非「永遠有效」，而是「環境適用」。在債券牛市（1982-2020）Sharpe ratio 為 1.30，年化 10.6%；但在高通膨時代（1965-81）僅 0.59，年化 5.2%；升息週期（2022-23）更降至 -0.51。Block Bootstrap 檢定確認環境差異具統計意義（p = 0.0287，效果量 = -0.663）。過去 40 年的「黃金時代」是不可重複的歷史恩賜，不應直接外推至未來。
  2. 股債相關性存在結構性變化，決定分散效果的成敗。Fisher z 檢定以 z = 6.68、p = 2.41e-11 的極高顯著性，確認 2000 年前後的股債相關性從 +0.200（正）轉為 +0.036（接近零），又在 2022 年回到 +0.456（正）。相關性是時間函數，不是常數——依賴固定相關性假設的配置模型將產生重大誤判。
  3. 60/40 長期風險調整報酬仍優於純股票，但優勢有限。Bootstrap 檢定確認 60/40 Sharpe（0.890）顯著高於 100% 股票（0.659），p = 0.0000。但效果量僅 0.166（小），差異來自波動度降低而非報酬增加。效率前緣的最佳 Sharpe 約在 25% 股票，60/40 接近但非最優。
  4. 再平衡提升風險調整報酬，但可能犧牲絕對報酬。年度再平衡 Sharpe（0.890）優於不再平衡（0.789），但不再平衡的總報酬（762633%）遠高於再平衡（279870%）。配對 t 檢定（p = 0.0030）顯著但效果量極小（-0.087），再平衡的核心價值在風險控制而非報酬提升。
  5. 加入第三資產和全球分散是「後 60/40」時代的方向。永久投資組合 Sharpe 1.126、最大回檔 -12.3%（ETF 時代最佳）。台灣在地版 60/40（0050 + 定存）的分散效果有限（Sharpe 0.771），台灣投資人需考慮海外債券 ETF 或全球配置基金。
  6. 60/40 does not "always work" — it is regime-dependent. Bond bull (1982-2020) Sharpe 1.30, return 10.6%; high inflation (1965-81) Sharpe 0.59, return 5.2%; rate hike (2022-23) Sharpe -0.51. Block Bootstrap confirms regime difference is significant (p = 0.0287, effect = -0.663). The 40-year golden age was an unrepeatable gift; do not extrapolate.
  7. Stock-bond correlation shifts structurally, determining diversification success. Fisher z (z = 6.68, p = 2.41e-11) confirms structural shift from +0.200 to +0.036 around 2000, reverting to +0.456 in 2022. Correlation is time-varying — fixed-correlation models produce major misallocation.
  8. 60/40 still beats pure stock risk-adjusted, but advantage is small. Bootstrap confirms 60/40 Sharpe (0.890) > 100% stock (0.659), p = 0.0000, but small effect (0.166) — mostly from lower volatility, not higher returns. Frontier optimum at ~25% stock; 60/40 is close but not optimal.
  9. Rebalancing improves risk-adjusted returns but may sacrifice absolute returns. Annual Sharpe (0.890) > no-rebalance (0.789), but no-rebalance total (762633%) far exceeds annual (279870%). Paired t (p = 0.0030) significant but tiny effect (-0.087); value is in risk control.
  10. Third asset and global diversification are the post-60/40 direction. Permanent Portfolio Sharpe 1.126, max DD -12.3% (best in ETF era). Taiwan local 60/40 (0050 + deposit) has limited diversification (Sharpe 0.771); Taiwan investors should consider overseas bond ETFs or global allocation funds.

整體來看，四個假說中有三個獲得統計支持（H2、H3、H4），H1 在邊際顯著的水準上獲得支持。但更重要的是效果量的解讀：H2 的相關性結構變化是唯一具有中等以上效果量的發現，這意味著它是四個發現中對投資實務影響最大的一個。

Overall, three of four hypotheses received statistical support (H2, H3, H4), with H1 supported at marginal significance. But effect size interpretation matters more: H2's correlation structural change is the only finding with medium-or-larger effect size, making it the most practically impactful discovery for investment practice.

5.3 研究限制總結5.3 Limitations Summary

  • 債券報酬為殖利率模型推估，非實際指數績效。
  • 數據為月度頻率，可能低估極端事件的影響。
  • 未納入交易成本、管理費和稅務。
  • 僅以美國數據為主，可能存在存活者偏差。
  • 擴展配置（含黃金）樣本期間僅約 20 年。
  • 台灣以定存代替債券，性質根本不同。
  • 本研究為歷史回測，不構成投資建議。過去績效不代表未來表現。
  • Bond returns are model-estimated from yields, not actual index performance.
  • Monthly frequency may understate extreme event impact.
  • Transaction costs, management fees, and taxes excluded.
  • Primarily US data; possible survivorship bias.
  • Extended allocations (with gold) cover only ~20 years.
  • Taiwan uses deposits as bond proxy — fundamentally different.
  • This is a historical backtest, not investment advice. Past performance does not predict future results.

5.4 未來研究方向5.4 Future Research

  1. 通膨保護債券（TIPS）的替代效果：TIPS 自 1997 年發行以來，提供了直接的通膨對沖。以 TIPS 取代傳統債券是否能改善 60/40 在高通膨環境的表現？這是最有實務價值的延伸研究方向。
  2. 動態配置策略：根據利率環境、波動度水準或趨勢信號動態調整股債比例。例如目標波動度策略（Target Volatility）或簡單的趨勢追蹤（Trend Following）。這些策略能否在環境轉換時「自動適應」，避免 60/40 的環境依賴問題？
  3. 跨國驗證：以日本、歐洲、新興市場的數據驗證本研究的結論是否具有普遍性。特別是日本的「失落三十年」和歐洲的負利率環境，對 60/40 的影響尤其值得探討。
  4. 交易成本和稅務的影響：加入實際的交易成本（如 ETF 管理費 0.03-0.5%）和稅務（如資本利得稅、股利稅），評估再平衡頻率的最適選擇。
  5. 起始時點效應：分析在不同估值水準（CAPE 高/低）和利率水準（高/低）開始投資，對 10 年、20 年、30 年結果的影響，這對退休規劃尤其重要。
  1. TIPS as bond alternative: Since 1997, TIPS provide direct inflation hedging. Can replacing traditional bonds with TIPS improve 60/40 in inflationary environments? This is the most practically valuable extension.
  2. Dynamic allocation strategies: Adjust stock/bond ratio based on rate environment, volatility levels, or trend signals (e.g., Target Volatility, Trend Following). Can these strategies "auto-adapt" across regimes, avoiding 60/40's environment dependence?
  3. Cross-country validation: Test conclusions with Japan, Europe, and emerging market data. Japan's "lost three decades" and Europe's negative rates are particularly interesting cases.
  4. Transaction costs and taxes: Include actual costs (ETF fees 0.03-0.5%) and taxes (capital gains, dividend taxes) to find optimal rebalancing frequency.
  5. Starting point effects: Analyze how starting at different CAPE and rate levels affects 10/20/30-year outcomes — especially critical for retirement planning.

一句話結論

60/40 沒有「死」，但它從來就不是「萬能藥」。在 98 年的歷史中，它是一個環境適用型的合理配置（Sharpe 0.890），但其效果高度取決於利率環境和股債相關性。「設定即忘記」的時代已經結束——投資人需要理解環境、主動調整，並考慮在傳統股債之外加入第三類資產。

Bottom Line

60/40 is not "dead," but it was never a "magic bullet." Over 98 years, it is a regime-appropriate allocation (Sharpe 0.890), but its effectiveness depends heavily on rates and stock-bond correlation. The "set and forget" era is over — investors must understand the environment, actively adjust, and consider adding a third asset class beyond traditional stocks and bonds.

免責聲明：本研究為學術性質的歷史回測分析，不構成任何形式的投資建議。過去的績效不代表未來的表現。投資人在做出投資決策前，應諮詢合格的財務顧問。
