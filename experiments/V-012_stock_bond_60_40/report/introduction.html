V-012：股債 60/40 永遠有效？V-012: Does the 60/40 Portfolio Always Work?

緒論Introduction

1.1 研究背景1.1 Background

「60% 股票 + 40% 債券」是全球最廣為人知的投資配置方式。自 1950 年代 Harry Markowitz 提出現代投資組合理論以來，60/40 一直被視為「懶人投資法」的黃金標準，被各大退休基金與理財顧問推薦。其核心邏輯很直觀：股票提供長期成長動力，債券在股市下跌時提供避風港，兩者的負相關性使得組合的波動度遠低於純股票。

然而，這個「股跌債漲」的假設並非天然定律。2022 年，聯準會為對抗 40 年來最高的通膨，從 0% 利率急速升至 5% 以上。這場激進升息讓金融市場經歷了極為罕見的「股債齊跌」：標普 500 全年下跌約 18%，Bloomberg 美國長期國庫券指數同時暴跌超過 30%。60/40 組合經歷了自 1937 年以來最差的表現，「分散投資」的核心邏輯被嚴重質疑。

這不是歷史上第一次股債齊跌。1970 年代的石油危機和滯脹，同樣導致股票和債券同時虧損。但在 1982-2020 年長達 38 年的「大穩定」時代，利率持續下行、股債負相關成為常態，許多投資人誤以為這是永恆不變的規律。2022 年的經驗是一記警鐘：60/40 的黃金時代可能已經結束。

本研究的核心問題是：如果我們將時間拉長到 150 年以上，涵蓋各種利率環境、通膨水準、股債相關性狀態，60/40 配置的真實面貌是什麼？它是一個「任何環境都有效的萬能公式」，還是一個「特定時代產物」？

The "60% stock + 40% bond" allocation is the most well-known investment strategy worldwide. Since Harry Markowitz proposed Modern Portfolio Theory in the 1950s, 60/40 has been the gold standard, recommended by pension funds and advisors everywhere. Its core logic is intuitive: stocks provide growth, bonds provide a safe haven during equity declines, and their negative correlation creates portfolio volatility far below pure stocks.

However, the "stocks down, bonds up" assumption is not a natural law. In 2022, the Fed aggressively hiked rates from 0% to over 5% to combat 40-year-high inflation. This triggered an exceptionally rare "stocks and bonds falling together" event: the S&P 500 fell ~18% while long-term Treasuries plunged over 30%. 60/40 posted its worst performance since 1937, and the diversification thesis came under serious scrutiny.

This was not the first stock-bond co-decline in history. The 1970s oil crisis and stagflation also caused simultaneous losses. But during the 38-year "Great Moderation" (1982-2020), declining rates and negative stock-bond correlation became the norm, leading many investors to mistake a temporary pattern for a permanent law. 2022 was a wake-up call: the golden age of 60/40 may be over.

The core question of this study: viewed over 150+ years spanning diverse rate environments, inflation levels, and stock-bond correlation regimes, what is the true picture of 60/40? Is it an "all-weather formula" or an "era-specific product"?

1.2 文獻回顧1.2 Literature Review

Markowitz (1952) 的現代投資組合理論奠定了 60/40 的理論基礎——只要兩個資產不完全正相關，組合的風險就會低於個別資產的加權平均。這個「免費午餐」是分散投資的數學保證。然而，Markowitz 理論假設相關性為常數，這在現實中並不成立。

Campbell & Viceira (2002) 的重要貢獻在於揭示股債相關性隨總經環境動態變化。他們發現通膨是關鍵驅動因素：高通膨時期，升息同時壓低股票和債券價格，使兩者正相關（分散效果消失）；低通膨時期，經濟衰退引發「逃向品質」（flight to quality），資金從股票流入債券，使兩者負相關（分散效果強）。

Asness, Ilmanen & Maloney (2012) 在 AQR 的研究中指出，60/40 近 30 年的卓越表現很大程度上是「不可重複的歷史恩賜」——利率從 1981 年的 15% 一路下滑至 2020 年的 0%，債券經歷了史無前例的 38 年大牛市。未來利率再次大幅下降的空間極為有限，60/40 的 Sharpe ratio 可能無法維持。

Neville, Draaisma, Funnell & Harvey (2021) 分析了多國數百年的數據，發現「股債齊跌」並非異常事件，而是歷史上大約每 20-30 年就會出現一次。最常發生的觸發因素是通膨衝擊和貨幣政策緊縮。

Anarkulova, Cederburg & O'Doherty (2023) 以 38 個國家的長期數據進行跨國研究，發現 60/40 的出色表現在很大程度上是「美國例外」——許多國家的 60/40 配置並沒有展現出類似美國的風險調整報酬優勢。

Markowitz (1952) established the theoretical foundation for 60/40 — as long as two assets are not perfectly correlated, portfolio risk is lower than the weighted average. This "free lunch" is the mathematical guarantee of diversification. However, Markowitz assumed constant correlations, which does not hold in practice.

Campbell & Viceira (2002) revealed that stock-bond correlation dynamically varies with macro conditions. Inflation is the key driver: during high inflation, rising rates depress both stocks and bonds (positive correlation); during low inflation, recessions trigger "flight to quality" from stocks to bonds (negative correlation).

Asness, Ilmanen & Maloney (2012) argued that 60/40's stellar 30-year performance was largely an "unrepeatable historical gift" — rates fell from 15% in 1981 to 0% in 2020, giving bonds an unprecedented 38-year bull market. Future rate declines of similar magnitude are extremely unlikely.

Neville et al. (2021) analyzed multi-country, multi-century data, finding stock-bond co-declines are not anomalies but occur roughly every 20-30 years, typically triggered by inflation shocks and monetary tightening.

Anarkulova et al. (2023) studied 38 countries and found 60/40 success is largely "US exceptionalism" — many countries' 60/40 portfolios do not show similar advantages.

1.3 研究問題與假說1.3 Research Questions & Hypotheses

本研究提出四個核心研究問題，每個問題對應一個可以統計檢定的假說：

#研究問題虛無假說 H0對立假說 H1
Q160/40 在不同利率環境的表現是否一致？高通膨與債券牛市的 Sharpe 無差異高通膨 Sharpe 顯著低於債券牛市
Q2股債相關性是否發生結構性改變？2000 年前後相關係數無差異相關係數顯著不同
Q360/40 是否優於 100% 股票（風險調整後）？60/40 與 100% 股票的 Sharpe 無差異60/40 Sharpe 顯著較高
Q4再平衡是否顯著改善 60/40 績效？再平衡與不再平衡的 Sharpe 無差異再平衡顯著改善

We propose four core research questions, each with a testable statistical hypothesis:

#Research QuestionNull H0Alternative H1
Q1Is 60/40 performance consistent across rate regimes?Sharpe equal in high inflation vs bond bullHigh inflation Sharpe significantly lower
Q2Has stock-bond correlation structurally shifted?No difference pre/post 2000Significantly different correlation
Q3Does 60/40 beat 100% stock (risk-adjusted)?60/40 Sharpe = 100% stock Sharpe60/40 Sharpe significantly higher
Q4Does rebalancing improve 60/40?No Sharpe difference with rebalancingRebalancing significantly improves

Q1 的意義：若 60/40 在不同環境的表現差異顯著，代表「放著不管」的懶人投資法需要因應環境調整，不能一成不變。

Q2 的意義：若股債相關性存在結構性變化，代表用過去 20 年的負相關經驗來預測未來可能不可靠，60/40 的分散效果會隨環境而大幅波動。

Q3 的意義：若 60/40 整體而言仍顯著優於純股票，代表分散投資的核心邏輯雖然在特定環境下會失效，但長期平均仍然成立。

Q4 的意義：再平衡是 60/40 實務操作中最重要的環節。若再平衡能顯著改善績效，代表「紀律性操作」本身就是報酬來源。

Q1 significance: If 60/40 varies significantly across regimes, the "set-and-forget" approach needs regime-adaptive adjustments.

Q2 significance: If correlations shifted structurally, using the past 20 years of negative correlation to predict the future may be unreliable.

Q3 significance: If 60/40 is still significantly better risk-adjusted, the diversification thesis holds on average despite environment-specific failures.

Q4 significance: Rebalancing is the most critical operational element. If it significantly improves performance, disciplined execution itself generates returns.

1.4 研究貢獻1.4 Research Contributions

  • 超長期視角：以 150+ 年的 Shiller 數據，涵蓋從大蕭條到 2022 年升息衝擊的所有主要經濟環境。
  • 環境分層分析：將歷史劃分為四個利率環境，獨立評估 60/40 在每個環境下的表現。
  • 相關性結構變化：以 Fisher z 檢定量化股債相關性的結構性轉變。
  • 多資產擴展：測試加入黃金的永久投資組合和四資產配置，探索「後 60/40」方案。
  • 台灣在地驗證：以 0050 + 定存檢驗 60/40 概念在台灣的可行性。
  • Ultra-long horizon: 150+ years of Shiller data covering all major economic environments from the Great Depression to 2022 rate shocks.
  • Regime-stratified analysis: History divided into four rate environments, evaluating 60/40 independently in each.
  • Correlation structural change: Fisher z-test quantifies the structural shift in stock-bond correlation.
  • Multi-asset extension: Tests Permanent Portfolio and four-asset allocation as "post-60/40" alternatives.
  • Taiwan local validation: Tests 60/40 concept in Taiwan using 0050 + bank deposits.
