V-012：股債 60/40 永遠有效？V-012: Does the 60/40 Portfolio Always Work?

討論Discussion

4.1 核心發現解讀4.1 Interpretation of Key Findings

發現一：60/40 的黃金時代已經結束。1982-2020 年長達 38 年的債券牛市，讓 60/40 擁有 1.30 的高 Sharpe ratio，年化報酬達 10.6%。但這更多是時代產物，而非配置本身的優越性。在高通膨時代（1965-81），同樣的配置 Sharpe ratio 僅 0.59，年化報酬降至 5.2%。Block Bootstrap 檢定（H1, p = 0.0287）確認了這種環境差異具有統計意義，效果量（-0.663）為中等偏大，顯示差異不僅是統計雜訊。

這意味著什麼？過去 40 年許多理財建議都建立在「60/40 長期有效」的前提上，但這個前提實際上是建立在一個不可重複的歷史環境上——利率不可能再從 15% 降到 0%。對於正在規劃退休的投資人而言，以 1982-2020 的經驗來設計退休配置可能會嚴重高估 60/40 的保護能力。

Finding 1: The golden age of 60/40 is over. The 38-year bond bull (1982-2020) gave 60/40 a Sharpe of 1.30 and 10.6% annualized return. But this was era-specific, not inherent to the allocation. During high inflation (1965-81), Sharpe was only 0.59 with 5.2% return. Block Bootstrap (H1, p = 0.0287) confirms this regime difference is statistically meaningful, with medium-large effect size (-0.663).

What does this mean? Much financial advice over the past 40 years was built on "60/40 works long-term," but this premise rests on an unrepeatable historical environment — rates cannot fall from 15% to 0% again. For investors planning retirement, using 1982-2020 experience to design allocations may seriously overestimate 60/40's protective ability.

發現二：相關性結構性改變已被統計確認。Fisher z 檢定（H2）以 z = 6.68、p = 2.41e-11 的極高顯著性，確認 2000 年前後的股債相關性存在結構性差異。2000 年前的相關性多為正值或接近零，2000 年後轉為持續負值。最重要的是：2022 年的升息讓相關性又回到了 +0.456（正相關），暗示 2000-2020 年的負相關可能是「低利率時代的特例」，而非新常態。

這對 60/40 的含義是深遠的。如果未來利率維持在 3-5% 的水準（而非回到 0%），股債相關性可能會回到歷史平均水準（接近零或微正相關），60/40 的分散效果將大幅低於 2000-2020 年的體驗。

Finding 2: Structural correlation change is statistically confirmed. Fisher z-test (H2) with z = 6.68, p = 2.41e-11 confirms with extreme significance that stock-bond correlation shifted structurally around 2000. Pre-2000 correlation was mostly positive or near zero; post-2000 it turned persistently negative. Crucially, 2022 rate hikes pushed correlation back to +0.456 (positive), suggesting 2000-2020 negative correlation may be a "low-rate era anomaly," not a new normal.

The implication is profound. If rates stay at 3-5% (rather than returning to 0%), correlation may revert to its historical average (near zero or mildly positive), and 60/40 diversification will be far weaker than the 2000-2020 experience.

發現三：60/40 長期仍近效率前緣，但優勢有限。Bootstrap 檢定（H3, p = 0.0000）確認 60/40 的 Sharpe（0.890）顯著高於 100% 股票（0.659），但效果量僅 0.166（小效果量），差異在實際操作中可能不顯著。效率前緣顯示最佳 Sharpe 約在 25% 股票配置，60/40 接近但略偏右。

換言之，60/40 在 98 年的全期間確實提供了更好的風險調整報酬，但優勢主要來自降低波動度，而非提高報酬。對於風險承受能力較高、投資期限較長的投資人，80/20 甚至 100/0 可能是更好的選擇——純股票的長期報酬優勢難以被分散投資完全取代。

Finding 3: 60/40 remains near the efficient frontier, but advantage is limited. Bootstrap (H3, p = 0.0000) confirms 60/40 Sharpe (0.890) is significantly higher than 100% stock (0.659), but with small effect size (0.166) — practically marginal. Efficient frontier places max Sharpe at ~25% stock; 60/40 is close but slightly right.

In other words, 60/40 did provide better risk-adjusted returns over 98 years, but mainly by reducing volatility, not by increasing returns. For investors with higher risk tolerance and longer horizons, 80/20 or even 100/0 may be better choices — stocks' long-term return advantage is hard to fully replicate through diversification.

發現四：再平衡的效果與直覺相反。配對 t 檢定（H4, p = 0.0030）顯示再平衡確實對月報酬有顯著影響，但效果量極小（-0.087），且方向出人意料——不再平衡的總報酬（762633%）遠高於年度再平衡（279870%）。這是因為不再平衡讓股票比重隨長期上漲而自然增加，最終捕捉了更多股票報酬。

然而，這並不代表再平衡沒有價值。年度再平衡的 Sharpe（0.890）仍優於不再平衡（0.789）。再平衡的核心價值是維持投資人原本設定的風險水準——不再平衡實質上是「不知不覺地增加了風險承受」，這可能不符合投資人的初衷。

Finding 4: Rebalancing effect contradicts intuition. Paired t-test (H4, p = 0.0030) shows rebalancing significantly affects monthly returns, but with tiny effect size (-0.087), and in a surprising direction — no-rebalance total return (762633%) far exceeds annual rebalancing (279870%). This is because drift lets stock weight rise with long-term gains, capturing more equity returns.

However, this doesn't mean rebalancing is valueless. Annual rebalancing Sharpe (0.890) still beats no-rebalance (0.789). Rebalancing's core value is maintaining the investor's intended risk level — not rebalancing effectively means "unknowingly increasing risk exposure," which may not align with the investor's original intent.

4.2 60/40 真的「過時」了嗎？4.2 Is 60/40 Really "Obsolete"?

2022 年之後，「60/40 已死」成為金融媒體的熱門標題。本研究的數據提供了更為細膩的答案：60/40 沒有「死」，但它從未「永生」。

從 98 年的長期角度來看，60/40 確實提供了合理的風險調整報酬（Sharpe 0.890，優於 100% 股票的 0.659），最大回撤也顯著低於純股票（-59.8% vs -81.8%）。但這個「平均」數字掩蓋了環境間的巨大差異。

更準確的說法是：60/40 是一個「環境適用型」而非「萬能型」的配置。它在以下環境中表現最好：(1) 利率下降趨勢、(2) 經濟溫和成長、(3) 通膨受控。它在以下環境中容易失效：(1) 通膨快速上升、(2) 央行激進升息、(3) 供給面衝擊。

對於投資人而言，關鍵不是放棄 60/40，而是理解它的適用條件，並在環境改變時主動調整。「設定即忘記」的時代確實已經結束。

After 2022, "60/40 is dead" became a popular media headline. Our data provides a more nuanced answer: 60/40 is not "dead," but it was never "immortal."

Over 98 years, 60/40 indeed offers reasonable risk-adjusted returns (Sharpe 0.890, better than 100% stock's 0.659), with significantly lower max drawdown (-59.8% vs -81.8%). But this "average" masks enormous regime variation.

More accurately: 60/40 is an "environment-adaptive" rather than "all-weather" allocation. It works best with: (1) declining rates, (2) moderate growth, (3) controlled inflation. It tends to fail with: (1) rising inflation, (2) aggressive rate hikes, (3) supply shocks.

The key for investors is not abandoning 60/40 but understanding its conditions and actively adjusting when the environment changes. The "set and forget" era is indeed over.

4.3 擴展配置的啟示4.3 Insights from Extended Allocations

在 ETF 時代的數據中，兩種擴展配置提供了有趣的參考：

  • 永久投資組合（25/25/25/25）：Sharpe ratio 1.126，最大回撤僅 -12.3%——在同期間所有配置中表現最佳。黃金的加入提供了額外的通膨對沖，現金部位降低了整體波動。然而，這段期間包含了 2008-2020 年的黃金大牛市，未來能否維持尚待觀察。
  • 四資產配置（60/20/10/10）：Sharpe ratio 1.025，最大回撤 -26.1%——在維持較高股票比重的同時，加入黃金和現金提供了額外的穩定性。

這些結果暗示，「後 60/40 時代」的投資配置可能需要超越傳統的兩資產框架，納入與股債相關性低的第三類資產（如黃金、大宗商品、不動產投資信託等）。不過，擴展配置的數據期間僅約 20 年，統計穩定性有限。

In ETF-era data, two extended allocations provide interesting references:

  • Permanent Portfolio (25/25/25/25): Sharpe 1.126, max DD only -12.3% — best performance among all allocations in this period. Gold adds inflation hedging; cash reduces overall volatility. However, this period includes the 2008-2020 gold bull market, so future sustainability is uncertain.
  • Four-Asset (60/20/10/10): Sharpe 1.025, max DD -26.1% — maintains higher stock weight while adding gold and cash for extra stability.

These results suggest "post-60/40" allocation may need to go beyond two-asset frameworks, incorporating a third asset class with low stock-bond correlation (gold, commodities, REITs). However, extended allocations cover only ~20 years, limiting statistical robustness.

4.4 台灣在地驗證的意義4.4 Taiwan Local Validation

台灣在地版 60/40（0050 + 定存）的分析揭示了一個現實問題：台灣缺乏等效的本地債券投資工具。

0050（100% 股票）在 22 年期間的年化報酬為 12.3%，Sharpe ratio 0.663，最大回撤 -53.9%。加入 40% 定存後（「60/40」），年化報酬降至 8.6%，Sharpe 提高至 0.771，最大回撤縮小至 -34.0%。

問題在於：台灣定存利率極低（長期平均約 1.4% 年化），且波動度接近零——它提供的不是「債券的分散效果」，而是「現金的拖累效果」。美國的 60/40 之所以有效，是因為債券在危機時會上漲（負相關）；台灣定存只是固定低利息，不會在股市下跌時反向上漲。

對台灣投資人的建議：如果想複製 60/40 的分散效果，應考慮使用海外債券 ETF（如美國公債 ETF）取代定存，或者直接投資全球性的 60/40 基金。

Taiwan's local 60/40 (0050 + deposits) reveals a practical reality: Taiwan lacks equivalent local bond investment instruments.

0050 (100% stock) over 22 years: 12.3% return, Sharpe 0.663, max DD -53.9%. Adding 40% deposits ("60/40"): return drops to 8.6%, Sharpe improves to 0.771, max DD narrows to -34.0%.

The problem: Taiwan deposit rates are extremely low (1.4% annualized average), with near-zero volatility — providing "cash drag" rather than "bond diversification." US 60/40 works because bonds rally during crises (negative correlation); Taiwan deposits just pay fixed low interest without offsetting equity losses.

For Taiwan investors: to replicate 60/40 diversification, consider overseas bond ETFs (e.g., US Treasury ETFs) instead of deposits, or invest directly in global 60/40 funds.

4.5 研究限制4.5 Limitations

本研究存在以下限制，讀者在應用結論時應謹慎考慮：

  1. 債券報酬模型的近似性：使用殖利率推算債券報酬是學術界的標準做法，但並非實際指數報酬。模型假設固定存續期間和線性價格變動，忽略了凸性效應、信用風險和流動性溢酬。在利率劇烈變動時期（如 1980-82），模型的近似誤差可能較大。
  2. 數據頻率限制：Shiller 數據為月度頻率，無法捕捉日內或日間的波動模式。某些極端事件（如 1987 年黑色星期一單日暴跌 22%）在月度數據中被稀釋。這可能低估了短期風險和尾端事件的影響。
  3. 交易成本未納入：所有分析未考慮交易成本、管理費和稅務。在高頻再平衡的情境下，交易成本可能顯著影響結論。但對於年度再平衡的低周轉率投資組合，這個影響預計很小。
  4. 存活者偏差：Shiller 數據基於美國市場，而美國是 20 世紀表現最好的股市之一。如 Anarkulova et al. (2023) 所示，其他國家的 60/40 績效可能大幅不同。本研究的結論可能對美國市場存在「選擇偏差」。
  5. 台灣「債券」替代的局限：以定存代替債券，性質完全不同於美國公債。定存沒有利率風險、沒有價格波動、沒有資本利得的可能性。因此台灣的「60/40」結果與美國不具直接可比性。
  6. 擴展配置的短期樣本：黃金 ETF（GLD）自 2004 年才開始交易，擴展配置的分析僅約 20 年。這段期間包含了黃金的歷史性大牛市（2008-2011）和長期低利率，結果可能不具代表性。
  7. 前瞻性限制：效率前緣和最優配置都是「事後」計算的結果。未來的最優配置可能與歷史最優有顯著差異，特別是在利率環境根本性改變的情況下。

This study has the following limitations that readers should consider:

  1. Bond return model approximation: Yield-based estimation is standard in academia but not actual index returns. The model assumes fixed duration and linear price changes, ignoring convexity, credit risk, and liquidity premiums. Approximation errors may be larger during extreme rate movements (e.g., 1980-82).
  2. Data frequency limitations: Monthly Shiller data cannot capture intraday or daily volatility patterns. Some extreme events (e.g., 1987 Black Monday -22% in one day) are diluted in monthly data, potentially understating short-term risk and tail events.
  3. Transaction costs excluded: No analysis accounts for trading costs, management fees, or taxes. High-frequency rebalancing results may be significantly affected, though annual rebalancing with low turnover should have minimal impact.
  4. Survivorship bias: Shiller data is US-only, and the US was one of the best-performing markets of the 20th century. As Anarkulova et al. (2023) showed, other countries' 60/40 may perform very differently. Our conclusions may carry "selection bias" toward US markets.
  5. Taiwan "bond" proxy limitations: Bank deposits differ fundamentally from US Treasuries — no interest rate risk, no price volatility, no capital gain potential. Taiwan "60/40" results are not directly comparable to US results.
  6. Short sample for extended allocations: GLD ETF started trading in 2004; extended analysis covers only ~20 years. This period includes gold's historic bull (2008-2011) and prolonged low rates, so results may not be representative.
  7. Forward-looking limitations: Efficient frontiers and optimal allocations are ex-post calculations. Future optimums may differ significantly, especially if the rate environment fundamentally shifts.

4.6 實務啟示4.6 Practical Implications

保守型投資人

60/40 仍是合理的起點，但需要更「主動」的心態：

  • 理解 60/40 在高通膨/升息環境下的保護能力會大幅降低。
  • 考慮加入黃金或抗通膨債券（TIPS）作為第三資產，形成 50/30/20（股/債/替代）。
  • 使用存續期間較短的債券（如 1-3 年期國庫券）來降低利率風險。
  • 維持年度再平衡紀律，確保風險水準不會因漂移而失控。

積極型投資人

如果風險承受能力較高且投資期限超過 20 年，可以考慮：

  • 配置 70-80% 股票，只保留 20-30% 的避險部位。歷史數據顯示，長期來看股票的報酬優勢難以被分散投資完全取代。
  • 根據利率環境動態調整股債比例——在升息週期減少長期債券部位，增加現金或短天期債券。
  • 考慮全球分散而非僅美國市場，以降低單一國家風險。

台灣投資人

台灣的投資環境有其特殊性：

  • 本地缺乏可投資的公債 ETF，定存的分散效果極為有限。
  • 建議透過複委託或海外券商投資全球債券 ETF（如 BND、AGG、TLT）。
  • 或直接投資全球性的 60/40 基金（如 Vanguard LifeStrategy 系列）。
  • 若堅持純台股配置，應認知到風險集中在單一市場、單一貨幣，缺乏真正的債券避險功能。

Conservative Investors

60/40 remains a reasonable starting point, but requires a more "active" mindset:

  • Understand that 60/40 protection weakens significantly in high inflation/rate environments.
  • Consider adding gold or TIPS as a third asset for a 50/30/20 (stock/bond/alternatives) mix.
  • Use shorter-duration bonds (e.g., 1-3 year Treasuries) to reduce interest rate risk.
  • Maintain annual rebalancing discipline to prevent risk drift.

Aggressive Investors

With higher risk tolerance and 20+ year horizon, consider:

  • 70-80% stocks with only 20-30% hedging. History shows stocks' long-term return advantage is hard to replicate through diversification alone.
  • Dynamic stock/bond adjustment by rate environment — reduce long-term bonds during rate hikes, increase cash or short-duration bonds.
  • Global diversification beyond US-only to reduce single-country risk.

Taiwan Investors

Taiwan's investment environment has unique characteristics:

  • No investable local government bond ETFs; deposit diversification is extremely limited.
  • Use sub-brokerage or overseas platforms to invest in global bond ETFs (BND, AGG, TLT).
  • Or invest directly in global 60/40 funds (e.g., Vanguard LifeStrategy series).
  • If committed to Taiwan-only allocation, acknowledge concentrated risk in a single market/currency without true bond hedging capability.
